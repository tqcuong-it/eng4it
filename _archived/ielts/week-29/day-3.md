## 📝 Bài 1: Process Diagram (20 phút)

### Đề bài

> *The diagram below shows the process of producing chocolate from cacao beans.*

**Các bước trong quy trình:**

1. **Thu hoạch** (Harvesting): Quả cacao được hái từ cây → tách hạt ra khỏi quả
2. **Lên men** (Fermentation): Hạt được đặt trong thùng gỗ, phủ lá chuối, lên men 5-7 ngày
3. **Phơi khô** (Drying): Hạt được phơi dưới nắng 7-14 ngày
4. **Vận chuyển** (Shipping): Hạt khô được đóng bao, vận chuyển đến nhà máy
5. **Rang** (Roasting): Hạt được rang ở 120-150°C trong 30 phút
6. **Nghiền** (Grinding): Hạt rang được nghiền thành bột cacao (cocoa mass)
7. **Ép** (Pressing): Bột cacao được ép → tách bơ cacao và bột cacao
8. **Trộn** (Mixing): Bơ cacao + bột cacao + đường + sữa → trộn đều
9. **Đổ khuôn** (Moulding): Hỗn hợp được đổ vào khuôn → làm mát
10. **Đóng gói** (Packaging): Sô cô la thành phẩm được bao gói → phân phối

### ✍️ Lưu ý quan trọng cho Process

- ✅ Dùng **passive voice** (is harvested, are fermented, is ground)
- ✅ Dùng **sequencing words** (First, Then, Next, After that, Finally)
- ✅ Mô tả **tổng quan** số bước + giai đoạn chính
- ❌ Không đưa ý kiến cá nhân
- ❌ Không bỏ sót bước nào

### 📄 Bài mẫu (Band 6.5)

> The diagram illustrates the step-by-step process of manufacturing chocolate from raw cacao beans.
>
> Overall, the production of chocolate involves ten main stages, beginning with the harvesting of cacao pods and ending with the packaging of finished chocolate products. The process can be broadly divided into two phases: the preparation of raw beans and the manufacturing of chocolate.
>
> In the first phase, ripe cacao pods are harvested from trees and the beans are extracted. These beans are then placed in wooden containers, covered with banana leaves, and left to ferment for five to seven days. After fermentation, the beans are spread out and dried in the sun for one to two weeks before being packed into bags and shipped to a chocolate factory.
>
> At the factory, the dried beans are roasted at temperatures between 120 and 150 degrees Celsius for approximately 30 minutes. The roasted beans are then ground into a paste known as cocoa mass, which is subsequently pressed to separate cocoa butter from cocoa powder. In the final stage, the cocoa butter and powder are combined with sugar and milk, poured into moulds to cool and solidify, and finally packaged for distribution.

**Số từ:** 185 từ ✅

### 💡 Từ vựng cho Process

| Sequencing | Passive Voice | Kết nối |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| First / Initially | is harvested | after which |
| Then / Next | are fermented | following this |
| Subsequently | is ground | once completed |
| After that | are roasted | at this stage |
| Finally / Lastly | is packaged | during this phase |

## 📝 Bài 2: Map (20 phút)

### Đề bài

> *The two maps below show the changes in the town of Millford between 1990 and 2020.*

**Millford năm 1990:**
- **Trung tâm:** Tòa thị chính (Town Hall) + Chợ trời (Open market) + Bưu điện (Post Office)
- **Phía Bắc:** Khu dân cư nhỏ (20 ngôi nhà) + Cánh đồng (Farmland)
- **Phía Nam:** Nhà ga xe lửa (Train Station) + Nhà máy (Factory)
- **Phía Đông:** Rừng cây (Woodland)
- **Phía Tây:** Sông (River) + Cầu cũ (Old Bridge)

**Millford năm 2020:**
- **Trung tâm:** Tòa thị chính (giữ nguyên) + Trung tâm mua sắm (Shopping Centre — thay thế chợ trời) + Bưu điện chuyển thành quán cà phê
- **Phía Bắc:** Khu dân cư mở rộng (80 ngôi nhà) + Farmland → Khu chung cư (Apartment blocks)
- **Phía Nam:** Nhà ga (giữ nguyên, thêm bãi đỗ xe) + Nhà máy → Công viên (Park)
- **Phía Đông:** Rừng cây → Trường học (School) + Sân thể thao (Sports ground)
- **Phía Tây:** Sông (giữ nguyên) + Cầu mới rộng hơn (New Bridge)

### ✍️ Lưu ý quan trọng cho Map

- ✅ Dùng **was/were replaced by, was converted into, was demolished**
- ✅ Chỉ rõ **vị trí**: to the north, in the centre, on the eastern side
- ✅ Overview: xu hướng chung (đô thị hóa, mở rộng, hiện đại hóa)
- ✅ Nhắc cả **thứ không thay đổi**: remained unchanged, was retained

### 📄 Bài mẫu (Band 6.5)

> The two maps compare the town of Millford in 1990 and 2020, showing the changes that took place over this 30-year period.
>
> Overall, Millford underwent significant urbanisation and modernisation. The town expanded considerably, with agricultural and natural areas being replaced by residential and commercial developments, while some original buildings were retained.
>
> In the centre of town, the Town Hall remained in its original location. However, the open market was replaced by a large shopping centre, and the post office was converted into a café. To the north, the small residential area of 20 houses expanded dramatically to 80 houses, and the farmland was developed into apartment blocks.
>
> In the south, the train station was retained but a car park was added beside it. The factory was demolished and replaced by a public park. On the eastern side, the woodland was cleared to make way for a school and sports ground. To the west, the river remained unchanged, but the old bridge was replaced by a wider, more modern bridge.

**Số từ:** 168 từ ✅

### 💡 Từ vựng cho Map

| Thay đổi | Giữ nguyên | Vị trí |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| was replaced by | remained unchanged | to the north/south |
| was converted into | was retained | in the centre |
| was demolished | stayed the same | on the eastern side |
| was developed into | was preserved | adjacent to |
| was expanded | — | opposite / beside |
