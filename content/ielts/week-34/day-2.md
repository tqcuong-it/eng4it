## ✍️ Writing Test

### Task 1 (20 phút — ít nhất 150 từ)

**The bar chart below shows the number of tourists visiting five different cities in 2015 and 2020 (in millions).**

| City | 2015 | 2020 |
|------|------|------|
| Bangkok | 21.5 | 6.7 |
| London | 19.8 | 7.1 |
| Paris | 16.9 | 5.3 |
| Dubai | 14.2 | 5.5 |
| Tokyo | 11.3 | 3.8 |

*Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.*

### Task 2 (40 phút — ít nhất 250 từ)

**In many countries, the gap between the rich and the poor is increasing. What problems does this cause? What solutions can you suggest?**

### Gợi ý Task 2:

**Problems:**
- Crime and social unrest
- Unequal access to education and healthcare
- Mental health issues / stress
- Social division and resentment

**Solutions:**
- Progressive taxation
- Improved access to education
- Minimum wage increases
- Social welfare programs

## 🎤 Speaking Test

Ghi âm toàn bộ!

### Part 1: Introduction (4-5 phút)

**Topic: Food & Cooking**
1. Do you enjoy cooking? Why or why not?
2. What kind of food do you usually eat?
3. Have you ever tried cooking a dish from another country?
4. Do you think it's important to know how to cook?

**Topic: Travel**
5. Do you like traveling?
6. Where was the last place you traveled to?
7. Do you prefer traveling alone or with others?
8. What place would you most like to visit?

### Part 2: Long Turn (3-4 phút)

> **Describe a time when you helped someone.**
>
> You should say:
> - who you helped
> - what you helped them with
> - how you helped them
>
> and explain how you felt about it.

**1 phút chuẩn bị → 2 phút nói**

### Part 3: Discussion (4-5 phút)

1. Do you think people are less willing to help others nowadays?
2. What are the benefits of volunteering?
3. Should governments do more to help people in need?
4. How can schools encourage children to be more helpful?
5. Is it better to give money or time when helping others?

## 📝 Bài mẫu tham khảo

{{< spoiler "Task 1 Model Answer" >}}

The bar chart compares tourist arrivals in five major cities in 2015 and 2020, measured in millions.

Overall, all five cities experienced dramatic declines in tourist numbers between 2015 and 2020, largely attributable to the global pandemic. Bangkok and London were the most popular destinations in both years.

In 2015, Bangkok attracted the highest number of tourists at 21.5 million, followed closely by London with 19.8 million. Paris, Dubai, and Tokyo received 16.9, 14.2, and 11.3 million visitors respectively. All five cities were thriving tourist destinations during this period.

By 2020, however, tourist numbers had plummeted across the board. London replaced Bangkok as the most visited city with 7.1 million tourists, compared to Bangkok's 6.7 million. Dubai saw 5.5 million visitors, slightly above Paris at 5.3 million. Tokyo recorded the fewest tourists at just 3.8 million. The most dramatic decline was in Bangkok, which lost nearly 69% of its tourists.

*(155 words)*

{{< /spoiler >}}

{{< spoiler "Speaking Part 2 Model" >}}

"I'd like to talk about a time I helped my elderly neighbor, Mrs. Tanaka, move some heavy furniture last winter.

She's a woman in her late seventies who lives alone next door. One evening, she knocked on my door looking quite distressed because she'd been trying to rearrange her living room and couldn't move her bookshelf by herself.

I immediately went over and spent about an hour helping her move the bookshelf, a small cabinet, and a dining table. While I was there, I also noticed that one of her ceiling lights had burned out, so I replaced that too. We ended up chatting over tea for a while afterward, and she told me about her grandchildren and showed me old family photos.

Honestly, I felt really good about it. It was such a small effort on my part, but it clearly meant a lot to her. It reminded me that sometimes the simplest acts of kindness can make someone's day. I also felt a bit sad that she had no one else to ask for help, and since then, I've made a point of checking in on her regularly."
