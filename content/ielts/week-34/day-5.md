## ✍️ Writing Test 2

### Task 1 (20 phút — ít nhất 150 từ)

**The pie charts below show the main sources of energy in two countries in 2010 and 2020.**

**Country A — 2010:**
| Source | Percentage |
|--------|-----------|
| Oil | 40% |
| Coal | 25% |
| Natural Gas | 20% |
| Nuclear | 10% |
| Renewables | 5% |

**Country A — 2020:**
| Source | Percentage |
|--------|-----------|
| Oil | 30% |
| Coal | 15% |
| Natural Gas | 22% |
| Nuclear | 12% |
| Renewables | 21% |

*Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.*

### Task 2 (40 phút — ít nhất 250 từ)

**Some people think that governments should invest more in public transportation, while others believe that building more roads is the better solution to traffic congestion. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.**

---

## ⚡ Checklist trước khi viết

### Task 1:
- [ ] Paraphrase đề bài (KHÔNG copy nguyên văn)
- [ ] Viết **overview** rõ ràng (xu hướng chung)
- [ ] So sánh số liệu nổi bật
- [ ] Dùng đa dạng: increased, rose, declined, remained stable...
- [ ] Kiểm tra đủ 150 từ

### Task 2:
- [ ] Trả lời **đúng** câu hỏi (discuss both + your opinion)
- [ ] Mỗi body paragraph có **topic sentence + example**
- [ ] Conclusion có **opinion rõ ràng**
- [ ] Dùng từ nối đa dạng
- [ ] Kiểm tra đủ 250 từ

## 🎤 Speaking Test 2

Ghi âm toàn bộ!

### Part 1: Introduction (4-5 phút)

**Topic: Entertainment**
1. What do you do in your free time?
2. Do you prefer watching movies at home or in a cinema?
3. What kind of music do you like?
4. Have your entertainment preferences changed since you were a child?

**Topic: Health**
5. Do you do any exercise regularly?
6. What do you think is the best way to stay healthy?
7. Do you think people in your country are generally healthy?
8. How important is sleep for health?

### Part 2: Long Turn (3-4 phút)

> **Describe a piece of advice that someone gave you.**
>
> You should say:
> - who gave you the advice
> - what the advice was
> - when they gave you this advice
>
> and explain whether you followed the advice and why.

**1 phút chuẩn bị → 2 phút nói**

### Part 3: Discussion (4-5 phút)

1. Do you think young people listen to advice from older people?
2. What kind of advice is most valuable — career, relationships, or health?
3. Should schools teach students how to give and receive advice?
4. Is it better to learn from your own mistakes or from others' advice?
5. Do you think people give too much unsolicited advice on social media?
