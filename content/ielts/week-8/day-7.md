## 📝 MINI TEST — Tuần 8

### Phần 1: Từ vựng (20 điểm)

**A. Chọn từ đúng (10 câu × 1 điểm)**

1. Tigers are ______ animals. Many of them have been killed.
   - a) extinct  b) endangered  c) sustainable

2. The ______ destroyed several villages last year.
   - a) recycling  b) earthquake  c) organic

3. We should ______ plastic bottles instead of throwing them away.
   - a) dump  b) pollute  c) recycle

4. The Amazon is the largest ______ in the world.
   - a) desert  b) rainforest  c) island

5. ______ energy comes from the sun.
   - a) Fossil  b) Solar  c) Toxic

6. A ______ is a large wave caused by an earthquake under the sea.
   - a) hurricane  b) tornado  c) tsunami

7. The ______ says it will rain tomorrow.
   - a) forecast  b) atmosphere  c) emission

8. Bring a ______ bag to the supermarket.
   - a) disposable  b) reusable  c) toxic

9. Scientists want to ______ the damaged coral reef.
   - a) destroy  b) collapse  c) restore

10. Your ______ footprint measures how much CO2 you produce.
    - a) carbon  b) ozone  c) fossil

### Phần 2: Ngữ pháp (30 điểm)

**A. Passive Voice — Chuyển sang bị động (5 câu × 2 điểm)**

1. People destroyed the forest. → The forest ______.
2. Volunteers cleaned the river. → The river ______.
3. The fire killed many animals. → Many animals ______.
4. Scientists discovered a new species. → A new species ______.
5. The government banned plastic bags. → Plastic bags ______.

**B. Conditionals — Hoàn thành câu (6 câu × 2 điểm)**

1. If you heat ice, it ______ (melt). [Zero]
2. If it rains tomorrow, I ______ (stay) home. [First]
3. If I ______ (be) the president, I would protect all forests. [Second]
4. If pollution ______ (increase), animals will lose their habitats. [First]
5. If you mix blue and yellow, you ______ (get) green. [Zero]
6. If everyone ______ (recycle), the world would be cleaner. [Second]

**C. Modals — Chọn modal đúng (4 câu × 1 điểm)**

1. The volcano is active. It ______ (will/might) erupt soon.
2. The forecast is certain: the storm ______ (will/may) arrive tonight.
3. Scientists are not sure — a tsunami ______ (will/might) follow.
4. The wildfire ______ (may/will) not reach our town, but we should be careful.

**D. Gerunds vs Infinitives — Điền dạng đúng (6 câu × 1 điểm)**

1. I enjoy ______ (walk) in the forest.
2. We need ______ (reduce) carbon emissions.
3. She decided ______ (buy) organic food.
4. Avoid ______ (use) disposable cups.
5. They plan ______ (install) solar panels.
6. Keep ______ (recycle) every day!

### Phần 3: Đọc hiểu (10 điểm)

Đọc đoạn văn sau và trả lời câu hỏi:

> Our planet faces many environmental problems. Forests **were destroyed** by **deforestation**, and many **species** became **endangered**. If **global warming** continues, **ice caps** will melt and **floods** will become more common.
>
> But there is hope. Many people enjoy **recycling** and want **to live** a greener **lifestyle**. **Volunteers** work to **restore** damaged **ecosystems**. If governments **invested** more in **renewable** **energy**, carbon **emissions** would decrease. A **tsunami** or **earthquake** **might** happen at any time, but if we **conserve** nature, we will be more prepared.
>
> Every person can make a difference. Consider **riding** a **bicycle**, **composting** food waste, and avoiding **disposable** products. Small **habits** create big changes!

*(100 từ)*

**Câu hỏi (5 câu × 2 điểm):**

1. What happened to forests because of deforestation?
2. What will happen if global warming continues? (Nêu 2 điều)
3. What do volunteers work to do?
4. What would happen if governments invested more in renewable energy?
5. Name 3 things every person can do to help.
