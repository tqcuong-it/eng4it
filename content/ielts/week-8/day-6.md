## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Gerunds vs Infinitives

### 📖 Gerunds và Infinitives là gì?

| Loại | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Gerund** | V + **-ing** | I enjoy **recycling**. |
| **Infinitive** | **to** + V | I want **to recycle**. |

Một số động từ đi với **gerund (-ing)**, một số đi với **infinitive (to V)**.

### 🔑 Động từ + Gerund (V-ing)

| Động từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **enjoy** | I enjoy **walking** in nature. |
| **avoid** | We should avoid **using** plastic. |
| **finish** | She finished **cleaning** the beach. |
| **keep** | Keep **recycling** every day. |
| **mind** | Do you mind **turning off** the light? |
| **consider** | Consider **buying** organic food. |
| **suggest** | I suggest **riding** a bicycle. |
| **practice** | Practice **saving** energy. |

### 🔑 Động từ + Infinitive (to V)

| Động từ | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **want** | I want **to reduce** waste. |
| **need** | We need **to conserve** water. |
| **decide** | She decided **to become** vegetarian. |
| **plan** | They plan **to install** solar panels. |
| **hope** | I hope **to live** a greener lifestyle. |
| **agree** | He agreed **to stop** using disposable cups. |
| **learn** | We must learn **to protect** the environment. |
| **promise** | I promise **to recycle** more. |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:**
> - **Enjoy, avoid, finish, keep, mind** → V-**ing** (những việc đang/đã làm)
> - **Want, need, decide, plan, hope** → **to** V (mong muốn, dự định tương lai)

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chọn Gerund hay Infinitive

1. I enjoy ______ (walk) in the forest.
2. We need ______ (reduce) our carbon footprint.
3. She decided ______ (buy) eco-friendly products.
4. Avoid ______ (use) disposable bags.
5. They plan ______ (install) solar panels.
6. Keep ______ (recycle) every week.
7. He wants ______ (live) a green lifestyle.
8. Consider ______ (ride) a bicycle to work.
9. I hope ______ (save) more energy this year.
10. She finished ______ (clean) the beach.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Hoàn thành câu

1. I suggest ______ (compost) food waste at home.
2. They agreed ______ (stop) using plastic bottles.
3. Do you mind ______ (turn off) the air conditioner?
4. We promised ______ (conserve) water.
5. She practices ______ (save) electricity every day.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. I enjoy to recycle plastic bottles.
2. We need reducing our emissions.
3. She decided buying organic food.
4. Avoid to use disposable cups.
5. He wants living an eco-friendly lifestyle.

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Fast fashion vs the environment](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2023-0105)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Many people want **to live** a greener **lifestyle**. They enjoy **riding** **bicycles** instead of driving cars. Some decide **to become** **vegetarian** to reduce their **carbon footprint**. Others plan **to install** **solar panels** on their homes.
>
> Small changes matter too. You can avoid **using** **disposable** cups. Consider **buying** **organic** and **eco-friendly** products. Keep **recycling** and **composting** food waste. If we practice **saving** **energy** and **conserving** water, our **habits** will help protect the planet for future generations.

*(90 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What do some people decide to become?
2. Why do they want to reduce their carbon footprint?
3. What can you avoid using?
4. What should we keep doing?
5. Who will benefit from our green habits?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Some people decide to become vegetarian. |
| 2 | To reduce emissions / help the environment. |
| 3 | We can avoid using disposable cups. |
| 4 | We should keep recycling and composting. |
| 5 | Future generations will benefit. |
