## 📋 Chiến lược: Bar Chart là gì?

**Bar Chart** (biểu đồ cột) dùng để **so sánh** giữa các đối tượng hoặc nhóm. Có 2 loại:

| Loại | Đặc điểm | Cách viết |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **Static** (1 thời điểm) | So sánh tại 1 thời điểm | Tập trung vào **so sánh** |
| **Dynamic** (nhiều thời điểm) | Thay đổi theo thời gian | Kết hợp **so sánh + xu hướng** |

### 3 bước phân tích Bar Chart

1. **Xác định loại** — Static hay Dynamic?
2. **Tìm điểm nổi bật** — Cao nhất, thấp nhất, chênh lệch lớn
3. **Nhóm dữ liệu** — Nhóm cột cao vs cột thấp, hoặc theo xu hướng

## 📝 Ngôn ngữ so sánh cho Bar Chart

### So sánh hơn (Comparatives)

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| **more... than** | The UK had **more** tourists **than** Japan. |
| **higher/lower than** | Sales were **higher than** the previous year. |
| **adj-er than** | The figure was **larger than** that of France. |
| **significantly/considerably more** | China spent **significantly more** than India. |
| **slightly/marginally higher** | Group A was **slightly higher** than Group B. |

### So sánh nhất (Superlatives)

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| **the highest/lowest** | The US had **the highest** number of visitors. |
| **the most/least** | Japan spent **the most** on education. |
| **the largest/smallest** | **The largest** proportion was for housing. |
| **by far the highest** | China's figure was **by far the highest**. |

### So sánh bằng & gần bằng

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| **as... as** | France was **as popular as** Spain. |
| **similar to** | The figure was **similar to** that of Germany. |
| **almost/nearly the same as** | Sales were **almost the same as** in 2019. |
| **twice/three times as much as** | The UK spent **twice as much as** Japan. |

### So sánh tỉ lệ

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| accounted for | Housing **accounted for** 35% of spending. |
| represented | Food **represented** the largest category. |
| comprised | Transport **comprised** 15% of the total. |
| made up | Healthcare **made up** a fifth of the budget. |

## 📝 Bài mẫu Band 6+

### Đề bài

*"The bar chart below shows the average monthly spending (in dollars) on four categories — food, transport, entertainment, and clothing — by people in three countries in 2022."*

**Dữ liệu:**

| Category | USA | UK | Japan |
|----------|-----|----|-------|
| Food | 500 | 400 | 450 |
| Transport | 350 | 300 | 200 |
| Entertainment | 250 | 200 | 150 |
| Clothing | 150 | 180 | 100 |

### Bài mẫu

> *The bar chart compares the average monthly expenditure on four different categories by consumers in the USA, the UK, and Japan in 2022.*
>
> *Overall, food was the largest area of spending in all three countries, while clothing represented the smallest. American consumers consistently spent more than their counterparts in the UK and Japan across all categories.*
>
> *In terms of food, Americans spent the most at $500 per month, followed by Japanese consumers at $450 and British consumers at $400. A similar pattern was observed for transport, where spending in the USA stood at $350, compared to $300 in the UK and just $200 in Japan.*
>
> *Regarding entertainment, the USA again had the highest expenditure at $250, which was $50 more than the UK and $100 more than Japan. Interestingly, clothing was the only category where British consumers ($180) spent more than Japanese consumers ($100), though both were lower than the American figure of $150. However, the UK figure of $180 was actually higher than the US figure for clothing.*

**Số từ:** 172 từ ✅

## 📝 Useful Phrases cho Bar Chart

### Mở đầu Body paragraph

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| In terms of... | **In terms of** food spending, the USA led. |
| Regarding... | **Regarding** transport, Japan spent the least. |
| With regard to... | **With regard to** entertainment... |
| As for... | **As for** clothing, the pattern differed. |
| Looking at... | **Looking at** the data for housing... |
| When it comes to... | **When it comes to** education spending... |

### Nêu số liệu

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| at + số | The USA spent **at $500**. |
| with + số | Japan, **with $450**, was the second highest. |
| at approximately | Food costs stood **at approximately** $400. |
| compared to / with | $500, **compared to** just $200 in Japan. |
| followed by | The USA led, **followed by** the UK and Japan. |
| respectively | The figures were 500, 400, and 300 **respectively**. |

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Phân tích dữ liệu

**Tỉ lệ (%) người dùng mạng xã hội theo độ tuổi, năm 2023:**

| Độ tuổi | Facebook | Instagram | TikTok |
|---------|----------|-----------|--------|
| 18-24 | 45 | 80 | 75 |
| 25-34 | 60 | 65 | 40 |
| 35-44 | 70 | 40 | 20 |
| 45+ | 75 | 20 | 10 |

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Mạng xã hội nào phổ biến nhất ở nhóm 18-24?
2. Xu hướng sử dụng Facebook theo tuổi là gì?
3. Viết 1 câu Overview cho biểu đồ này.

### Bài tập 2: Viết bài hoàn chỉnh

Dùng dữ liệu ở Bài tập 1, viết bài Task 1 hoàn chỉnh (≥150 từ).

### Bài tập 3: So sánh

Viết câu so sánh cho các tình huống sau:

1. USA: $500, Japan: $250 (gấp đôi)
2. UK: 40%, France: 42% (gần bằng)
3. China: 900 triệu, India: 300 triệu (gấp 3)
4. Germany: cao nhất trong các nước
5. Brazil: thấp nhất trong các nước

---

## ✅ Đáp án & Giải thích

### Bài tập 1

1. **Instagram** (80%) phổ biến nhất ở nhóm 18-24
2. Facebook **tăng theo tuổi** — càng lớn tuổi càng dùng nhiều (45% → 75%)
3. Gợi ý: *"Overall, Instagram and TikTok were most popular among younger age groups, while Facebook usage increased with age, being most favoured by those aged 45 and above."*

### Bài tập 3

1. *"Americans spent **twice as much as** Japanese consumers, at $500 compared to $250."*
2. *"The figures for the UK and France were **nearly identical**, at 40% and 42% respectively."*
3. *"China's figure was **three times higher than** that of India, at 900 million versus 300 million."*
4. *"Germany had **the highest** figure among all the countries surveyed."*
5. *"Brazil recorded **the lowest** figure of all the countries."*
