## 📋 Chiến lược: Sentence Completion

### Dạng này là gì?

Đề cho bạn **các câu chưa hoàn chỉnh** và yêu cầu điền từ/cụm từ từ passage để hoàn thành câu. Khác với Summary Completion — ở đây mỗi câu **độc lập**, không nằm trong một đoạn tóm tắt liên tục.

### Sentence Completion vs Summary Completion

| | Sentence Completion | Summary Completion |
|---|--------------------|--------------------|
| Format | Các câu riêng lẻ | Một đoạn tóm tắt liên tục |
| Thứ tự | Thường **theo thứ tự** trong bài | Có thể tóm tắt nhiều đoạn |
| Phạm vi | Mỗi câu từ 1 vùng cụ thể | Tóm tắt cả section |

### Chiến lược 4 bước

1. **Đọc câu cần hoàn thành** — gạch chân keywords, dự đoán loại từ cần điền
2. **Xác định vị trí** trong passage — dùng keywords để scan (câu hỏi theo thứ tự!)
3. **Đọc kỹ câu xung quanh** — tìm thông tin tương ứng
4. **Điền và kiểm tra** — đúng word limit? Đúng ngữ pháp? Đúng nghĩa?

> ⚠️ **Quan trọng:** Đáp án phải là từ **CHÍNH XÁC từ passage** — không được thay đổi dạng từ (ví dụ: bài viết "increased" → không được viết "increase")

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Sentence Completion Practice

Đọc passage:

> *The invention of the printing press by Johannes Gutenberg around 1440 is widely considered one of the most important events in human history. Before Gutenberg's press, books were copied by hand, a process that was extremely slow and expensive. A single book could take months to produce, and only wealthy individuals and religious institutions could afford them.*
>
> *Gutenberg's movable type system revolutionized the production of books. Individual metal letters could be arranged, used to print multiple copies, and then rearranged for different texts. This dramatically reduced the cost of producing books and made them accessible to a much wider audience. Within 50 years of the invention, an estimated 20 million volumes had been printed in Europe.*
>
> *The printing press had profound effects on society. It accelerated the spread of knowledge, contributed to the Renaissance and the Scientific Revolution, and played a crucial role in the Protestant Reformation. Martin Luther's 95 Theses, printed and distributed widely in 1517, reached audiences that would have been impossible to access through handwritten copies alone.*

**Complete the sentences below. Choose NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS from the passage.**

1. Before the printing press, books were produced by __________.
2. Gutenberg's system used individual __________ that could be rearranged.
3. Books became available to a __________ after the invention.
4. Approximately __________ had been printed within 50 years of the invention.
5. The printing press helped spread knowledge during the Renaissance and the __________.
6. Luther's 95 Theses were printed and __________ in 1517.

### Bài tập 2: Sentence Completion + Scanning Practice

Đọc passage:

> *Chocolate, one of the world's favourite foods, has a history stretching back over 3,000 years. The ancient Maya and Aztec civilizations in Central America were the first to cultivate cacao trees and process the beans into a bitter drink. The Aztec emperor Montezuma reportedly drank 50 cups of this chocolate beverage daily, believing it gave him energy and power.*
>
> *When Spanish explorers brought cacao beans to Europe in the 16th century, the drink was initially considered too bitter for European tastes. The addition of sugar and vanilla transformed it into a popular luxury drink among the European aristocracy. It remained exclusively a drink until 1847, when Joseph Fry created the first solid chocolate bar by mixing cocoa powder with sugar and cocoa butter.*
>
> *Today, the global chocolate industry is worth over $130 billion annually. Switzerland leads the world in chocolate consumption, with the average Swiss person eating approximately 10 kilograms per year. However, the industry faces growing concerns about sustainability, as cacao farming is linked to deforestation in West Africa, where over 70% of the world's cacao is produced.*

**Complete the sentences below. Choose NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS AND/OR A NUMBER from the passage.**

7. The Maya and Aztec people made cacao beans into a __________.
8. Montezuma believed chocolate gave him __________.
9. Europeans found the original chocolate drink too __________.
10. The first chocolate bar was made by __________ in 1847.
11. The chocolate industry generates more than __________ per year.
12. Swiss people consume about __________ of chocolate annually.
13. More than __________ of cacao comes from West Africa.

## 💡 Common Traps & Tips

1. **Bẫy thay đổi dạng từ:** Bài viết "increases" nhưng bạn viết "increased" → SAI
   - *Copy CHÍNH XÁC từ passage — kể cả số ít/nhiều, thì!*

2. **Bẫy từ thừa:** Điền nhiều hơn word limit
   - *Đếm từ! "a", "the", "and" ĐỀU tính là từ!*

3. **Bẫy thứ tự:** Câu 5 ở đoạn 3 nhưng bạn tìm ở đoạn 1
   - *Sentence Completion thường theo thứ tự — dùng điều này để locate nhanh!*

4. **Bẫy câu hỏi paraphrase:** Câu hỏi diễn đạt khác passage
   - *"The first person to create..." = passage nói "X created the first..."*

5. **Bẫy đáp án gần nhau:** 2 thông tin ở gần nhau, chọn nhầm
   - *Đọc lại CẢ câu hỏi + đáp án để chắc chắn khớp nghĩa!*
