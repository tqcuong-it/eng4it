## 📋 Chiến lược: Discussion Essay là gì?

### Nhận diện Discussion Essay

Discussion Essay xuất hiện khi đề bài yêu cầu:
- *"Discuss both views and give your opinion."*
- *"Some people think... while others believe... Discuss both sides."*

**Yêu cầu:** Bạn phải thảo luận **CẢ HAI quan điểm** rồi mới nêu ý kiến riêng.

### Cấu trúc chuẩn — 4 đoạn

```
📌 Introduction (40-50 từ)
   → Paraphrase 2 quan điểm
   → Thesis: nêu bạn nghiêng về phía nào

📌 Body 1 (80-100 từ)
   → Quan điểm 1 (phía bạn KHÔNG ủng hộ)
   → Giải thích + Ví dụ

📌 Body 2 (80-100 từ)
   → Quan điểm 2 (phía bạn ỦNG HỘ)
   → Giải thích + Ví dụ

📌 Conclusion (30-40 từ)
   → Tóm tắt cả 2 phía
   → Khẳng định quan điểm cá nhân
```

> 💡 **Mẹo quan trọng:** Đặt quan điểm bạn ỦNG HỘ ở **Body 2** — đoạn cuối cùng trước Conclusion sẽ để lại ấn tượng mạnh hơn!

### Khác biệt với Opinion Essay

| | Opinion Essay | Discussion Essay |
|---|---|---|
| Câu hỏi | "Do you agree or disagree?" | "Discuss both views" |
| Body 1 | Lý do 1 ủng hộ quan điểm | Quan điểm A (phía còn lại) |
| Body 2 | Lý do 2 ủng hộ quan điểm | Quan điểm B (phía bạn ủng hộ) |
| Quan trọng | Chỉ bàn 1 phía | **Phải bàn cả 2 phía** |

## 📝 Cụm từ hữu ích cho Discussion Essay

### Introduction

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Giới thiệu 2 phía | "There are differing opinions on whether..." |
| | "While some people argue that..., others believe that..." |
| Nêu quan điểm | "Although both sides have valid points, I personally believe that..." |
| | "In my view, ... is more convincing because..." |

### Body 1 — Quan điểm đối lập

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Mở đoạn | "On the one hand, those who support... argue that..." |
| | "Some people believe that... because..." |
| Giải thích | "The main reason for this view is that..." |
| Nhượng bộ | "While this argument has some merit, ..." |

### Body 2 — Quan điểm bạn ủng hộ

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Mở đoạn | "On the other hand, I believe that..." |
| | "However, those who disagree point out that..." |
| Nhấn mạnh | "A more compelling argument is that..." |
| | "What is more important is that..." |

### Conclusion

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Tóm tắt | "In conclusion, while both perspectives have their merits, ..." |
| Khẳng định | "I am more inclined to support the view that..." |

## 📖 Bài mẫu Band 6+

**Đề bài:** *"Some people think that children should be encouraged to study science subjects at school, while others believe that arts subjects are equally important. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*

---

**Introduction:**
> There is an ongoing debate about whether schools should prioritize science subjects or give equal importance to the arts. While both areas of study have clear benefits, I believe that a balanced education including both science and arts is the most beneficial approach.

**Body 1:**
> On the one hand, supporters of science education argue that scientific knowledge is essential in the modern world. Science subjects such as mathematics, physics, and biology help students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Furthermore, careers in science and engineering are often well-paid and in high demand. For instance, many countries are investing heavily in training scientists and engineers to drive economic growth. Therefore, focusing on science can provide students with practical skills for their future careers.

**Body 2:**
> On the other hand, I believe that arts subjects play an equally vital role in education. Studying music, literature, and visual arts helps children develop creativity and emotional intelligence, which are important qualities in all areas of life. For example, learning to play a musical instrument has been shown to improve concentration and memory in students. Moreover, arts education encourages self-expression and cultural awareness, helping young people become well-rounded individuals. Without exposure to the arts, children may miss out on developing important social and emotional skills.

**Conclusion:**
> In conclusion, although science subjects offer practical career benefits, I believe arts education is equally important for children's overall development. Schools should provide a balanced curriculum that includes both disciplines.

**Số từ:** ~260 từ ✅

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Phân biệt dạng essay

Xác định đây là **Opinion** hay **Discussion** essay:

1. *"Some people think that zoos are cruel, while others believe they serve an important purpose. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*
   → __________

2. *"Animals should not be kept in zoos. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"*
   → __________

3. *"Some believe that schools should focus on academic subjects. Others think physical education is just as important. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*
   → __________

### Bài tập 2: Viết Introduction + Conclusion

Cho đề bài: *"Some people think that the best way to learn a language is to live in the country where it is spoken, while others believe you can learn effectively in your own country. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*

Viết **Introduction** (40-50 từ) và **Conclusion** (30-40 từ).

### Bài tập 3: Viết essay hoàn chỉnh

⏱️ **Bấm giờ 40 phút:**

*"Some people believe that university graduates should work in the same field as their degree. Others think it does not matter. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Thảo luận cả 2 quan điểm?
- [ ] Có ý kiến cá nhân rõ ràng?
- [ ] Body 1 = quan điểm đối lập, Body 2 = quan điểm ủng hộ?
- [ ] Đủ 250+ từ?

## ✅ Đáp án gợi ý

### Bài tập 1

| Câu | Dạng | Giải thích |
|-----|------|-----------|
| 1 | Discussion | "Discuss both views and give your opinion" |
| 2 | Opinion | "To what extent do you agree or disagree?" |
| 3 | Discussion | "Discuss both views and give your opinion" |

### Bài tập 2 — Introduction mẫu

> *People have different opinions about the best way to learn a foreign language. While some argue that living abroad is the most effective method, others believe that learning at home can be equally successful. In my view, although living in a foreign country offers many advantages, it is not the only path to language proficiency.*

### Bài tập 2 — Conclusion mẫu

> *In conclusion, while immersion in a foreign country can accelerate language learning, I believe that with the right resources and dedication, learners can also achieve excellent results in their home country.*

## 💡 Tips cho Discussion Essay

1. **Phải bàn CẢ HAI phía** — nếu chỉ viết 1 phía → mất điểm Task Response
2. **Quan điểm cá nhân phải RÕ RÀNG** — đừng viết "both sides are equally important"
3. **Đặt phía bạn ủng hộ ở Body 2** — tạo ấn tượng mạnh trước Conclusion
4. **Dùng "On the one hand... On the other hand..."** — cấu trúc kinh điển
5. **Conclusion nhắc lại CẢ HAI phía** rồi mới khẳng định quan điểm
