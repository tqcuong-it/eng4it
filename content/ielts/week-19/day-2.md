## 📋 Chiến lược: Opinion Essay là gì?

### Nhận diện Opinion Essay

Opinion Essay xuất hiện khi đề bài hỏi:
- *"To what extent do you agree or disagree?"*
- *"Do you agree or disagree?"*
- *"What is your opinion?"*

**Yêu cầu:** Bạn phải nêu **quan điểm rõ ràng** và giải thích lý do.

### Cấu trúc chuẩn — 4 đoạn

```
📌 Introduction (40-50 từ)
   → Paraphrase đề bài
   → Thesis: "I completely/largely agree/disagree because..."

📌 Body 1 (80-100 từ)
   → Lý do 1 ủng hộ quan điểm
   → Giải thích + Ví dụ

📌 Body 2 (80-100 từ)
   → Lý do 2 ủng hộ quan điểm
   → Giải thích + Ví dụ

📌 Conclusion (30-40 từ)
   → Tóm tắt lại quan điểm
   → Khẳng định lại thesis
```

### 3 Cách trả lời

| Mức độ | Thesis | Khi nào dùng? |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Hoàn toàn đồng ý | "I completely agree that..." | 2 body đều ủng hộ |
| Đồng ý phần lớn | "I largely agree that..." | Body 1 ủng hộ, Body 2 nhượng bộ |
| Hoàn toàn không đồng ý | "I completely disagree that..." | 2 body đều phản đối |

## 📝 Cụm từ hữu ích cho Opinion Essay

### Introduction

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Paraphrase đề | "It is often argued that..." / "Many people believe that..." |
| Nêu quan điểm | "I completely agree with this view because..." |
| | "In my opinion, ... for two main reasons." |
| | "While some may disagree, I firmly believe that..." |

### Body Paragraphs

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Topic sentence | "The primary reason I agree is that..." |
| | "Another important factor is that..." |
| Giải thích | "This means that..." / "As a result, ..." |
| Ví dụ | "For example, ..." / "A clear example of this is..." |
| | "Research has shown that..." |

### Conclusion

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Tóm tắt | "In conclusion, I believe that..." |
| | "To sum up, the evidence suggests that..." |
| Khẳng định | "Therefore, I maintain my view that..." |

## 📖 Bài mẫu Band 6+

**Đề bài:** *"Some people think that parents should teach their children to manage money at a young age. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"*

---

**Introduction:**
> Many people believe that parents should educate their children about money management from an early age. I completely agree with this view because financial skills are essential for children's future success and independence.

**Body 1:**
> The primary reason I support this idea is that learning about money helps children develop responsibility. When children understand the value of money, they learn to make thoughtful decisions about spending and saving. For example, if a child receives pocket money and has to budget it for the week, they learn to prioritize their needs over their wants. This practical experience builds good habits that will benefit them throughout their lives.

**Body 2:**
> Another important reason is that early financial education prevents problems in adulthood. Many young adults struggle with debt because they were never taught how to manage their finances. Research has shown that people who learn about saving and budgeting as children are less likely to face serious financial difficulties later. Therefore, teaching money skills early can protect children from making costly mistakes in the future.

**Conclusion:**
> In conclusion, I firmly believe that parents should teach their children about money management at a young age. This helps develop responsible habits and prevents financial problems in adulthood.

**Số từ:** ~195 từ (body), tổng ~260 từ ✅

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Viết Introduction

Viết Introduction (2-3 câu, 40-50 từ) cho đề bài sau:

*"Some people think that all university students should study whatever they like. Others believe that they should only be allowed to study subjects that will be useful in the future. Discuss this view and give your opinion."*

⚠️ **Chú ý:** Đây KHÔNG phải Discussion essay! Câu hỏi cuối hỏi "give your opinion" → vẫn cần quan điểm rõ ràng.

### Bài tập 2: Viết Body Paragraph

Viết **1 Body Paragraph** (80-100 từ) cho đề bài:

*"Children should spend more time playing outdoors rather than using electronic devices. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"*

Sử dụng cấu trúc: **Topic Sentence → Explanation → Example**

### Bài tập 3: Viết essay hoàn chỉnh

⏱️ **Bấm giờ 40 phút** và viết essay hoàn chỉnh (250+ từ):

*"Some people think that competitive sports are good for children. Others believe that competition can be harmful. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"*

**Checklist trước khi nộp:**
- [ ] Có thesis statement rõ ràng?
- [ ] 4 đoạn: Intro + Body 1 + Body 2 + Conclusion?
- [ ] Mỗi body paragraph có example?
- [ ] Đủ 250+ từ?
- [ ] Kiểm tra lỗi grammar và spelling?

## ✅ Đáp án gợi ý

### Bài tập 1 — Introduction mẫu

> *There is a debate about whether university students should have the freedom to choose any subject or only study practical fields. In my opinion, while having some freedom is important, students should focus on subjects that are useful for their future careers.*

### Bài tập 2 — Body Paragraph mẫu

> *The most important reason I agree is that outdoor play is essential for children's physical health. When children play outside, they engage in physical activities such as running, climbing, and cycling, which help them develop strong muscles and maintain a healthy weight. For example, studies have found that children who spend at least one hour outdoors daily have a significantly lower risk of obesity compared to those who mainly use electronic devices. This shows that outdoor activities provide health benefits that screens simply cannot offer.*

## 💡 Tips cho Opinion Essay

1. **Chọn 1 phía và giữ nhất quán** — đừng đổi quan điểm giữa bài
2. **"I largely agree"** an toàn hơn "I completely agree" — cho phép bạn nhượng bộ ở Body 2
3. **Mỗi body paragraph = 1 ý chính** — đừng nhồi nhiều ý vào 1 đoạn
4. **Ví dụ không cần có số liệu thật** — "Research suggests..." hoặc "For instance..." là đủ
5. **Conclusion KHÔNG thêm ý mới** — chỉ tóm tắt lại
