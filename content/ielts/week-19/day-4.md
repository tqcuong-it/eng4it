## 📋 Chiến lược: Problem/Solution Essay là gì?

### Nhận diện Problem/Solution Essay

Đề bài thường hỏi:
- *"What are the problems and what solutions can you suggest?"*
- *"What are the causes of this? What measures could be taken?"*
- *"Why is this happening? How can this problem be solved?"*

**Yêu cầu:** Nêu **nguyên nhân/vấn đề** VÀ **giải pháp** — phải có CẢ HAI!

### Cấu trúc chuẩn — 4 đoạn

```
📌 Introduction (40-50 từ)
   → Paraphrase vấn đề
   → Preview: sẽ bàn về problems và solutions

📌 Body 1 — Problems/Causes (80-100 từ)
   → 2-3 vấn đề/nguyên nhân chính
   → Giải thích + Ví dụ

📌 Body 2 — Solutions (80-100 từ)
   → 2-3 giải pháp tương ứng
   → Giải thích + Kết quả mong đợi

📌 Conclusion (30-40 từ)
   → Tóm tắt problems + solutions
   → Nhấn mạnh tầm quan trọng của hành động
```

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Mỗi solution nên **tương ứng** với 1 problem — bài viết sẽ logic và mạch lạc hơn!

## 📝 Cụm từ hữu ích

### Nêu vấn đề

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Giới thiệu | "One of the main problems is that..." |
| | "A significant issue is..." |
| Nguyên nhân | "This is largely due to..." / "The root cause of this is..." |
| Hậu quả | "As a consequence, ..." / "This leads to..." |
| Thêm vấn đề | "Another serious concern is that..." |

### Đề xuất giải pháp

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Giới thiệu | "One effective solution would be to..." |
| | "To address this issue, ... could..." |
| Chính phủ | "Governments should implement policies that..." |
| Cá nhân | "Individuals can contribute by..." |
| Kết quả | "This would help to..." / "As a result, ..." |
| Thêm giải pháp | "In addition, ... could also..." |

## 📖 Bài mẫu Band 6+

**Đề bài:** *"In many countries, the amount of household waste is increasing. What are the causes of this? What solutions can you suggest?"*

---

**Introduction:**
> The volume of household waste has been rising significantly in many parts of the world. This essay will examine the main causes of this problem and suggest some practical solutions to address it.

**Body 1 — Problems:**
> One of the primary reasons for increasing household waste is the growth of consumer culture. People today tend to buy more products than they actually need, and many items are designed to be used once and thrown away. For example, fast fashion encourages people to purchase cheap clothing that is discarded after only a few uses. Another contributing factor is excessive packaging. Many products come wrapped in multiple layers of plastic, which ends up in household bins. As a consequence, the amount of non-recyclable waste continues to grow at an alarming rate.

**Body 2 — Solutions:**
> To address this issue, governments could introduce stricter regulations on packaging. For instance, they could require companies to use recyclable or biodegradable materials, which would significantly reduce plastic waste. In addition, public awareness campaigns could educate people about reducing, reusing, and recycling. Schools could also include environmental education in their curriculum to teach children responsible consumption habits from a young age. As a result, both producers and consumers would contribute to reducing household waste.

**Conclusion:**
> In conclusion, consumer culture and excessive packaging are the main causes of increasing household waste. By implementing stronger regulations and raising public awareness, this growing problem can be effectively addressed.

**Số từ:** ~260 từ ✅

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Brainstorm Problems & Solutions

Cho đề bài: *"Many people in cities suffer from stress. What are the causes? What solutions would you suggest?"*

Liệt kê **3 nguyên nhân** và **3 giải pháp tương ứng**:

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| 1. __________ | 1. __________ |
| 2. __________ | 2. __________ |
| 3. __________ | 3. __________ |

### Bài tập 2: Viết Body Paragraph (Solutions)

Dùng 2 giải pháp từ Bài tập 1, viết **1 Body Paragraph** (80-100 từ) về Solutions.

### Bài tập 3: Viết essay hoàn chỉnh

⏱️ **Bấm giờ 40 phút:**

*"Many young people today are choosing not to exercise regularly. What problems does this cause? What solutions can you suggest?"*

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Body 1 nêu rõ problems/causes?
- [ ] Body 2 đề xuất solutions cụ thể?
- [ ] Solutions tương ứng với problems?
- [ ] Đủ 250+ từ?

> *There are several solutions that could help reduce stress levels in urban areas. Firstly, companies should implement flexible working arrangements, such as remote work and shorter working hours, to give employees more time for rest and personal activities. Secondly, city governments could invest in building more parks and recreational facilities where residents can exercise and relax. Research has shown that spending time in green spaces significantly lowers stress levels. These measures would create a healthier and more balanced lifestyle for city dwellers.*

## 💡 Tips cho Problem/Solution Essay

1. **Phải có CẢ problems VÀ solutions** — thiếu 1 phần = mất điểm Task Response
2. **Solutions phải CỤ THỂ** — "the government should do something" là quá chung chung
3. **Nêu AI nên làm** — government, schools, individuals, companies?
4. **2-3 ý mỗi đoạn** là đủ — đừng liệt kê quá nhiều mà không giải thích
5. **Dùng "could/should"** cho solutions — tránh "must" (quá mạnh)
