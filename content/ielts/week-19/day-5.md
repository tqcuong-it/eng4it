## 📋 Chiến lược: Advantages/Disadvantages Essay

### Nhận diện

Đề bài thường hỏi:
- *"What are the advantages and disadvantages of...?"*
- *"Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?"*
- *"Is this a positive or negative development?"*

### 2 Biến thể quan trọng

| Biến thể | Câu hỏi | Bạn cần làm gì? |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| **Liệt kê** | "What are the advantages and disadvantages?" | Bàn cả 2 phía, KHÔNG cần chọn phe |
| **So sánh** | "Do advantages outweigh disadvantages?" | Bàn cả 2 phía + PHẢI chọn phe |

### Cấu trúc chuẩn — 4 đoạn

```
📌 Introduction (40-50 từ)
   → Paraphrase đề bài
   → Preview hoặc thesis (tùy biến thể)

📌 Body 1 — Advantages (80-100 từ)
   → 2 lợi ích chính + Giải thích + Ví dụ

📌 Body 2 — Disadvantages (80-100 từ)
   → 2 hạn chế chính + Giải thích + Ví dụ

📌 Conclusion (30-40 từ)
   → Tóm tắt + Quan điểm (nếu đề hỏi "outweigh")
```

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nếu đề hỏi "outweigh", đặt phía bạn ủng hộ ở **Body 2** (đoạn cuối trước Conclusion).

## 📝 Cụm từ hữu ích

### Nêu Advantages

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Mở đoạn | "There are several advantages to..." |
| | "One of the main benefits is that..." |
| Thêm ý | "Another significant advantage is..." |
| | "Furthermore, ... also offers the benefit of..." |
| Ví dụ | "For instance, ..." / "A good example of this is..." |

### Nêu Disadvantages

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Mở đoạn | "However, there are also some drawbacks." |
| | "On the other hand, ... has certain disadvantages." |
| Thêm ý | "A further concern is that..." |
| | "In addition, ... can also lead to..." |
| Nhượng bộ | "Despite these benefits, ..." |

### Conclusion (biến thể "outweigh")

| Chức năng | Cụm từ |
|-----------|--------|
| Chọn phe | "On balance, I believe the advantages outweigh the disadvantages." |
| | "Overall, while there are drawbacks, the benefits are more significant." |

## 📖 Bài mẫu Band 6+

**Đề bài:** *"More and more people are choosing to work from home. Do the advantages of this trend outweigh the disadvantages?"*

---

**Introduction:**
> In recent years, remote working has become increasingly popular. While this trend offers notable benefits, it also presents some challenges. In my opinion, the advantages of working from home outweigh the disadvantages.

**Body 1 — Advantages:**
> One of the most significant advantages of working from home is the flexibility it provides. Employees can manage their own schedules, which allows them to achieve a better work-life balance. For example, parents can spend more time with their children while still meeting their professional responsibilities. Additionally, remote workers save considerable time and money by eliminating the daily commute. Studies have shown that the average worker spends over an hour per day traveling to and from the office, and working from home allows this time to be used more productively.

**Body 2 — Disadvantages:**
> However, working from home also has some drawbacks. The most common problem is the feeling of isolation, as remote workers miss out on social interaction with colleagues. This can lead to loneliness and decreased motivation over time. Furthermore, it can be difficult to separate work and personal life when both take place in the same environment. Some people find that they work longer hours because there is no clear boundary between the office and home. Despite these concerns, many companies have found effective solutions, such as regular video meetings and flexible co-working arrangements.

**Conclusion:**
> In conclusion, although remote work can cause isolation and blurred boundaries, I believe the flexibility and time savings it offers make it a largely positive development. The advantages clearly outweigh the disadvantages.

**Số từ:** ~265 từ ✅

## 💡 Tips cho Advantages/Disadvantages Essay

1. **Đọc kỹ câu hỏi** — "What are..." vs "Do... outweigh..." → cách viết khác nhau!
2. **Mỗi body nên có 2 ý chính** — đủ để phát triển mà không quá dài
3. **Dùng "However" để chuyển đoạn** — từ Advantages sang Disadvantages
4. **Nếu phải chọn phe** → viết phía bạn ủng hộ DÀI HƠN một chút
5. **Đừng viết "There are many advantages and disadvantages"** — quá chung chung
