#### Phần 1: Từ vựng (10 điểm — mỗi câu 1 điểm)

Chọn đáp án đúng:

**1.** Education is ______ for children from age 6 to 16.
- a) flexible  b) compulsory  c) virtual  d) digital

**2.** She won a ______ to study at a top university.
- a) certificate  b) curriculum  c) scholarship  d) deadline

**3.** The ______ includes maths, science, and languages.
- a) dormitory  b) curriculum  c) platform  d) seminar

**4.** Many students feel ______ before exams.
- a) pressure  b) connectivity  c) curiosity  d) literacy

**5.** ______ learning allows students to study from home.
- a) Primary  b) Compulsory  c) Distance  d) Critical

**6.** Teachers ______ students through exams and essays.
- a) invest  b) evaluate  c) enroll  d) download

**7.** Good ______ and discipline help students succeed.
- a) distraction  b) attendance  c) connectivity  d) platform

**8.** He is doing ______ in biology at the university.
- a) revision  b) research  c) assessment  d) feedback

**9.** The teacher tries to ______ all students in the lesson.
- a) engage  b) upload  c) achieve  d) require

**10.** Education is everyone's ______.
- a) opportunity  b) challenge  c) benefit  d) responsibility

---

#### Phần 2: Ngữ pháp (20 điểm — mỗi câu 2 điểm)

**11.** You ______ study harder if you want to pass. (lời khuyên)
- a) must  b) should  c) can  d) wish

**12.** Students ______ wear uniforms at this school. (quy định bắt buộc)
- a) should  b) can  c) must  d) could

**13.** She ______ speak three languages fluently. (khả năng hiện tại)
- a) could  b) can  c) was able to  d) must

**14.** If I ______ harder, I would have passed the exam.
- a) studied  b) had studied  c) have studied  d) would study

**15.** I wish I ______ English better. (ước muốn hiện tại)
- a) speak  b) spoke  c) had spoken  d) will speak

**16.** ______ she studied hard, she didn't pass the exam.
- a) Unless  b) While  c) Although  d) Because

**17.** You won't improve ______ you practice every day.
- a) although  b) while  c) even though  d) unless

**18.** He ______ cheat in exams. (cấm)
- a) doesn't have to  b) mustn't  c) shouldn't  d) can't

**19.** If they had started earlier, they ______ on time.
- a) would finish  b) will have finished  c) would have finished  d) had finished

**20.** She wishes she ______ more time to study.
- a) has  b) had  c) will have  d) is having

---

#### Phần 3: Đọc hiểu (10 điểm — mỗi câu 2 điểm)

Đọc đoạn văn sau và trả lời câu hỏi:

> **Education** is one of the most important things in life. **Although** not everyone has equal **access** to good schools, governments **should** **invest** more in education. In many countries, **primary** and **secondary** education is **compulsory**. Students **must** attend school and **participate** in lessons. **While** traditional classrooms are still common, **online learning** has become very popular. It is **flexible** and **convenient**, but students need strong **motivation** and **discipline**. If all children **had had** **access** to **quality** education, the world **would have been** a better place. I **wish** every child **had** the **opportunity** to learn.

**21.** What should governments do?
**22.** What is compulsory in many countries?
**23.** What do students need for online learning?
**24.** What would have happened if all children had had access to quality education?
**25.** What does the writer wish?

---

#### Phần 4: Viết (10 điểm — mỗi câu 2 điểm)

Viết câu hoàn chỉnh:

**26.** Viết 1 câu dùng **should** về lời khuyên cho học sinh.
**27.** Viết 1 câu dùng **must** về quy định ở trường.
**28.** Viết 1 câu dùng **Third Conditional** về thi cử.
**29.** Viết 1 câu dùng **wish** về việc học.
**30.** Viết 1 câu dùng **although** hoặc **unless** về giáo dục.
