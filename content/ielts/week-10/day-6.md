## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Complex Sentences

### 📖 Complex Sentences là gì?

**Câu phức** (Complex Sentences) gồm **1 mệnh đề chính** + **1 mệnh đề phụ**, nối bằng các liên từ:

| Liên từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|--------|-------|
| **although** | mặc dù | **Although** he studied hard, he failed the exam. |
| **even though** | mặc dù (nhấn mạnh hơn) | **Even though** she was tired, she kept studying. |
| **while** | trong khi | **While** some students prefer lectures, others like self-study. |
| **unless** | trừ khi | You won't pass **unless** you study. |

### 🔑 Cấu trúc

| Vị trí mệnh đề phụ | Cấu trúc | Dấu phẩy? |
|---------------------|----------|-----------|
| Đầu câu | **Although** + mệnh đề phụ **,** mệnh đề chính. | ✅ Có dấu phẩy |
| Cuối câu | Mệnh đề chính + **although** + mệnh đề phụ. | ❌ Không dấu phẩy |

### Chi tiết từng liên từ

#### 1. Although / Even though (Mặc dù)
- Diễn đạt **sự tương phản** — hai điều trái ngược nhau
- **Even though** mạnh hơn **although**
- *Although education is important, many children cannot go to school.*
- *Even though he failed twice, he didn't give up.*

#### 2. While (Trong khi)
- Diễn đạt **sự đối lập** giữa hai ý
- *While some people learn fast, others need more time.*
- *While online learning is convenient, it has some disadvantages.*

#### 3. Unless (Trừ khi)
- Nghĩa = **If... not** (Nếu không...)
- *Unless you study hard, you will fail.* = *If you don't study hard, you will fail.*
- *She won't pass unless she revises.*

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chọn liên từ phù hợp

Điền **although, even though, while, unless**:

1. ______ she studied very hard, she didn't get the highest mark.
2. You won't improve ______ you practice every day.
3. ______ some students prefer online learning, others like going to school.
4. ______ he was sick, he still went to the exam.
5. ______ you read more, your vocabulary won't grow.
6. ______ the course was difficult, many students passed.
7. ______ education is free in some countries, it is expensive in others.
8. She won't get a scholarship ______ she gets very high marks.
9. ______ he had no money, he still wanted to go to university.
10. ______ technology helps learning, it can also be a distraction.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Nối câu

Nối hai câu thành một câu phức:

1. Education is expensive. / Many families still send their children to school. (although)
2. You don't revise. / You will fail the exam. (unless)
3. Some students like group work. / Others prefer working alone. (while)
4. She was very young. / She could already read. (even though)
5. He practises every day. / He will be able to pass the exam. (unless → viết lại)

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. Although she studied hard but she failed.
2. Unless you will study, you won't pass.
3. While education is important. Many people don't value it.
4. Even though he is tired, he keep studying.
5. She passed although didn't study much.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | Although / Even though | Tương phản — học chăm nhưng không đạt điểm cao nhất |
| 2 | unless | Trừ khi — điều kiện cần thiết |
| 3 | While | Đối lập — hai nhóm khác nhau |
| 4 | Even though | Tương phản mạnh — ốm vẫn đi thi |
| 5 | Unless | Trừ khi — nếu không đọc thì từ vựng không tăng |
| 6 | Although | Tương phản — khóa khó nhưng nhiều người đậu |
| 7 | While | Đối lập — miễn phí ở nước này, đắt ở nước khác |
| 8 | unless | Trừ khi — điều kiện để được học bổng |
| 9 | Even though | Tương phản mạnh — không tiền vẫn muốn học |
| 10 | While / Although | Đối lập hoặc tương phản đều OK |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Although education is expensive, many families still send their children to school. |
| 2 | Unless you revise, you will fail the exam. |
| 3 | While some students like group work, others prefer working alone. |
| 4 | Even though she was very young, she could already read. |
| 5 | Unless he practises every day, he won't be able to pass the exam. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | although...but | **Although** she studied hard, she failed. | Không dùng "but" sau "although" |
| 2 | will study | Unless you **study**, you won't pass. | Sau unless → Present Simple (không dùng will) |
| 3 | Hai câu riêng | **While** education is important**,** many people don't value it. | Phải nối thành 1 câu với dấu phẩy |
| 4 | keep | Even though he is tired, he **keeps** studying. | He → keeps (thêm -s) |
| 5 | although didn't | She passed although **she** didn't study much. | Cần có chủ ngữ "she" |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - The importance of education](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2024-0815)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Education is **essential** for building a better world. **Although** not everyone has equal access to **quality** schools, many countries are working to change this. Governments should **invest** more in education and **provide** better **resources** for teachers. **While** some people believe traditional schools are best, others see the **benefits** of online learning. Teachers play a **significant** role — they **encourage** students, **develop** their skills, and help them **achieve** their goals. **Unless** we **support** education for all, many children will lose the **opportunity** to build a better future. Education is everyone's **responsibility**.

*(90 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What is essential for building a better world?
2. What should governments do?
3. What role do teachers play?
4. What will happen unless we support education for all?
5. Whose responsibility is education?

| 3 | They encourage students, develop their skills, and help them achieve their goals. |

| 5 | It is everyone's responsibility. |
