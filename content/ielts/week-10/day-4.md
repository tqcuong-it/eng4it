## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Third Conditional

### 📖 Third Conditional là gì?

**Third Conditional** dùng để nói về **điều không có thật trong quá khứ** — tưởng tượng nếu quá khứ khác đi thì kết quả sẽ khác:
- 🕐 *If I **had studied** harder, I **would have passed** the exam.* (Nếu tôi đã học chăm hơn, tôi đã đậu kỳ thi rồi.)
- 💭 Thực tế: Tôi KHÔNG học chăm → tôi KHÔNG đậu.

### 🔑 Cấu trúc

| Phần | Cấu trúc |
|------|----------|
| Mệnh đề IF | If + S + **had** + V3 (past participle) |
| Mệnh đề chính | S + **would have** + V3 (past participle) |

| Loại câu | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|
| Khẳng định | If she **had revised**, she **would have passed**. |
| Phủ định | If he **hadn't cheated**, he **wouldn't have failed**. |
| Câu hỏi | **Would** you **have passed** if you **had studied** more? |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:** Third Conditional = **HỐI TIẾC** về quá khứ! Dùng khi bạn muốn nói "giá mà..."

### So sánh 3 loại Conditional

| Loại | Dùng khi | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|
| First (Type 1) | Có thể xảy ra (tương lai) | If I study, I **will** pass. |
| Second (Type 2) | Không có thật (hiện tại) | If I studied, I **would** pass. |
| **Third (Type 3)** | **Không có thật (quá khứ)** | If I **had** studied, I **would have** passed. |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chia động từ đúng (Third Conditional)

1. If she ______ (study) harder, she ______ (pass) the exam.
2. If I ______ (not/forget) my notes, I ______ (do) better on the test.
3. If they ______ (prepare) earlier, they ______ (not/feel) so much pressure.
4. If he ______ (read) the questions carefully, he ______ (not/make) so many mistakes.
5. If we ______ (have) more time, we ______ (finish) the essay.
6. If she ______ (not/cheat), she ______ (not/fail) the course.
7. If the teacher ______ (explain) better, the students ______ (understand) the lesson.
8. If I ______ (know) about the test, I ______ (revise) more.
9. If he ______ (get) better marks, he ______ (receive) a scholarship.
10. If they ______ (start) revision earlier, their results ______ (be) better.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Viết câu Third Conditional

Viết câu Third Conditional cho các tình huống sau:

1. Tôi không ôn thi → tôi trượt. → If I had...
2. Cô ấy đến muộn → cô ấy lỡ bài kiểm tra. → If she hadn't...
3. Anh ấy không hỏi giáo viên → anh ấy không hiểu bài. → If he had...
4. Chúng tôi không làm bài nhóm → chúng tôi không được điểm tốt. → If we had...
5. Bạn ấy gian lận → bạn ấy bị đuổi học. → If he hadn't...

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. If I had study harder, I would have passed.
2. If she had prepared, she would passed the test.
3. If they would have studied, they had passed.
4. If he hadn't cheated, he would have not failed.
5. If I had knew the answer, I would have written it.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | had studied / would have passed | Third Conditional chuẩn |
| 2 | hadn't forgotten / would have done | Phủ định ở mệnh đề IF |
| 3 | had prepared / wouldn't have felt | Phủ định ở mệnh đề chính |
| 4 | had read / wouldn't have made | Phủ định ở mệnh đề chính |
| 5 | had had / would have finished | had had (had + V3 của have) |
| 6 | hadn't cheated / wouldn't have failed | Cả hai phủ định |
| 7 | had explained / would have understood | Third Conditional chuẩn |
| 8 | had known / would have revised | Third Conditional chuẩn |
| 9 | had got / would have received | Third Conditional chuẩn |
| 10 | had started / would have been | Third Conditional chuẩn |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | If I had revised, I would have passed the exam. |
| 2 | If she hadn't arrived late, she wouldn't have missed the test. |
| 3 | If he had asked the teacher, he would have understood the lesson. |
| 4 | If we had done the group work, we would have got a good mark. |
| 5 | If he hadn't cheated, he wouldn't have been expelled. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | had study | If I **had studied** harder... | had + V3 (studied) |
| 2 | would passed | ...she **would have passed** the test. | would have + V3 |
| 3 | would have studied / had passed | If they **had studied**, they **would have passed**. | Sai thứ tự cấu trúc |
| 4 | would have not | ...he **wouldn't have** failed. | Phủ định: wouldn't have + V3 |
| 5 | had knew | If I **had known** the answer... | had + V3 (known, không phải knew) |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Exams](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2024-0502)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> **Exams** are an important part of education. Every year, millions of students **prepare** for **tests** and hope to get good **marks**. Some students feel a lot of **pressure** and worry about their **results**. Good **revision** is the key to success. If students **had** started studying earlier, they **would have felt** less stressed. Teachers give **feedback** on **essays** and **assessments** to help students improve their **performance**. Unfortunately, some students **cheat**, but this always leads to serious problems. Getting a good **score** and a **certificate** opens many doors for the future.

*(85 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What do millions of students do every year?
2. What is the key to success in exams?
3. What would have happened if students had started studying earlier?
4. Why do teachers give feedback?
5. What does getting a good score open?
