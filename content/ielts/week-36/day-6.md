## Part 1: Top 50 Grammar Errors — Sửa ngay!

### A. Subject-Verb Agreement (10 lỗi)

Sửa lỗi trong mỗi câu:

1. "The number of students who study abroad **have** increased." → __________
2. "Each of the candidates **are** qualified." → __________
3. "The news **are** shocking." → __________
4. "Neither the teacher nor the students **was** happy." → __________
5. "A variety of options **is** available." → __________

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
1. has increased 2. is qualified 3. is shocking 4. were happy 5. are available
{{< /spoiler >}}

### B. Article Usage (10 lỗi)

Thêm a/an/the hoặc Ø (không cần article):

6. "___ education is important for ___ future of ___ country."
7. "I went to ___ university in ___ London."
8. "___ rich should help ___ poor."
9. "She plays ___ piano every ___ evening."
10. "___ Mount Everest is ___ highest mountain in ___ world."

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
6. Ø / the / a (hoặc the) 7. a / Ø 8. The / the 9. the / Ø 10. Ø / the / the
{{< /spoiler >}}

### C. Tense Errors (10 lỗi)

Chọn thì đúng:

11. The graph shows that sales (increase / have increased / increased) **since 2010**.
12. By 2050, the population (will reach / will have reached / reaches) 10 billion.
13. Currently, many people (are working / work / worked) from home.
14. The percentage (rose / has risen / rises) sharply **in 2015**.
15. If the government (invests / invested / will invest) more, education will improve.

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
11. have increased 12. will have reached 13. work (hoặc are working) 14. rose 15. invests
{{< /spoiler >}}

### D. Preposition Errors (10 lỗi)

Điền preposition đúng:

16. The increase ___ the number of cars...
17. According ___ the chart...
18. This results ___ higher pollution.
19. People depend ___ public transportation.
20. There is a demand ___ better services.

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
16. in 17. to 18. in 19. on 20. for
{{< /spoiler >}}

### E. Common Writing Mistakes (10 lỗi)

Sửa lỗi:

21. "Nowaday, people use internet a lot." → __________
22. "The chart is about how much people spending." → __________
23. "I am agree with this opinion." → __________
24. "There are many advantages, for example, save time." → __________
25. "In my opinion, I think that education is important." → __________

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
21. "Nowadays, people use the internet a lot."
22. "The chart shows how much people spend/are spending."
23. "I agree with this opinion."
24. "...for example, it saves time." (hoặc "such as saving time")
25. "In my opinion, education is important." (bỏ "I think" — redundant)
{{< /spoiler >}}

## Part 3: Bài tập tổng hợp

### Exercise 1: Điền collocation đúng (10 câu)

1. Climate change __________ a serious __________ to biodiversity. (pose/threat)
2. The government should __________ measures to reduce unemployment. (take)
3. There was a __________ increase in population between 2000 and 2010. (sharp/gradual/slight)
4. Education plays a __________ role in economic development. (crucial)
5. __________ my perspective, technology has improved our quality of life. (From)
6. The benefits of exercise far __________ the drawbacks. (outweigh)
7. It is essential to __________ a balance between work and leisure. (strike)
8. Fossil fuels __________ for 80% of global energy consumption. (account)
9. Many countries have __________ significant changes in recent decades. (undergone)
10. People should __________ awareness of environmental issues. (raise)

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
1. poses / threat 2. take 3. sharp 4. crucial 5. From 6. outweigh 7. strike 8. account 9. undergone 10. raise
{{< /spoiler >}}

### Exercise 2: Sửa lỗi grammar (10 câu)

Tìm và sửa lỗi trong mỗi câu:

1. "The amount of people using social media have risen."
2. "She suggested me to apply for the job."
3. "Despite of the rain, we went outside."
4. "The informations in the report is useful."
5. "He is more taller than his brother."
6. "I look forward to hear from you."
7. "The company has been established since 20 years."
8. "Everyone should follows the rules."
9. "She asked me where did I live."
10. "This is the most unique design."

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
1. "The **number** of people... **has** risen."
2. "She suggested **that I apply** for the job." (hoặc "She suggested applying...")
3. "**Despite** the rain..." (bỏ "of")
4. "The **information**... **is** useful." (không đếm được)
5. "He is **taller** than his brother." (bỏ "more")
6. "I look forward to **hearing** from you."
7. "...has been established **for** 20 years."
8. "Everyone should **follow** the rules."
9. "She asked me where **I lived**."
10. "This is a **unique** design." (unique không dùng most)
{{< /spoiler >}}
