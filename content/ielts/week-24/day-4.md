## 📋 Chuẩn bị

- 🎤 Thiết bị ghi âm (điện thoại OK)
- ⏱️ Đồng hồ bấm giờ
- 📝 Giấy bút (cho Part 2)
- 🤫 Phòng yên tĩnh
- ⏰ Tổng thời gian: **11-14 phút**

### Quy tắc:
1. **Ghi âm từ đầu đến cuối** — không dừng
2. **Bấm giờ nghiêm ngặt** theo từng Part
3. **Đọc to câu hỏi** rồi trả lời (giả lập giám khảo đọc)
4. **Không chuẩn bị trước** — phản xạ tự nhiên

## 🎤 SPEAKING TEST — Bắt đầu!

---

### Part 1: Introduction & Interview (4-5 phút)

*20-30 giây mỗi câu*

**Chủ đề 1: Home**
1. Do you live in a house or an apartment?
2. What do you like most about where you live?
3. Is there anything you would like to change about your home?

**Chủ đề 2: Food & Cooking**
4. Do you enjoy cooking?
5. What kind of food do you like to eat?
6. Do you think it's important to eat healthy food?

**Chủ đề 3: Holidays**
7. Do you prefer to relax or be active on holidays?
8. Where did you go on your last holiday?
9. What's your ideal holiday destination?

---

### Part 2: Individual Long Turn (3-4 phút)

**⏱️ Chuẩn bị: 1 phút** | **Nói: 1-2 phút**

> **Describe a time when you helped someone.**
>
> You should say:
> - who you helped
> - what you helped them with
> - why they needed help
>
> and explain how you felt about helping this person.

**Follow-up:**
- Do you often help other people?
- Do you think helping others is important?

---

### Part 3: Discussion (4-5 phút)

*30-45 giây mỗi câu*

1. *Why do some people enjoy helping others while some don't?*
2. *Do you think people are less willing to help strangers nowadays compared to the past?*
3. *Should schools teach children about the importance of helping others?*
4. *How do you think volunteering will change in the future?*
5. *Is it better to give money or give time to help people in need?*

---

## 📝 Model Answers (Tham khảo sau khi làm bài)

### Part 1 — Mẫu câu 6:

> "Yes, **I think** it's extremely important to eat healthy food. **The main reason is that** what we eat directly affects our energy levels and overall health. **For instance**, since I started eating more vegetables and less processed food, I've noticed that I feel much more energetic during the day. **That said**, I think it's also okay to enjoy treats occasionally — it's all about balance."

**Phân tích:** Opinion → Reason → Example → Balanced view ✅

### Part 2 — Mẫu:

> "I'd like to talk about a time when I helped my neighbor move to a new apartment. This was about six months ago. She's an elderly woman who lives alone, and she needed to relocate because her building was being renovated.
>
> I helped her pack her belongings and carried boxes to the moving truck. It took almost a whole day because she had lived there for over twenty years and had accumulated quite a lot of things.
>
> She needed help because she couldn't manage all the heavy lifting on her own, and her family lives in another city. I think she was also feeling a bit overwhelmed by the whole process of moving.
>
> Honestly, I felt really good about helping her. **Although** it was tiring, seeing her relief and gratitude made it completely worthwhile. It reminded me that sometimes small acts of kindness can make a huge difference in someone's life."

### Part 3 — Mẫu câu 2:

> "That's an interesting question. **I tend to think** that people are **somewhat** less willing to help strangers today, **although** it's not entirely true.
>
> **One key reason** is that life has become more fast-paced, **which means that** people often feel too busy to stop and help. **On top of that**, there's sometimes a fear of being taken advantage of.
>
> **However**, I **wouldn't say** kindness has disappeared. **For example**, during natural disasters, we often see communities come together to support each other. **It's possible that** the way people help has simply **changed** — nowadays, people **might** donate online rather than help in person."

## ✅ Rubric tự chấm điểm

Nghe lại bản ghi âm và chấm:

| Tiêu chí | 1-3 (Yếu) | 4-5 (TB) | 6 (Khá) | 7+ (Tốt) | Điểm |
|-----------|-----------|----------|---------|----------|:----:|
| **Fluency** | Dừng rất nhiều, câu ngắn | Nói được nhưng hay ngập ngừng | Nói khá trôi chảy | Tự nhiên, ít dừng | /9 |
| **Vocabulary** | Rất cơ bản | Đủ dùng, hay lặp | Đa dạng, ít lặp | Phong phú, chính xác | /9 |
| **Grammar** | Toàn câu đơn, nhiều lỗi | Có câu phức nhưng sai nhiều | Mix đơn + phức, ít lỗi | Câu phức tự nhiên | /9 |
| **Pronunciation** | Khó hiểu | Hiểu được, accent nặng | Rõ ràng, đôi chỗ sai | Tự nhiên, ngữ điệu tốt | /9 |
