## 📊 PART 1: GRAMMAR (20 câu)

**Chọn đáp án đúng (A, B, C hoặc D):**

**1.** She ______ to the gym every morning.
- A. go  B. goes  C. going  D. is go

**2.** Look! The baby ______ !
- A. smiles  B. smile  C. is smiling  D. smiled

**3.** They ______ a new house last year.
- A. buy  B. buyed  C. bought  D. have bought

**4.** I ______ never ______ sushi before I came to Japan.
- A. have / eaten  B. had / eaten  C. was / eating  D. did / eat

**5.** While she ______ dinner, the phone rang.
- A. cooks  B. cooked  C. was cooking  D. has cooked

**6.** We ______ visit our friends next weekend.
- A. will  B. are  C. do  D. have

**7.** You ______ drive faster. The speed limit is 60 km/h.
- A. can't  B. shouldn't  C. mustn't  D. won't

**8.** If it rains tomorrow, we ______ at home.
- A. stay  B. stayed  C. will stay  D. would stay

**9.** If I ______ a bird, I would fly around the world.
- A. am  B. was  C. were  D. be

**10.** If she had studied harder, she ______ the exam.
- A. passed  B. would pass  C. will pass  D. would have passed

**11.** English ______ in many countries around the world.
- A. speaks  B. is spoken  C. is speaking  D. spoke

**12.** The new bridge ______ last month.
- A. built  B. was built  C. is built  D. has built

**13.** She said that she ______ tired.
- A. is  B. was  C. be  D. been

**14.** He told me that he ______ call me the next day.
- A. will  B. would  C. can  D. shall

**15.** The man ______ lives next door is a doctor.
- A. which  B. where  C. who  D. whose

**16.** This is the restaurant ______ we had our first date.
- A. who  B. which  C. where  D. that

**17.** You ______ wear a helmet when riding a motorcycle. It's the law!
- A. should  B. might  C. must  D. could

**18.** She ______ swim when she was three years old.
- A. can  B. could  C. must  D. should

**19.** I have lived in this city ______ ten years.
- A. since  B. for  C. ago  D. from

**20.** He ______ already ______ lunch, so he's not hungry.
- A. has / eaten  B. had / eaten  C. is / eating  D. was / eating

## 📖 PART 3: READING (10 câu)

**Đọc đoạn văn và trả lời câu hỏi:**

### Passage: Public Libraries in the Modern World

Public libraries have been important community spaces for hundreds of years. In the past, they were mainly places where people went to borrow books. However, modern libraries have changed significantly and now offer much more than just books.

Today, many libraries provide free internet access, computer workstations, and digital resources such as e-books and online databases. Some libraries also offer classes in various subjects, including language courses, computer skills, and even cooking. These programs help people of all ages continue learning throughout their lives.

Libraries also serve as social spaces where community members can meet and connect. Many libraries have meeting rooms that local groups can use for free. Children's reading programs and story hours are popular activities that encourage young people to develop a love of reading from an early age.

Despite the rise of the internet and digital media, libraries remain relevant and important. A recent survey found that 65% of adults in the United States had visited a public library in the past year. Many people still prefer reading physical books, and libraries provide equal access to information for everyone, regardless of their income level.

However, libraries face challenges too. Government funding for libraries has decreased in many countries, forcing some to reduce their hours or close branches. Library supporters argue that investing in libraries is investing in education and community development.

*(223 từ)*

**Questions 31-35: True / False / Not Given**

**31.** Modern libraries only offer books for borrowing.

**32.** Some libraries provide free cooking classes.

**33.** Libraries are the most popular community spaces in the world.

**34.** 65% of US adults visited a library in the past year.

**35.** Government funding for libraries has increased recently.

**Questions 36-40: Chọn đáp án đúng**

**36.** What is the main idea of the passage?
- A. Libraries are no longer useful  B. Libraries have evolved and remain important  C. All libraries will close soon  D. People don't read anymore

**37.** What do modern libraries offer besides books?
- A. Only internet access  B. Internet, classes, and digital resources  C. Only e-books  D. Nothing new

**38.** Why are children's reading programs important?
- A. They help children learn to cook  B. They encourage a love of reading  C. They teach computer skills  D. They provide free food

**39.** What challenge do libraries face?
- A. Too many visitors  B. Too many books  C. Decreased government funding  D. No internet access

**40.** The word "relevant" in paragraph 4 is closest in meaning to:
- A. old-fashioned  B. important and useful  C. expensive  D. boring
