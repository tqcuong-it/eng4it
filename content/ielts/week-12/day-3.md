## 📊 Phần 1: Passive Voice (Câu Bị Động)

### Công thức chung

**S + be + V3/ed (+ by + tác nhân)**

### Chuyển đổi theo thì

| Thì | Active | Passive |
|-----|--------|---------|
| Present Simple | She **writes** a letter. | A letter **is written** (by her). |
| Present Continuous | She **is writing** a letter. | A letter **is being written**. |
| Past Simple | She **wrote** a letter. | A letter **was written**. |
| Past Continuous | She **was writing** a letter. | A letter **was being written**. |
| Future Simple | She **will write** a letter. | A letter **will be written**. |
| Present Perfect | She **has written** a letter. | A letter **has been written**. |

### Khi nào dùng?

- Khi **không biết** ai thực hiện hành động: The window **was broken**.
- Khi **muốn nhấn mạnh** đối tượng chịu tác động: English **is spoken** worldwide.
- Trong **văn viết trang trọng**, báo cáo, tin tức

## 📊 Phần 2: Reported Speech (Câu Tường Thuật)

### Quy tắc lùi thì

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|--------------|----------------|
| Present Simple → | Past Simple |
| Present Continuous → | Past Continuous |
| Past Simple → | Past Perfect |
| will → | would |
| can → | could |
| may → | might |

### Đổi đại từ & trạng từ

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| I / we | he / she / they |
| today | that day |
| tomorrow | the next day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| here | there |
| now | then |
| this | that |

### Ví dụ

- **Direct:** "I **am** happy," she said.
- **Reported:** She said (that) she **was** happy.

- **Direct:** "I **will call** you tomorrow," he said.
- **Reported:** He said (that) he **would call** me the next day.

## 📊 Phần 3: Relative Clauses (Mệnh Đề Quan Hệ)

### Đại từ quan hệ

| Từ | Thay cho | Ví dụ |
|----|---------|-------|
| **who** | người (chủ ngữ) | The man **who** lives next door is friendly. |
| **whom** | người (tân ngữ) | The woman **whom** I met was kind. |
| **which** | vật | The book **which** I bought is interesting. |
| **that** | người/vật | The car **that** he drives is new. |
| **where** | nơi chốn | The restaurant **where** we ate was great. |
| **when** | thời gian | I remember the day **when** we first met. |
| **whose** | sở hữu | The girl **whose** bag was stolen cried. |

### Defining vs Non-defining

| Loại | Đặc điểm | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Defining** | Cần thiết, không có dấu phẩy | The man **who called** is my brother. |
| **Non-defining** | Thêm thông tin, có dấu phẩy | My brother, **who lives in London**, called me. |

## ✏️ 15 Bài Tập Tổng Hợp

**A. Passive Voice — Chuyển sang câu bị động (1-5):**

**1.** They build new houses every year.
→ New houses ______________________.

**2.** Someone stole my bicycle yesterday.
→ My bicycle ______________________.

**3.** The teacher is explaining the lesson.
→ The lesson ______________________.

**4.** They will open a new school next year.
→ A new school ______________________.

**5.** She has written three books.
→ Three books ______________________.

**B. Reported Speech — Chuyển sang câu tường thuật (6-10):**

**6.** "I am tired," she said.
→ She said ______________________.

**7.** "We will visit you tomorrow," they said.
→ They said ______________________.

**8.** "I can't find my keys," he said.
→ He said ______________________.

**9.** "I bought a new car yesterday," she told me.
→ She told me ______________________.

**10.** "Do you like coffee?" he asked me.
→ He asked me ______________________.

**C. Relative Clauses — Điền who/which/where/whose/when (11-15):**

**11.** The woman ______ lives next door is a teacher.

**12.** This is the book ______ I told you about.

**13.** That's the restaurant ______ we had dinner last week.

**14.** The boy ______ father is a doctor won the prize.

**15.** I remember the summer ______ we went to the beach every day.

## ✅ Đáp Án

**Passive Voice:**

| # | Đáp án |
|---|--------|
| 1 | New houses **are built** every year. |
| 2 | My bicycle **was stolen** yesterday. |
| 3 | The lesson **is being explained** by the teacher. |
| 4 | A new school **will be opened** next year. |
| 5 | Three books **have been written** (by her). |

**Reported Speech:**

| # | Đáp án |
|---|--------|
| 6 | She said (that) she **was** tired. |
| 7 | They said (that) they **would** visit me/us the next day. |
| 8 | He said (that) he **couldn't** find his keys. |
| 9 | She told me (that) she **had bought** a new car the day before. |
| 10 | He asked me **if/whether** I **liked** coffee. |

**Relative Clauses:**

| # | Đáp án |
|---|--------|
| 11 | who |
| 12 | which / that |
| 13 | where |
| 14 | whose |
| 15 | when |

## 💡 Mẹo ôn tập

1. **Passive:** Tìm tân ngữ trong câu chủ động → đưa lên đầu câu bị động
2. **Reported Speech:** Nhớ quy tắc "lùi 1 thì" + đổi đại từ
3. **Relative Clauses:** Xác định từ được bổ nghĩa là người/vật/nơi/thời gian → chọn đại từ phù hợp

> 🎯 **Tự đánh giá:** 12-15 đúng → Giỏi lắm! 9-11 → Khá, cần ôn phần yếu nhất. Dưới 9 → Đừng lo, quay lại bài gốc rồi thử lại! 💪
