## 📚 Tổng Hợp Từ Vựng Theo Chủ Đề

### Tuần 1: Chào Hỏi & Cơ Bản
**Từ khóa:** hello, goodbye, please, thank you, name, work, live, go, eat, drink, read, write, speak, listen, understand, learn, like, want, need, have

### Tuần 2: Nơi Chốn & Di Chuyển
**Từ khóa:** hospital, station, airport, bank, restaurant, bridge, turn left/right, straight, opposite, between, bus, train, taxi, bicycle, apartment, kitchen, bedroom, bathroom

### Tuần 3: Gia Đình & Con Người
**Từ khóa:** parents, siblings, husband, wife, daughter, son, uncle, aunt, cousin, neighbor, tall, short, friendly, kind, honest, patient, cheerful, appearance, personality, relationship

### Tuần 4: Thức Ăn & Sức Khỏe
**Từ khóa:** ingredient, recipe, dessert, appetizer, nutrition, protein, vitamin, allergy, headache, fever, cough, medicine, exercise, diet, symptom, treatment, appointment, pharmacy

### Tuần 5: Công Việc & Thói Quen
**Từ khóa:** schedule, deadline, colleague, salary, promotion, resign, meeting, report, presentation, experience, responsibility, routine, commute, overtime, break, interview, application

### Tuần 6: Du Lịch & Khám Phá
**Từ khóa:** destination, accommodation, luggage, passport, boarding pass, reservation, itinerary, sightseeing, tourist, souvenir, culture, adventure, guide, landscape, monument, explore

### Tuần 7: Giáo Dục & Học Tập
**Từ khóa:** curriculum, assignment, scholarship, tuition, lecture, semester, graduate, diploma, research, knowledge, certificate, exam, campus, library, major, department, academic

### Tuần 8: Môi Trường & Thiên Nhiên
**Từ khóa:** pollution, climate, recycle, renewable, deforestation, endangered, ecosystem, drought, flood, earthquake, atmosphere, conservation, wildlife, habitat, greenhouse, sustainable

### Tuần 9: Truyền Thông & Công Nghệ
**Từ khóa:** broadcast, journalist, headline, advertisement, social media, digital, download, upload, network, privacy, software, device, application, communication, information, artificial

### Tuần 10: Văn Hóa & Xã Hội
**Từ khóa:** tradition, ceremony, celebration, festival, custom, heritage, community, generation, diversity, equality, religion, population, urban, rural, volunteer, charity, identity

### Tuần 11: Kinh Tế & Tài Chính
**Từ khóa:** economy, inflation, budget, investment, profit, loss, tax, loan, debt, savings, income, expense, currency, market, trade, import, export, unemployment, consumer, industry

## 💡 Cách ôn từ vựng hiệu quả

1. **Đọc qua danh sách** — đánh dấu từ nào bạn quên
2. **Che nghĩa** — nhìn từ tiếng Anh, nói nghĩa tiếng Việt
3. **Đặt câu** — mỗi từ quên, viết 1 câu ví dụ
4. **Nhóm theo chủ đề** — não nhớ tốt hơn khi có liên kết

## ✏️ 20 Bài Tập Điền Từ

**Chọn từ thích hợp điền vào chỗ trống:**

*Từ gợi ý: schedule, destination, pollution, salary, ingredient, scholarship, luggage, symptom, tradition, budget, appointment, renewable, accommodation, headline, community, currency, commute, heritage, sustainable, ceremony*

**1.** I need to make an ______ with the dentist next week.

**2.** What is the main ______ in this soup?

**3.** The ______ of the newspaper says "Economy Growing Fast."

**4.** She received a ______ to study at a top university.

**5.** Air ______ is a serious problem in many big cities.

**6.** Our travel ______ is Paris — the City of Light!

**7.** He ______ to work by train every morning. It takes 40 minutes.

**8.** My monthly ______ is not enough to cover all expenses.

**9.** Don't forget to pack your ______ the night before the flight.

**10.** The wedding ______ was beautiful and very emotional.

**11.** We need to find ______ for our trip — a hotel or Airbnb.

**12.** Solar energy is a ______ source of power.

**13.** A cough is a common ______ of a cold.

**14.** The local ______ organized a clean-up event in the park.

**15.** Eating local food is part of Japanese ______.

**16.** We must create a ______ before we start spending money.

**17.** The ______ in Japan is the yen (¥).

**18.** I have a very busy ______ this week — meetings every day!

**19.** The government wants to protect cultural ______ sites.

**20.** We need ______ development to protect the environment for future generations.
