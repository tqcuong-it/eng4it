## 📖 Mini Reading Test (20 phút — 13 câu)

Đọc **1 passage** (chưa làm bao giờ), tập trung vào:
- [ ] Skim trước (2 phút max)
- [ ] T/F/NG: phân biệt FALSE vs NOT GIVEN
- [ ] Quản lý thời gian (max 20 phút)
- [ ] Không bỏ trống

**Passage practice — Technology and Privacy:**

As digital technology becomes increasingly woven into the fabric of daily life, concerns about personal privacy have grown exponentially. Every smartphone interaction, online purchase, and social media post generates data that can be collected, analyzed, and monetized by corporations. A recent study estimated that the average internet user generates approximately 1.7 megabytes of data every second.

Governments around the world are responding with varying approaches to data protection. The European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), implemented in 2018, has become a global benchmark for privacy legislation. It gives individuals the right to access, correct, and delete their personal data held by organizations. Companies that violate GDPR can face fines of up to 4% of their annual global revenue.

In contrast, the United States lacks a comprehensive federal privacy law. Instead, privacy protections are provided through a patchwork of sector-specific regulations and state laws. California's Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is among the strongest, but its protections do not extend to residents of other states. This fragmented approach has been criticized by privacy advocates who argue it leaves significant gaps in consumer protection.

The tension between privacy and innovation is particularly evident in the healthcare sector. Electronic health records, wearable devices, and health apps collect vast amounts of sensitive personal information. While this data can improve medical research and patient outcomes, unauthorized access or data breaches could have devastating consequences for individuals.

**Questions 1-5: T/F/NG**

1. Digital technology generates data from all online activities. → __________
2. GDPR was the first privacy law ever created. → __________
3. GDPR fines can reach 4% of a company's global revenue. → __________
4. The US has a comprehensive federal privacy law. → __________
5. California's privacy law protects all US residents. → __________

**Questions 6-9: Multiple Choice**

6. The average internet user generates data at a rate of:
   - A) 1.7 gigabytes per second
   - B) 1.7 megabytes per second
   - C) 1.7 kilobytes per second

7. GDPR gives individuals the right to:
   - A) access only their data
   - B) access, correct, and delete their data
   - C) sell their data

8. The US approach to privacy is described as:
   - A) comprehensive
   - B) fragmented
   - C) non-existent

9. In healthcare, data concerns include:
   - A) only data breaches
   - B) unauthorized access and data breaches
   - C) too much research

**Questions 10-13: Summary Completion**

The EU's (10) __________ regulation has set a global standard for privacy. Companies violating it face significant (11) __________. The US relies on (12) __________ regulations rather than one federal law. In healthcare, (13) __________ records collect sensitive information.

{{< spoiler "Đáp án" >}}
1. TRUE 2. NOT GIVEN 3. TRUE 4. FALSE 5. FALSE
6. B 7. B 8. B 9. B
10. GDPR 11. fines 12. sector-specific 13. electronic health
{{< /spoiler >}}

## ✍️ Mini Writing Test (25 phút)

Viết **1 bài Task 2 hoàn chỉnh** trong 25 phút (ít hơn 15 phút so với thi thật):

**"Some people believe that social media has brought people closer together, while others argue it has made people more isolated. Discuss both views and give your own opinion."**

### Checklist khi viết:
- [ ] Plan trước (3 phút)
- [ ] Introduction có thesis statement
- [ ] Body 1: view 1 + example
- [ ] Body 2: view 2 + example
- [ ] Conclusion có opinion
- [ ] Dùng linking words đa dạng
- [ ] Grammar phức: relative clauses, conditionals, passive
- [ ] Ít nhất 250 từ

## 🎤 Mini Speaking Test (10 phút)

Ghi âm toàn bộ:

**Part 1 (3 phút):** Trả lời 4 câu hỏi, mỗi câu 20-30 giây
1. Do you prefer spending time indoors or outdoors?
2. What's your favorite way to relax?
3. Do you enjoy learning new things?
4. What skill would you like to learn in the future?

**Part 2 (3 phút):** 1 phút prep + 2 phút nói
> Describe a goal you want to achieve in the near future.

**Part 3 (4 phút):** 2 câu, mỗi câu 45-60 giây
1. Why do some people find it hard to achieve their goals?
2. Do you think setting goals is important for success?

### Checklist:
- [ ] Ít filler (um/uh)
- [ ] Part 2 đủ 2 phút
- [ ] Part 3 dùng OREO method
- [ ] Phát âm rõ ràng
