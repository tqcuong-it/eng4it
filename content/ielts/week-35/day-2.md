## Option A: Listening — Section 3-4 khó

### Vấn đề: Không nghe kịp nội dung học thuật

**Chiến lược cải thiện:**

1. **Nghe chủ động (Active Listening):**
   - Nghe 1 đoạn TED Talk 5 phút
   - Pause mỗi 30 giây → tóm tắt bằng 1 câu
   - Lặp lại 3 lần, mỗi lần pause ít hơn

2. **Dictation Practice:**
   Nghe và viết lại chính xác:
   
   a) "The research demonstrates that **environmental** factors play a significantly greater role in **cognitive** development than previously **acknowledged**."
   
   b) "Participants were randomly **assigned** to either the **experimental** or **control** group, with **demographic** variables carefully **matched**."
   
   c) "The findings suggest a strong **correlation** between socioeconomic status and academic **achievement**, though **causation** cannot be definitively **established**."

3. **Prediction Practice:**
   Đọc câu hỏi → dự đoán đáp án TRƯỚC khi nghe:
   
   *"The professor says the main advantage of the new method is __________."*
   - Dự đoán: efficiency? accuracy? cost reduction? speed?
   - Nghe → kiểm tra dự đoán

**Bài tập:** Làm 2 sections (Section 3 + 4) từ Cambridge IELTS. Ghi chú mỗi câu sai: **tại sao sai?**

## Option B: Reading — T/F/NG Accuracy

### Vấn đề: Nhầm FALSE với NOT GIVEN

**Quy tắc vàng:**
- **FALSE:** Bài đọc nói **ngược lại** (contradiction)
- **NOT GIVEN:** Bài đọc **không nói gì** về vấn đề này

**Bài tập — 10 câu mini:**

Đoạn văn: *"The museum was established in 1952 and contains over 10,000 artifacts. It is open every day except Mondays and receives approximately 500,000 visitors annually."*

1. The museum was founded in the 20th century. → __________
2. The museum has exactly 10,000 artifacts. → __________
3. The museum is closed on Mondays. → __________
4. The museum is the most popular in the country. → __________
5. The museum receives over a million visitors per year. → __________

{{< spoiler "Đáp án + giải thích" >}}
1. TRUE — 1952 = 20th century ✓
2. FALSE — "over 10,000" ≠ "exactly 10,000" (contradiction)
3. TRUE — "open every day except Mondays" = closed on Mondays ✓
4. NOT GIVEN — không có thông tin so sánh với museum khác
5. FALSE — "approximately 500,000" ≠ "over a million" (contradiction)
{{< /spoiler >}}

**Thêm 5 câu — Đoạn văn 2:**

*"Dr. Sarah Chen has been researching artificial intelligence for 15 years at Cambridge University. Her team recently published a paper on machine learning applications in healthcare, which has been cited over 200 times."*

6. Dr. Chen has worked at Cambridge for 15 years. → __________
7. Her research focuses on AI. → __________
8. The paper was published last year. → __________
9. The paper has been influential in the field. → __________
10. Dr. Chen's team has 10 members. → __________

{{< spoiler "Đáp án + giải thích" >}}
6. NOT GIVEN — Bà ấy researching AI 15 năm, nhưng không nói ở Cambridge 15 năm
7. TRUE — "researching artificial intelligence"
8. NOT GIVEN — "recently" không có nghĩa là "last year"
9. NOT GIVEN — "cited over 200 times" nhưng bài không nói "influential"
10. NOT GIVEN — không có thông tin về số thành viên team
{{< /spoiler >}}

## Option C: Writing — Task Achievement

### Vấn đề: Viết không đủ ý / thiếu overview / lạc đề

**Exercise 1: Overview Writing Drill**

Viết overview (2-3 câu) cho mỗi dataset:

**Data 1:** CO2 emissions: China (10B tons), USA (5B), India (2.5B), Russia (1.5B), Japan (1.1B)

**Data 2:** University enrollment by gender: 1990 (Male 60%, Female 40%) → 2020 (Male 45%, Female 55%)

**Data 3:** Transport mode to work: Car (65%), Bus (15%), Train (10%), Bicycle (5%), Walk (5%)

{{< spoiler "Bài mẫu overview" >}}
**Data 1:** "Overall, China produces by far the most CO2 emissions, accounting for approximately twice as much as the USA, the second-largest emitter. The remaining three countries contribute relatively small proportions."

**Data 2:** "Overall, there was a significant shift in university enrollment patterns, with female students overtaking males over the 30-year period. By 2020, women made up the majority of university students."

**Data 3:** "Overall, private cars are the dominant mode of transport to work, used by nearly two-thirds of commuters. Public transport and active modes (cycling and walking) account for the remaining third."
{{< /spoiler >}}

**Exercise 2: Task 2 — Trả lời đúng câu hỏi**

Đọc đề → viết **thesis statement** (1 câu) cho mỗi đề:

1. "Some people think children should start school at age 7. Others say age 4 is better. **Discuss both views and give your opinion.**"

2. "The main purpose of education is to prepare people for work. **To what extent do you agree or disagree?**"

3. "Many cities are facing serious air pollution. **What are the causes? What solutions can you suggest?**"

> ⚠️ Chú ý: Mỗi loại đề cần cách trả lời KHÁC NHAU!

## Option D: Speaking — Fluency & Coherence

### Vấn đề: Nói ngắn / dừng nhiều / không mạch lạc

**Exercise 1: 1-Minute Speaking**

Nói **1 phút liên tục** (không dừng >2 giây) về mỗi chủ đề:
1. Your favorite season
2. A recent purchase you made
3. Something you're good at

Ghi âm → đếm số lần dừng → luyện lại giảm dần.

**Exercise 2: STAR Method cho Part 2**

Sử dụng **STAR** để cấu trúc câu trả lời:
- **S**ituation: Tình huống
- **T**ask: Nhiệm vụ/vấn đề
- **A**ction: Bạn làm gì
- **R**esult: Kết quả

**Cue card:** "Describe a challenge you faced and overcame."
→ Ghi âm 2 phút sử dụng STAR.

**Exercise 3: Extending Answers**

Biến câu trả lời ngắn thành dài:

Short: "I like reading."
Long: "I'm quite passionate about reading, actually. I try to read for at least 30 minutes before bed every night. Recently, I've been really into historical fiction — there's something fascinating about learning about the past through stories. My favorite book last year was probably..."

Luyện mở rộng 5 câu trả lời Part 1.
