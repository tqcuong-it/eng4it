## 📱 Topic 1: Technology

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: How often do you use your phone?**

> To be honest, I use my phone pretty much all day long. I mainly use it for messaging, checking social media, and taking photos. I know I should probably cut down on my screen time, but it's become such an essential part of daily life that it's hard to put it away!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Honesty:** thừa nhận dùng nhiều → chân thực
- **Collocations:** "cut down on screen time", "essential part of daily life"
- **Self-awareness:** "I know I should probably..." — trưởng thành

---

**Q2: What technology do you use every day?**

> Apart from my phone, I use my laptop every day for work and studying. I also rely heavily on apps like Google Maps for navigation and translation apps when I need to look something up. I think technology has made our lives so much more convenient.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Variety:** phone → laptop → apps — liệt kê đa dạng
- **Expression:** "rely heavily on" — collocation mạnh
- **Opinion:** kết thúc bằng nhận xét chung

---

**Q3: Do you think technology is good for society?**

> Well, I think it's a double-edged sword, to be honest. On the one hand, technology has brought incredible benefits — better healthcare, faster communication, access to information. On the other hand, it can lead to problems like addiction and privacy concerns. So I'd say the key is finding the right balance.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Idiom:** "double-edged sword" — nâng điểm Lexical Resource
- **Structure:** "On the one hand... On the other hand..." — coherent
- **Conclusion:** "finding the right balance" — kết luận hay

---

**Q4: Is there any technology you don't like using?**

> I'm not a huge fan of smartwatches, actually. I know they're popular, but I find them a bit distracting — I don't need constant notifications on my wrist. I prefer keeping things simple with a regular watch. Maybe I'm a bit old-fashioned in that way!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Humor:** "Maybe I'm a bit old-fashioned" — nhẹ nhàng, dễ thương
- **Reason:** "distracting", "constant notifications" — lý do cụ thể

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Technology

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| cut down on screen time | giảm thời gian màn hình | I should cut down on screen time |
| rely heavily on | phụ thuộc nhiều vào | I rely heavily on my phone |
| a double-edged sword | con dao hai lưỡi | Technology is a double-edged sword |

## ✈️ Topic 2: Travel

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: Do you like travelling?**

> Absolutely! I love travelling — it's one of my favourite things to do. There's something exciting about exploring new places, trying local food, and meeting people from different cultures. Even short trips to nearby cities make me feel refreshed and inspired.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Enthusiasm:** "Absolutely!" — thể hiện passion
- **List of 3:** "exploring, trying, meeting" — rhythm tự nhiên
- **Range:** trips lớn + nhỏ đều đề cập

---

**Q2: Where was the last place you visited?**

> The last place I visited was Da Lat, a beautiful mountain city. I went there with my family last summer. What I loved most about it was the cool weather and the stunning scenery — there were pine forests and flower gardens everywhere. We also tried some amazing local coffee. It was such a memorable trip!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Narrative:** kể chuyện mạch lạc — where, when, who, what
- **Descriptive:** "stunning scenery", "pine forests", "flower gardens"
- **Feeling:** "memorable trip" — kết thúc cảm xúc

---

**Q3: Do you prefer travelling alone or with others?**

> I'd say I prefer travelling with friends or family because it's more fun to share experiences with others. You know, those moments when you discover a hidden restaurant or get lost together — those become the best memories. Having said that, I can see the appeal of solo travel too, for the freedom it gives you.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Preference + concession:** "Having said that..." — balanced
- **Vivid examples:** "hidden restaurant", "get lost together" — cụ thể

---

**Q4: Where would you like to travel in the future?**

> I've always dreamed of visiting Japan, actually. I'm really fascinated by the culture — the traditional temples, the food, the cherry blossoms. I'd also love to experience the contrast between modern cities like Tokyo and the peaceful countryside. Hopefully I'll get the chance to go one day!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Dream language:** "I've always dreamed of..." — cảm xúc
- **Specific details:** temples, food, cherry blossoms, Tokyo
- **Hope:** "Hopefully..." — ending tự nhiên

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Travel

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| explore new places | khám phá nơi mới | I love exploring new places |
| stunning scenery | cảnh đẹp tuyệt vời | The scenery was absolutely stunning |
| a memorable trip | chuyến đi đáng nhớ | It was such a memorable trip |
| get lost | bị lạc | We got lost but it was fun |
| hidden gem | viên ngọc ẩn | We found a hidden gem restaurant |
| the appeal of | sức hấp dẫn của | I can see the appeal of solo travel |
| be fascinated by | bị cuốn hút bởi | I'm fascinated by Japanese culture |

## 👫 Topic 3: Friends

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: Do you have many friends?**

> I'd say I have a few close friends rather than a large circle. I've known my best friends since high school, and even though we don't see each other very often anymore, we still keep in touch regularly through messaging. I think quality is more important than quantity when it comes to friendships.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Distinction:** "close friends" vs "large circle" — nuance
- **Timeline:** "since high school" — Present Perfect
- **Opinion:** "quality > quantity" — kết luận hay

---

**Q2: What do you usually do with your friends?**

> When we get together, we usually go out for a meal or grab coffee somewhere. Sometimes we go to the cinema or just hang out at someone's house and chat for hours. We also enjoy going on short trips together — we try to plan one every few months.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Variety:** meals, coffee, cinema, hanging out, trips — nhiều hoạt động
- **Phrasal verbs:** "go out for", "grab coffee", "hang out" — tự nhiên

---

**Q3: Do you prefer spending time with friends or alone?**

> Honestly, I need both. I'm quite sociable, so I enjoy being around friends — laughing, sharing stories, that kind of thing. But I also value my alone time because it helps me recharge. I'd say I need a good balance between socialising and having time to myself.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Balance:** cả hai → trưởng thành
- **Vocabulary:** "sociable", "recharge", "value my alone time"
- **Expression:** "that kind of thing" — informal, tự nhiên

---

**Q4: How do you make new friends?**

> To be honest, I'm not the type of person who makes friends easily. I tend to be a bit shy at first. But once I find common interests with someone, I open up quite quickly. I've also made some friends through hobbies — for example, I met some great people at a photography class last year.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Self-awareness:** "I'm not the type..." — chân thực
- **Process:** shy → common interests → open up — narrative
- **Example:** photography class — cụ thể

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Friends

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| a close friend | bạn thân | I have a few close friends |
| keep in touch | giữ liên lạc | We keep in touch via messaging |
| hang out | đi chơi, ở cùng | We hang out on weekends |
| grab coffee | đi uống cà phê | Let's grab coffee sometime |
| common interests | sở thích chung | We share common interests |
| open up | cởi mở | I open up quite quickly |
| quality over quantity | chất hơn lượng | I value quality over quantity |

1. "How has technology changed the way you communicate?"
2. "Tell me about your best holiday."

### Bài 2: Vocabulary Upgrade

Thay thế các từ đơn giản bằng từ nâng cao:

| Câu gốc | Nâng cấp |
|----------|----------|
| "I like travelling very much" | → ? |
| "My friend is nice" | → ? |
| "Technology is useful" | → ? |

**Gợi ý:**
- "I'm absolutely passionate about travelling"
- "My friend is incredibly supportive and warm-hearted"
- "Technology has become indispensable in modern life"

### Bài 3: Record & Compare

Thu âm trả lời **2 câu hỏi bất kỳ**. Sau đó:
1. Nghe lại → đếm số lần ngập ngừng
2. Ghi lại những từ bạn lặp lại nhiều
3. Thử trả lời lại lần 2 → so sánh sự cải thiện

## 💡 Tip của ngày

> **The Rule of 3:** Khi liệt kê, hãy nói **3 items** — không ít hơn (quá ngắn), không nhiều hơn (quá dài):
>
> ✅ "I enjoy **reading, cooking, and going for walks**."
> ✅ "Technology helps with **communication, education, and entertainment**."
>
> 3 items tạo **rhythm tự nhiên** và nghe rất professional! 🎵
