## 🏠 Topic 1: Home / Accommodation

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: Where do you live?**

> Well, I currently live in a small apartment in the city centre. It's quite convenient because it's close to my workplace, and there are lots of shops and restaurants nearby. I've been living there for about two years now.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Filler:** "Well" — tạo thời gian suy nghĩ tự nhiên
- **Từ vựng:** "convenient", "nearby" — không lặp lại "close"
- **Mở rộng:** lý do (convenient) + chi tiết (shops, restaurants) + thời gian (two years)

---

**Q2: Do you like your home?**

> Yes, I do. Actually, I'm quite happy with my current place. The main reason is that it gets a lot of natural light, which makes it feel really warm and cozy. The only downside is that it's a bit small, but overall I think it's a comfortable place to live.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Filler:** "Actually" — tự nhiên, không gượng
- **Cấu trúc:** "The main reason is that..." — complex sentence
- **Balance:** nói cả ưu điểm + nhược điểm → tự nhiên hơn

---

**Q3: What's your favourite room?**

> I'd say my favourite room is the living room. It's where I spend most of my free time — I usually watch movies or read books there in the evening. It has a big window with a nice view, so it's really relaxing.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Opening:** "I'd say..." — cách nói tự nhiên thay vì "My favourite room is..."
- **Chi tiết:** hoạt động cụ thể (watch movies, read books) + đặc điểm (big window, nice view)

---

**Q4: Would you like to move to a different place?**

> To be honest, I'm thinking about moving to a bigger apartment in the future. I'd love to have an extra room that I could use as a study. But for now, I'm happy where I am because the location is really convenient.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Filler:** "To be honest" — tạo sự chân thành
- **Grammar:** "I'd love to have" (conditional), "that I could use" (relative clause)
- **Cấu trúc:** tương lai + hiện tại → câu trả lời cân bằng

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Home

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| in the suburbs | ở ngoại ô | I live in the suburbs |
| in the city centre | ở trung tâm | My flat is in the city centre |
| a cozy apartment | căn hộ ấm cúng | It's a cozy little apartment |
| natural light | ánh sáng tự nhiên | The room gets lots of natural light |
| a nice view | view đẹp | There's a nice view from the balcony |
| convenient location | vị trí thuận tiện | The location is really convenient |
| the only downside | nhược điểm duy nhất | The only downside is the noise |

## 💼 Topic 2: Work

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: Do you work or are you a student?**

> I'm currently working. I have a full-time job at a trading company. I've been working there for about three years now, and I'm mainly responsible for handling client accounts.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Present Perfect Continuous:** "I've been working" — thể hiện thời gian
- **Từ vựng:** "full-time job", "responsible for", "handling" — chuyên nghiệp

---

**Q2: What do you like about your job?**

> What I enjoy most about my job is the teamwork. I get to work with people from different backgrounds, and we often brainstorm ideas together, which I find really stimulating. Also, the working hours are quite flexible, so I can manage my time well.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Opening:** "What I enjoy most..." — cấu trúc emphasis, nâng điểm grammar
- **Linking:** "Also" — thêm ý thứ hai tự nhiên
- **Từ vựng:** "stimulating", "flexible", "brainstorm" — đa dạng

---

**Q3: Is there anything you dislike about your job?**

> Well, I suppose the most challenging thing is the workload. Sometimes there are tight deadlines, and I have to work overtime to finish everything on time. But I think that's pretty common in most jobs, so I try not to complain too much.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Hedging:** "I suppose" — nói giảm, lịch sự
- **Từ vựng:** "workload", "tight deadlines", "work overtime" — collocations mạnh
- **Balance:** nhận xét + chấp nhận → trưởng thành

---

**Q4: What kind of job would you like to do in the future?**

> In the future, I'd like to start my own business. I've always been interested in entrepreneurship, and I think it would give me more freedom and flexibility. Of course, it's risky, but I believe it's worth trying.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Conditional:** "I'd like to", "it would give me" — nói về tương lai lịch sự
- **Linking:** "Of course... but..." — balance risk + ambition

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Work

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| full-time/part-time job | công việc toàn/bán thời gian | I have a full-time job |
| be responsible for | chịu trách nhiệm về | I'm responsible for marketing |
| tight deadline | deadline gấp | We often have tight deadlines |
| work overtime | làm thêm giờ | I sometimes work overtime |
| flexible hours | giờ linh hoạt | The working hours are flexible |
| teamwork | làm việc nhóm | I enjoy the teamwork |
| start my own business | mở công ty riêng | I'd like to start my own business |

## 📚 Topic 3: Study

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: What are you studying?**

> I'm studying Business Administration at university. I chose this major because I've always been interested in how companies operate and make decisions. It's a pretty broad field, so I get to learn about marketing, finance, and management.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Lý do:** "because I've always been interested in..." — mở rộng tự nhiên
- **Chi tiết:** liệt kê cụ thể (marketing, finance, management)

---

**Q2: Do you enjoy studying?**

> Honestly, it depends on the subject. I really enjoy practical courses like case studies because I can apply what I learn to real situations. However, I find some theoretical subjects a bit dry and boring. But overall, I think studying is a valuable experience.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Nuance:** "it depends on..." — câu trả lời không đen trắng → tự nhiên
- **Contrast:** "However" — linking word nối ý đối lập
- **Từ vựng:** "practical", "theoretical", "dry and boring", "valuable"

---

**Q3: How do you usually study?**

> I usually study by making notes and reviewing them regularly. I find that writing things down helps me remember better. I also like to study with my classmates because we can explain difficult concepts to each other, which is really helpful.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Method:** cụ thể (making notes, reviewing, study with classmates)
- **Reason:** "because we can explain..." — mở rộng ý

---

**Q4: Would you like to study abroad?**

> Definitely! I've always dreamed of studying abroad, especially in an English-speaking country. I think it would be an amazing opportunity to improve my language skills and experience a different culture. The only concern is the cost, but I'd love to get a scholarship.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Enthusiasm:** "Definitely!" — thể hiện cảm xúc, tự nhiên
- **Conditional:** "it would be", "I'd love to" — grammar đa dạng
- **Balance:** mong muốn + lo ngại → thực tế

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Study

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| major in | chuyên ngành | I major in Business |
| practical/theoretical | thực hành/lý thuyết | I prefer practical courses |
| make notes | ghi chép | I study by making notes |
| review regularly | ôn tập thường xuyên | I review my notes regularly |
| study abroad | du học | I'd love to study abroad |
| get a scholarship | được học bổng | I hope to get a scholarship |
| a valuable experience | trải nghiệm quý giá | Studying is a valuable experience |

## 🎯 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài 1: Trả lời và thu âm

Chọn **1 topic** (Home, Work, hoặc Study) và trả lời **4 câu hỏi** ở trên. Thu âm lại và nghe lại:

**Checklist đánh giá:**
- [ ] Mỗi câu trả lời 2-4 câu?
- [ ] Có dùng fillers (Well, Actually, To be honest)?
- [ ] Có mở rộng với lý do hoặc ví dụ?
- [ ] Có dùng ít nhất 1 linking word (because, so, however)?
- [ ] Nói tự nhiên, không đọc?

### Bài 2: Paraphrase Practice

Viết lại các câu sau bằng cách thay từ đồng nghĩa:

1. "I like my home" → _______________
2. "My job is good" → _______________
3. "Studying is difficult" → _______________

**Gợi ý:**
1. "I'm quite fond of my place" / "I'm really happy with where I live"
2. "I find my job quite rewarding" / "I'm satisfied with my career"
3. "Studying can be quite challenging" / "I find some subjects demanding"

---

## 💡 Tip của ngày

> **Công thức trả lời Part 1:** **Answer + Reason + Example/Detail**
>
> ❌ "Yes, I like my job." (quá ngắn)
>
> ✅ "Yes, I really enjoy my job **(Answer)**. The main reason is that I get to meet interesting people every day **(Reason)**. For example, last week I worked with a client from Japan, which was a great experience **(Example)**."
