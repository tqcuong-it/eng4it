## 🎨 Topic 1: Hobbies / Free Time

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: What do you do in your free time?**

> In my free time, I usually go for a walk in the park near my house. I find it really refreshing, especially after a long day. Apart from that, I enjoy reading novels — I'm currently reading a mystery book, and I can't put it down!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Chi tiết cụ thể:** "park near my house", "mystery book"
- **Idiom:** "can't put it down" — nâng điểm Lexical Resource
- **Linking:** "Apart from that" — chuyển ý tự nhiên

---

**Q2: Do you have any hobbies?**

> Yes, I do! My main hobby is photography. I started taking photos a few years ago, and now I really enjoy capturing everyday moments — like sunsets, street scenes, things like that. I find it's a great way to express creativity and appreciate the world around me.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Timeline:** "a few years ago" → "now" — thể hiện sự phát triển
- **Collocations:** "capturing moments", "express creativity", "appreciate the world"

---

**Q3: Have you recently started any new hobbies?**

> Actually, yes! I've recently taken up yoga. A friend recommended it to me, and I thought I'd give it a try. I've been doing it for about two months now, and I feel so much more relaxed and energetic. I wish I'd started sooner!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Phrasal verb:** "taken up" (bắt đầu hobby mới) — tự nhiên hơn "started"
- **Tenses:** Present Perfect + Past Simple + Conditional → đa dạng grammar
- **Expression:** "give it a try", "I wish I'd started sooner" — tự nhiên

---

**Q4: Do you prefer indoor or outdoor activities?**

> It really depends on the weather, to be honest. On nice days, I prefer outdoor activities like cycling or hiking because I love being in nature. But when it's rainy or cold, I'd rather stay indoors and do something relaxing like watching films or cooking.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Nuance:** "It really depends on..." — không đen trắng
- **Contrast:** outdoor vs indoor — cấu trúc rõ ràng
- **Grammar:** "I'd rather" — conditional preference

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Hobbies

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |

## 🍜 Topic 2: Food / Cooking

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: What kind of food do you like?**

> I'm a big fan of Asian cuisine, especially Vietnamese food. I love dishes like pho and spring rolls because they're fresh, flavourful, and not too heavy. I also enjoy trying new cuisines whenever I get the chance — last month I tried Thai food for the first time, and it was amazing!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Expression:** "I'm a big fan of" — tự nhiên hơn "I like"
- **Adjectives:** "fresh, flavourful, not too heavy" — mô tả cụ thể
- **Example:** "last month I tried Thai food" — ví dụ thực tế

---

**Q2: Do you like cooking?**

> Well, I wouldn't say I'm a great cook, but I do enjoy making simple meals at home. I usually cook on weekends when I have more time. I find it quite therapeutic — it helps me relax and forget about work for a while. My signature dish is fried rice!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Honest opener:** "I wouldn't say I'm a great cook, but..." — khiêm tốn, tự nhiên
- **Từ vựng:** "therapeutic", "signature dish" — nâng điểm
- **Humor:** "My signature dish is fried rice!" — nhẹ nhàng, dễ gần

---

**Q3: Do you prefer eating at home or eating out?**

> I'd say I prefer eating at home most of the time because it's healthier and more affordable. But I do enjoy eating out once in a while, especially with friends — it's a nice way to socialise and try different restaurants. So I guess it's a balance of both.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Balance:** cả hai phía → trả lời trưởng thành
- **Idiom:** "once in a while" — tự nhiên
- **Linking:** "But", "So" — mạch lạc

---

**Q4: Is there any food you dislike?**

> To be honest, I'm not a big fan of bitter vegetables like bitter melon. I know they're healthy, but I just can't get used to the taste. Other than that, I'm pretty open to trying most foods — I believe you should at least try everything once!

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Negative expression:** "I'm not a big fan of" — lịch sự hơn "I hate"
- **Concession:** "I know they're healthy, but..." — thể hiện tư duy
- **Attitude:** "open to trying" — positive ending

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Food

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| be a big fan of | rất thích | I'm a big fan of seafood |
| flavourful | đậm vị | The soup is really flavourful |
| home-cooked meal | bữa cơm nhà | I prefer home-cooked meals |
| eat out | ăn ngoài | We eat out once a week |
| signature dish | món đặc trưng | My signature dish is curry |
| therapeutic | có tác dụng thư giãn | Cooking is quite therapeutic |
| get used to | quen với | I can't get used to the taste |

## 🌤️ Topic 3: Weather

### Câu hỏi thường gặp + Model Answers

**Q1: What's the weather like in your country?**

> Vietnam has a tropical climate, so it's generally hot and humid for most of the year. We have two main seasons — the rainy season and the dry season. The temperature can get really high in summer, sometimes over 35 degrees, which can be quite uncomfortable.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Structure:** overview → details → personal feeling
- **Từ vựng:** "tropical climate", "hot and humid", "rainy/dry season"
- **Data:** "over 35 degrees" — cụ thể, convincing

---

**Q2: What kind of weather do you like?**

> I'm a big fan of cool, sunny weather — you know, around 20 to 25 degrees with a gentle breeze. It's perfect for going out and doing outdoor activities. I'm not really keen on extremely hot weather because it makes me feel tired and sluggish.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Specific:** "20 to 25 degrees", "gentle breeze" — mô tả sinh động
- **Contrast:** thích vs không thích — balance
- **Expression:** "I'm not really keen on" — cách nói không thích lịch sự

---

**Q3: Does the weather affect your mood?**

> Absolutely! I think the weather has a huge impact on how I feel. On sunny days, I tend to be more energetic and productive. But on rainy or gloomy days, I just want to stay in bed and do nothing. I suppose that's pretty normal for most people though.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Strong opener:** "Absolutely!" — thể hiện sự chắc chắn
- **Contrast:** sunny vs rainy → mood changes
- **Hedging:** "I suppose" — khiêm tốn

---

**Q4: What do you usually do on rainy days?**

> On rainy days, I tend to stay indoors and do quiet activities. I might curl up on the sofa with a good book, or binge-watch a TV series. Sometimes I'll make a cup of hot tea and just listen to the rain — I find it surprisingly peaceful and calming.

📝 **Phân tích:**
- **Imagery:** "curl up on the sofa", "listen to the rain" — mô tả sinh động
- **Variety:** "might", "sometimes I'll" — đa dạng cách diễn đạt
- **Feeling:** "surprisingly peaceful" — cảm xúc thật

### 🔑 Useful Phrases cho topic Weather

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| tropical climate | khí hậu nhiệt đới | Vietnam has a tropical climate |
| hot and humid | nóng và ẩm | It's hot and humid in summer |
| a gentle breeze | gió nhẹ | There's a gentle breeze today |
| have a huge impact on | ảnh hưởng lớn đến | Weather has a huge impact on mood |
| curl up | cuộn mình | I curl up with a book |
| binge-watch | xem liền tay | I binge-watch TV series |
| gloomy | u ám | It's a gloomy day today |

## 🎯 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài 1: Mix & Match

Chọn **1 câu hỏi từ mỗi topic** (tổng 3 câu) và trả lời bằng giọng nói. Thu âm lại.

**Tiêu chí:**
- Mỗi câu 2-4 câu
- Dùng ít nhất 1 useful phrase mới học
- Có filler + linking word

### Bài 2: Tạo câu trả lời riêng

Trả lời câu hỏi sau bằng **câu chuyện thật của bạn**:

1. *"What's your favourite food and why?"*
2. *"What hobby would you like to try in the future?"*
3. *"What's the best weather for a holiday?"*

### Bài 3: Spot the Mistakes

Tìm lỗi trong các câu trả lời sau:

1. "I like food very much. Food is delicious." → ❌ Quá chung chung, lặp "food"
2. "The weather is sometimes hot sometimes cold." → ❌ Thiếu linking
3. "I am hobby is reading." → ❌ Sai grammar

**Sửa lại:**
1. "I'm really into Vietnamese cuisine — dishes like pho and banh mi are incredibly flavourful."
2. "The weather varies quite a lot — sometimes it's scorching hot, while other times it can be surprisingly chilly."
3. "My main hobby is reading — I particularly enjoy mystery novels."
