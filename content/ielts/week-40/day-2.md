## ✏️ Final Grammar Refresh

Ngữ pháp IELTS không cần quá phức tạp — chỉ cần **chính xác**. Hôm nay chúng ta rà soát 20 lỗi phổ biến nhất mà thí sinh Việt Nam hay mắc. Sửa được những lỗi này = tăng ít nhất **0.5 band Writing & Speaking**! 🎯

## 🚨 Top 20 Lỗi Ngữ Pháp IELTS — Checklist

### ❌ Lỗi 1: Thiếu mạo từ (a/an/the)
- ❌ *Pollution is **Ø** big problem.*
- ✅ *Pollution is **a** big problem.*
- 💡 **Quy tắc:** Danh từ đếm được số ít LUÔN cần mạo từ hoặc determiner.

### ❌ Lỗi 2: Subject-Verb Agreement
- ❌ *The number of students **are** increasing.*
- ✅ *The number of students **is** increasing.*
- 💡 **Nhớ:** "The number of" + singular verb. "A number of" + plural verb.

### ❌ Lỗi 3: Nhầm thì (Tense confusion)
- ❌ *Last year, the government **increase** taxes.*
- ✅ *Last year, the government **increased** taxes.*
- 💡 **Quy tắc:** Có time marker trong quá khứ → dùng past tense.

### ❌ Lỗi 4: Run-on sentences (Câu chạy)
- ❌ *Education is important it helps people get jobs.*
- ✅ *Education is important **because** it helps people get jobs.*
- 💡 **Fix:** Thêm liên từ (because, so, and) hoặc tách thành 2 câu.

### ❌ Lỗi 5: Dùng sai "depend"
- ❌ *It is **depend** on the situation.*
- ✅ *It **depends** on the situation.*
- 💡 **Nhớ:** "depend" là động từ, không cần "is" phía trước.

### ❌ Lỗi 6: Uncountable nouns + plural
- ❌ *Many **informations** are available.*
- ✅ *Much **information** is available.*
- 💡 **Từ không đếm được:** information, advice, research, knowledge, equipment, furniture.

### ❌ Lỗi 7: Sai comparative/superlative
- ❌ *This is **more better** than before.*
- ✅ *This is **better** than before.*
- 💡 **Quy tắc:** Không dùng "more" + từ đã có dạng so sánh riêng.

### ❌ Lỗi 8: Thiếu "it" trong cấu trúc
- ❌ ***Is** important to study hard.*
- ✅ ***It is** important to study hard.*
- 💡 **Nhớ:** Tiếng Anh luôn cần chủ ngữ — dùng "It" làm formal subject.

### ❌ Lỗi 9: Although... but...
- ❌ ***Although** it rained, **but** we went out.*
- ✅ ***Although** it rained, we went out.* HOẶC *It rained, **but** we went out.*
- 💡 **Quy tắc:** Chỉ dùng MỘT trong hai: although HOẶC but, không cả hai.

### ❌ Lỗi 10: Sai giới từ (Preposition errors)
- ❌ *I am interested **for** music.*
- ✅ *I am interested **in** music.*
- 💡 **Collocation phổ biến:** interested IN, depend ON, consist OF, result IN, lead TO.

### ❌ Lỗi 11: Passive voice sai
- ❌ *The problem **was solve** quickly.*
- ✅ *The problem **was solved** quickly.*
- 💡 **Cấu trúc:** be + V3 (past participle). Luôn dùng V3!

### ❌ Lỗi 12: Dùng "the" trước tên quốc gia
- ❌ *I live in **the** Vietnam.*
- ✅ *I live in Vietnam.*
- 💡 **Ngoại lệ có "the":** the UK, the US, the Netherlands, the Philippines.

### ❌ Lỗi 13: Gerund vs Infinitive
- ❌ *I enjoy **to play** sports.*
- ✅ *I enjoy **playing** sports.*
- 💡 **Theo sau enjoy/avoid/consider/suggest:** dùng V-ing.

### ❌ Lỗi 14: "People" là số nhiều
- ❌ *People **is** concerned about pollution.*
- ✅ *People **are** concerned about pollution.*
- 💡 **Nhớ:** "People" = plural. "Person" = singular.

### ❌ Lỗi 15: Sai word form (Sai loại từ)
- ❌ *The **pollute** is serious.* (dùng verb thay noun)
- ✅ *The **pollution** is serious.*
- 💡 **Tip:** Xác định vị trí cần noun/verb/adj/adv trước khi điền.

### ❌ Lỗi 16: Double negative
- ❌ *I don't have **nothing**.*
- ✅ *I don't have **anything**.* HOẶC *I have **nothing**.*
- 💡 **Quy tắc:** Tiếng Anh chuẩn KHÔNG dùng double negative.

### ❌ Lỗi 17: "Very" + extreme adjectives
- ❌ *The test was **very excellent**.*
- ✅ *The test was **excellent**.* HOẶC *The test was **absolutely excellent**.*
- 💡 **Quy tắc:** "Very" + gradable adj. "Absolutely" + extreme adj.

### ❌ Lỗi 18: Conditional sentences sai
- ❌ *If I **will study** hard, I **will pass**.*
- ✅ *If I **study** hard, I **will pass**.*
- 💡 **Conditional 1:** If + present simple, will + V.

### ❌ Lỗi 19: "Make" vs "Do"
- ❌ *I need to **make** my homework.*
- ✅ *I need to **do** my homework.*
- 💡 **Do:** homework, research, exercise, business. **Make:** decision, mistake, progress, effort.

### ❌ Lỗi 20: Fragment (Câu không đầy đủ)
- ❌ *Because the weather was bad.*  (thiếu main clause)
- ✅ *We stayed home **because the weather was bad**.*
- 💡 **Quy tắc:** Mệnh đề phụ không thể đứng một mình.

## 📝 Bài tập: Sửa lỗi nhanh (20 câu)

**Tìm và sửa lỗi trong mỗi câu:**

1. Education is very important for develop a country.
2. The government should provides more funding.
3. Although technology is useful, but it has disadvantages.
4. Many peoples believe that exercise is good.
5. The number of cars are increasing rapidly.
6. I am agree with this opinion.
7. Pollution affect our health seriously.
8. Children should to study hard every day.
9. It has many advantages of living in cities.
10. The informations in this report is incorrect.
11. If I will have more time, I will study more.
12. People who lives in cities face many problems.
13. This problem is more worse than before.
14. I interested in learning English.
15. He suggested to ban smoking in public.
16. The environment is being destroy by humans.
17. In recently years, technology has changed rapidly.
18. It is importance to protect the environment.
19. Most of people prefer living in cities.
20. Despite of the rain, we went to school.


{{< spoiler "🔑 Đáp án" >}}

1. *...important for **developing** a country.* (gerund sau preposition)
2. *...should **provide** more funding.* (bare infinitive sau modal)
3. *Although technology is useful, it has disadvantages.* (bỏ "but")
4. *Many **people** believe...* ("people" đã là số nhiều)
5. *The number of cars **is** increasing...* ("the number of" + singular)
6. ***I agree** with this opinion.* (bỏ "am" — agree là verb)
7. *Pollution **affects** our health...* (subject-verb agreement)
8. *Children should **study** hard...* (bỏ "to" sau should)
9. ***There are** many advantages of living in cities.* (There are, không phải It has)
10. *The **information** in this report **is** incorrect.* (uncountable)
11. *If I **have** more time, I will study more.* (present simple trong if-clause)
12. *People who **live** in cities...* (who + plural subject = plural verb)
13. *This problem is **worse** than before.* (bỏ "more")
14. *I **am** interested in learning English.* (thêm "am")
15. *He suggested **banning** smoking...* (suggest + V-ing)
16. *...is being **destroyed** by humans.* (past participle)
17. *In **recent** years...* (adjective, không phải adverb)
18. *It is **important** to protect...* (adjective form)
19. *Most **Ø** people prefer...* (bỏ "of" — most + noun trực tiếp)
20. ***Despite** the rain...* (bỏ "of" — despite không có "of")
