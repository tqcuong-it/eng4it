## 🎯 Mini Mock Test — 30 phút

Đây là bài kiểm tra **confidence check** cuối cùng. 20 câu, 30 phút, hỗn hợp 4 kỹ năng. Không cần căng thẳng — chỉ cần kiểm tra xem bạn đã sẵn sàng chưa! 😊

**Quy tắc:**
- ⏱️ Bấm giờ 30 phút

## 📖 Phần A: Reading Comprehension (Câu 1-8)

**Đọc đoạn văn sau và trả lời câu hỏi:**

> *The rapid growth of cities in developing countries has led to numerous challenges. Urban populations have more than doubled in the past three decades, putting enormous pressure on housing, transportation, and public services. While urbanization brings economic opportunities, it also creates problems such as overcrowding, pollution, and social inequality. Governments must invest in sustainable infrastructure to accommodate this growth without sacrificing quality of life.*
>
> *One approach that has shown promise is the development of "smart cities" — urban areas that use advanced planning and design to improve efficiency. For example, cities like Singapore and Copenhagen have implemented comprehensive public transport systems that reduce reliance on private vehicles. Similarly, green building standards have been adopted in many countries to minimize energy consumption and reduce carbon emissions.*

**Câu 1:** What has happened to urban populations in developing countries?

- A) They have decreased slightly
- B) They have more than doubled
- C) They have remained stable
- D) They have tripled

**Câu 2:** Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a challenge of urbanization?

- A) Overcrowding
- B) Pollution
- C) Unemployment
- D) Social inequality

**Câu 3:** True / False / Not Given: Smart cities use technology to improve urban efficiency.

**Câu 4:** True / False / Not Given: Singapore has the best public transport system in the world.

**Câu 5:** What is the purpose of green building standards?

- A) To make buildings taller
- B) To minimize energy consumption
- C) To increase housing supply
- D) To attract tourists

**Câu 6:** The word "accommodate" in paragraph 1 is closest in meaning to:

- A) Reject
- B) Handle/support
- C) Ignore
- D) Prevent

**Câu 7:** Fill in the blank: Governments must invest in sustainable _______ to support urban growth. *(1 word from the text)*

**Câu 8:** What does the passage suggest governments should do?

- A) Stop urbanization
- B) Build more private housing
- C) Invest in sustainable infrastructure
- D) Reduce population growth

## ✏️ Phần B: Grammar & Vocabulary (Câu 9-16)

**Câu 9:** Choose the correct word: The government should _______ more money on education.

- A) spend
- B) spending
- C) spent
- D) spends

**Câu 10:** The number of tourists _______ increased significantly.

- A) have
- B) has
- C) are
- D) is

**Câu 11:** _______ the bad weather, the event was successful.

- A) Although
- B) Despite
- C) However
- D) Because

**Câu 12:** Choose the WRONG sentence:

- A) I am interested in music.
- B) She suggested going to the park.
- C) Although it rained, but we went out.
- D) The information was useful.

**Câu 13:** Which word means "bền vững"?

- A) substantial
- B) sustainable
- C) susceptible
- D) sufficient

**Câu 14:** "The crime rate has _______ over the past decade." Choose the best word:

- A) declined
- B) denied
- C) delayed
- D) delivered

**Câu 15:** Which sentence uses the correct word form?

- A) The pollute of the river is serious.
- B) The pollution of the river is serious.
- C) The polluted of the river is serious.
- D) The polluting of the river is serious.

**Câu 16:** "Many people believe that education is _______ for personal development."

- A) essence
- B) essentially
- C) essential
- D) essentiality

## 🗣️ Phần C: Speaking & Writing (Câu 17-20)

**Câu 17 (Speaking):** Sắp xếp các bước nói Part 2 theo đúng thứ tự:

- A) Nói 1.5-2 phút
- B) Đọc cue card
- C) Viết keyword trong 1 phút chuẩn bị
- D) Trả lời câu hỏi follow-up

**Câu 18 (Writing Task 1):** Khi viết mô tả biểu đồ, phần nào là QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT?

- A) Introduction
- B) Overview
- C) Detailed description
- D) Conclusion

**Câu 19 (Writing Task 2):** Viết 1 câu mở bài cho đề sau:
> *"Some people believe that children should start learning a foreign language at primary school. Others think they should wait until secondary school. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*

*(Tự viết — không có đáp án đúng/sai, nhưng xem gợi ý bên dưới)*

**Câu 20 (Speaking):** Bạn đang trả lời Part 3 và examiner hỏi: *"Do you think social media has more advantages or disadvantages?"* Viết câu trả lời 4-5 câu.

*(Tự viết — xem gợi ý bên dưới)*

### Phần A: Reading

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | **B** | "have more than doubled in the past three decades" |
| 2 | **C** | Unemployment không được đề cập trong bài |

| 7 | **infrastructure** | "invest in sustainable infrastructure" |
| 8 | **C** | "must invest in sustainable infrastructure" |

### Phần B: Grammar & Vocabulary

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 9 | **A** | should + bare infinitive (spend) |
| 10 | **B** | "The number of" + has (singular) |
| 11 | **B** | Despite + noun phrase (không có subject + verb) |
| 12 | **C** | Although...but — không dùng cùng lúc |
| 13 | **B** | sustainable = bền vững |
| 14 | **A** | declined = giảm (hợp ngữ cảnh crime rate) |
| 15 | **B** | "pollution" — cần noun sau "The" |

### Phần C: Speaking & Writing

**Câu 17:** B → C → A → D (Đọc cue card → Viết keyword → Nói → Trả lời follow-up)

**Câu 18:** **B — Overview** là phần quan trọng nhất. Thiếu overview = mất điểm nặng!

**Câu 19 — Gợi ý mở bài:**
> *"The question of when children should begin studying a foreign language has been widely debated. While some argue that primary school is the ideal time to start, others believe that secondary school is more appropriate. This essay will examine both viewpoints before presenting my own perspective."*

**Câu 20 — Gợi ý trả lời:**
> *"That's an interesting question. I believe social media has both advantages and disadvantages, but overall I think the benefits outweigh the drawbacks. On the positive side, it allows people to stay connected with friends and family regardless of distance. It's also a powerful tool for sharing information and raising awareness about important issues. However, I do acknowledge that excessive use can lead to problems such as addiction and cyberbullying. So I think the key is using it responsibly."*
