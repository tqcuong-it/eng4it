## 📋 Hôm nay: Viết thật, bấm giờ thật!

Hôm nay bạn sẽ viết **2 bài essay** — mỗi bài **40 phút**, **250+ từ**. Đây là cách duy nhất để chuẩn bị cho phòng thi thật!

### Quy tắc

1. ⏱️ **Bấm giờ nghiêm ngặt** — 40 phút/bài, hết giờ = dừng
2. 📝 **Viết tay trên giấy** — mô phỏng thi thật (hoặc gõ máy nếu muốn)
3. 🚫 **KHÔNG dùng từ điển** — thi thật không có!
4. 📊 **Đếm từ** sau khi viết xong
5. ✅ **Tự chấm** bằng rubric sau mỗi bài

### Nhắc lại phân bổ thời gian

| Bước | Thời gian |
|------|-----------|
| Phân tích đề + Outline | 5 phút |
| Viết Introduction | 5 phút |
| Viết Body 1 | 10 phút |
| Viết Body 2 | 10 phút |
| Viết Conclusion | 5 phút |
| Kiểm tra lại | 5 phút |

## ✍️ Essay 1: Opinion Essay (40 phút)

### Đề bài

> *"Some people believe that children should not be given homework by their teachers, whereas others argue that homework plays an important role in the education of children. To what extent do you agree or disagree that children should be given homework?"*

### Hướng dẫn trước khi viết

1. **Xác định:** Dạng Opinion (Agree/Disagree)
2. **Chọn phe:** Bạn đồng ý hay không đồng ý rằng trẻ nên có homework?
3. **Brainstorm 2 ý chính** cho Body 1 và Body 2
4. **Viết outline** trước khi bắt đầu viết

### ⏱️ BẮT ĐẦU BẤM GIỜ — 40 PHÚT

*Viết essay của bạn ở đây (trên giấy hoặc máy tính)...*

---

### 📖 Bài mẫu tham khảo (ĐỌC SAU KHI VIẾT XONG!)

> **Introduction:**
> The question of whether children should receive homework has been widely debated. While some argue that it places unnecessary pressure on young learners, I largely agree that homework is beneficial because it reinforces learning and develops self-discipline.
>
> **Body 1:**
> The primary reason I support homework is that it helps students consolidate what they have learned in class. During school hours, teachers introduce new concepts, but students need additional practice to fully understand and remember them. For example, a child who practices mathematics problems at home is more likely to master the subject than one who only studies during lessons. Without this extra practice, many students would struggle to retain important knowledge and fall behind in their studies.
>
> **Body 2:**
> Furthermore, homework teaches children valuable life skills such as time management and self-discipline. When students are given assignments with deadlines, they learn to plan their time and work independently. These habits are essential not only for academic success but also for their future careers. For instance, adults who developed strong organizational skills through homework during childhood often perform better in professional settings. However, I acknowledge that excessive homework can be counterproductive, so teachers should assign reasonable amounts that do not overwhelm students.
>
> **Conclusion:**
> In conclusion, I believe that homework plays a crucial role in education by reinforcing classroom learning and developing important life skills. Teachers should ensure that assignments are appropriate in quantity to maximize their benefits.
>
> **Số từ:** ~255 từ

## ✍️ Essay 2: Problem/Solution Essay (40 phút)

### Đề bài

> *"In many countries, the number of people suffering from stress-related illnesses is increasing. What are the causes of this problem? What measures could be taken to address it?"*

### Hướng dẫn trước khi viết

1. **Xác định:** Dạng Problem/Solution (Causes + Solutions)
2. **Brainstorm:** 2-3 causes + 2-3 solutions tương ứng
3. **Viết outline** — Body 1 = Causes, Body 2 = Solutions
4. **Nhớ:** Solutions phải CỤ THỂ và khả thi

### ⏱️ BẮT ĐẦU BẤM GIỜ — 40 PHÚT

*Viết essay của bạn ở đây...*

---

### 📖 Bài mẫu tham khảo (ĐỌC SAU KHI VIẾT XONG!)

> **Introduction:**
> Stress-related health problems have become increasingly common in many parts of the world. This essay will examine the main causes of this trend and suggest some practical measures to address it.
>
> **Body 1 — Causes:**
> One of the primary causes of rising stress levels is the demanding nature of modern work. Many people face long working hours, tight deadlines, and constant pressure to perform, which takes a serious toll on their mental and physical health. In addition, financial concerns contribute significantly to stress. The rising cost of living, housing, and healthcare means that many individuals constantly worry about their ability to meet basic needs. Furthermore, the widespread use of social media creates additional pressure, as people frequently compare their lives to the idealized images they see online, leading to feelings of inadequacy and anxiety.
>
> **Body 2 — Solutions:**
> To address this issue, employers should implement policies that promote work-life balance, such as flexible working hours and regular breaks. Research has shown that employees who have more control over their schedules report lower stress levels and higher productivity. Additionally, governments could invest in free or affordable mental health services, making it easier for people to seek professional help when needed. Public awareness campaigns about stress management techniques, such as exercise, meditation, and healthy eating, could also empower individuals to take proactive steps to protect their well-being.
>
> **Conclusion:**
> In conclusion, work pressure, financial worries, and social media are the main drivers of stress-related illness. By promoting work-life balance and improving access to mental health support, both employers and governments can help reduce this growing problem.
>
> **Số từ:** ~270 từ

## 💡 Phân tích sau khi viết

Trả lời các câu hỏi sau cho mỗi bài:

1. **Bạn có lập outline trước không?** Nếu không, bạn có bị lạc ý giữa chừng?
2. **Phần nào mất nhiều thời gian nhất?** Introduction? Body? → Cần luyện thêm phần đó
3. **Bạn có kịp viết Conclusion không?** Nếu không → cần quản lý thời gian tốt hơn
4. **Từ nào bạn muốn dùng nhưng không biết?** → Ghi lại và học
5. **So sánh với bài mẫu:** Bài bạn thiếu gì? Thừa gì?
