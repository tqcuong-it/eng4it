## 📋 Introduction — Ấn tượng đầu tiên

### Introduction gồm 2 phần

```
Câu 1-2: PARAPHRASE đề bài (viết lại bằng từ khác)
Câu cuối: THESIS STATEMENT (nêu quan điểm/preview)
```

**Mục tiêu:** 40-60 từ, 2-3 câu. KHÔNG viết dài!

### Tại sao Introduction quan trọng?

- Giám khảo đọc Introduction **đầu tiên** → quyết định ấn tượng ban đầu
- Introduction cho thấy bạn **hiểu đề bài** (Task Response)
- Thesis statement giúp bài viết có **hướng đi rõ ràng**

## 📝 Kỹ thuật Paraphrase

### 5 cách paraphrase đề bài

| Kỹ thuật | Đề gốc | Paraphrase |
|----------|--------|------------|
| 1. Đổi từ đồng nghĩa | "children" | "young people" / "youngsters" |
| 2. Đổi cấu trúc câu | "People think that X is good" | "X is considered beneficial by many" |
| 3. Đổi dạng từ | "education" (n) | "educate" (v) / "educational" (adj) |
| 4. Active ↔ Passive | "People believe that..." | "It is believed that..." |
| 5. Đổi vị trí | "A causes B" | "B is caused by A" |

### Ví dụ paraphrase

**Đề gốc:** *"Some people think that the internet has a negative effect on society."*

| Cách | Paraphrase |
|------|------------|
| Đồng nghĩa | "Many individuals believe that the internet has a harmful impact on communities." |
| Đổi cấu trúc | "The internet is considered by some to have a detrimental influence on society." |
| Passive | "It is argued that society has been negatively affected by the internet." |

> ⚠️ **KHÔNG paraphrase từ chuyên ngành** — "internet", "government", "education" không cần đổi!

## 📝 Thesis Statement cho từng dạng essay

### Opinion Essay

| Mẫu | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|
| "I completely agree that..." | "I completely agree that children should learn a foreign language early." |
| "In my opinion, ... because..." | "In my opinion, this is beneficial because it improves cognitive skills." |
| "I strongly believe that..." | "I strongly believe that governments should invest more in education." |

### Discussion Essay

| Mẫu | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|
| "While both views have merit, I believe..." | "While both views have merit, I believe that experience is more valuable than qualifications." |
| "Although ..., I am more inclined to..." | "Although some disagree, I am more inclined to support the view that..." |

### Problem/Solution Essay

| Mẫu | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|
| "This essay will examine the causes and suggest solutions." | (Không cần nêu quan điểm) |
| "There are several reasons for this, and steps can be taken to address it." | |

### Advantages/Disadvantages Essay

| Mẫu | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|
| "In my view, the advantages outweigh the disadvantages." | (Khi đề hỏi "outweigh") |
| "This essay will discuss both the benefits and drawbacks." | (Khi đề chỉ hỏi liệt kê) |

## 📖 Introduction mẫu cho 4 dạng

### 1. Opinion

> **Đề:** *"Some people think university education should be free. To what extent do you agree?"*
>
> **Intro:** *It is often argued that higher education should be provided at no cost to students. I strongly agree with this view because free university education can reduce inequality and benefit society as a whole.*

### 2. Discussion

> **Đề:** *"Some people prefer to live in the city, while others prefer the countryside. Discuss both views."*
>
> **Intro:** *There is a growing debate about whether urban or rural living offers a better quality of life. While city life provides more opportunities, I believe that the countryside offers greater advantages in terms of health and well-being.*

### 3. Problem/Solution

> **Đề:** *"Many people are becoming overweight. What are the causes and solutions?"*
>
> **Intro:** *Obesity has become a significant health concern in many countries around the world. This essay will examine the main causes of this problem and propose some practical solutions to address it.*

### 4. Advantages/Disadvantages

> **Đề:** *"More people are shopping online. Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?"*
>
> **Intro:** *Online shopping has grown rapidly in recent years, transforming the way people purchase goods. Although there are some drawbacks, I believe the benefits of this trend significantly outweigh its disadvantages.*

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Paraphrase

Paraphrase các câu sau (dùng ít nhất 2 kỹ thuật):

1. *"Many children spend too much time watching television."*
   → __________

2. *"The government should invest more money in public healthcare."*
   → __________

3. *"Some people think that exercise is the key to good health."*
   → __________

### Bài tập 2: Viết Thesis Statement

Viết thesis statement cho mỗi đề bài:

1. (Opinion) *"Schools should teach students practical skills like cooking. Agree or disagree?"*
   → __________

2. (Discussion) *"Some think technology improves education. Others disagree. Discuss both views."*
   → __________

3. (Problem/Solution) *"Youth unemployment is rising. What causes this and how can it be solved?"*
   → __________

### Bài tập 3: Viết Introduction hoàn chỉnh

Viết **3 Introduction** (mỗi cái 40-60 từ) cho 3 đề bài ở Bài tập 2.

## ✅ Đáp án gợi ý

### Bài tập 1

1. *"A large number of young people are spending excessive amounts of time in front of the TV."*
2. *"Greater financial resources should be allocated to the public health sector by the government."*
3. *"Physical activity is regarded by some as the most important factor in maintaining good health."*

### Bài tập 2

1. *"I completely agree that schools should incorporate practical life skills such as cooking into their curriculum."*
2. *"While both perspectives are valid, I believe that technology, when used effectively, enhances the learning experience."*
3. *"This essay will explore the key factors contributing to rising youth unemployment and suggest measures to tackle this issue."*

## 💡 Tips cho Introduction

1. **ĐỪNG copy nguyên đề bài** — paraphrase là bắt buộc
2. **ĐỪNG viết quá dài** — 2-3 câu, 40-60 từ là đủ
3. **ĐỪNG cho ví dụ** trong Introduction — để dành cho Body
4. **ĐỪNG viết "In this essay, I will discuss..."** — quá máy móc
5. **Thesis phải RÕ RÀNG** — giám khảo phải biết ngay quan điểm của bạn
