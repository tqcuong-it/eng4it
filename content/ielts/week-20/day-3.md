## 📋 Conclusion — Ấn tượng cuối cùng

### Conclusion gồm gì?

```
Câu 1: Tóm tắt ý chính (summarize)
Câu 2: Khẳng định lại quan điểm hoặc đưa ra suy nghĩ cuối (final thought)
```

**Mục tiêu:** 30-50 từ, 2-3 câu. NGẮN GỌN nhưng ĐẦY ĐỦ.

### Tại sao Conclusion quan trọng?

- **Không có Conclusion = mất điểm** Task Response (chưa hoàn thành bài)
- Giám khảo đọc Conclusion để kiểm tra bạn có **nhất quán** với thesis không
- Đây là **ấn tượng cuối cùng** — kết mạnh = điểm tốt hơn

## 📝 Cấu trúc Conclusion cho từng dạng essay

### Opinion Essay

```
In conclusion, I [firmly/strongly] believe that [thesis].
[Tóm tắt lý do chính].
```

> *In conclusion, I firmly believe that children should learn financial skills from a young age. This helps them develop responsibility and prepares them for a secure financial future.*

### Discussion Essay

```
In conclusion, while [view A has merit], I believe [view B].
[Final thought].
```

> *In conclusion, while traditional classrooms provide valuable structure, I believe online learning offers greater flexibility and accessibility. A blended approach would combine the best of both worlds.*

### Problem/Solution Essay

```
In conclusion, [problem summary].
By [solution summary], this issue can be effectively addressed.
```

> *In conclusion, rising household waste is primarily caused by consumer culture and excessive packaging. By implementing stricter regulations and raising public awareness, governments can significantly reduce this problem.*

### Advantages/Disadvantages Essay

```
In conclusion, although [disadvantages exist], [advantages are more significant].
Overall, [final thought].
```

> *In conclusion, although remote work can lead to isolation, the flexibility and time savings it provides make it a positive trend overall. Companies should embrace this model while addressing its challenges.*

## 📝 Cụm từ mở đầu Conclusion

| Cụm từ | Ghi chú |
|--------|---------|
| "In conclusion, ..." | ⭐ Phổ biến nhất, an toàn nhất |
| "To conclude, ..." | OK |
| "To sum up, ..." | OK |
| "All things considered, ..." | Hơi formal nhưng tốt |
| ~~"In a nutshell, ..."~~ | ❌ Quá informal — ĐỪNG dùng |
| ~~"To summarize, ..."~~ | ❌ Nghe như academic paper — tránh |

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Dùng **"In conclusion"** là an toàn nhất. Đừng sáng tạo quá ở phần này!

## ❌ Lỗi thường gặp khi viết Conclusion

### 1. Thêm ý mới

❌ *"In conclusion, I agree. Additionally, another important point is that sports also help children's mental health."*
→ ✅ Conclusion KHÔNG được thêm ý mới — chỉ tóm tắt!

### 2. Copy nguyên Introduction

❌ *"Many people believe that children should learn about money. I completely agree with this view."* (giống y hệt Intro)
→ ✅ Paraphrase lại — dùng từ khác!

### 3. Quá dài

❌ Viết 80-100 từ cho Conclusion → lãng phí thời gian
→ ✅ 30-50 từ là đủ

### 4. Đổi quan điểm

❌ Introduction: "I agree" → Conclusion: "However, both sides have equal value"
→ ✅ Conclusion phải NHẤT QUÁN với thesis!

### 5. Không có Conclusion

❌ Viết hết Body 2 rồi dừng → thiếu Conclusion = mất điểm
→ ✅ Luôn dành 3-5 phút cuối cho Conclusion — kể cả chỉ 1-2 câu!

## 📖 So sánh Conclusion yếu vs mạnh

**Đề:** *"Some people think that the government should provide free healthcare. To what extent do you agree?"*

### ❌ Conclusion yếu

> *In conclusion, I think free healthcare is good. There are many advantages and disadvantages. Both sides have valid points.*

**Vấn đề:** Quan điểm không rõ, chung chung, mâu thuẫn.

### ✅ Conclusion mạnh

> *In conclusion, I strongly believe that governments should provide free healthcare to all citizens. This would reduce health inequalities and ensure that everyone, regardless of income, has access to essential medical services.*

**Tốt vì:** Quan điểm rõ ràng, tóm tắt lý do, nhất quán với thesis.

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Sửa Conclusion

Sửa các Conclusion yếu sau:

1. ❌ *"In conclusion, there are many things to consider. It is a complex issue."*
   → ✅ __________

2. ❌ *"To sum up, I agree. Also, I forgot to mention that education is important for jobs too."*
   → ✅ __________

3. ❌ *"In conclusion, both views are equally important and I cannot choose one side."* (Đề hỏi "agree or disagree")
   → ✅ __________

### Bài tập 2: Viết Conclusion

Viết Conclusion (30-50 từ) cho mỗi essay sau (chỉ dựa trên thesis đã cho):

1. **Opinion** — Thesis: "I agree that schools should teach practical life skills."
   → Conclusion: __________

2. **Discussion** — Thesis: "I believe technology enhances education more than it harms."
   → Conclusion: __________

3. **Problem/Solution** — Problems: traffic congestion, air pollution. Solutions: public transport, cycling infrastructure.
   → Conclusion: __________

### Bài tập 3: Introduction + Conclusion matching

Viết **cả Introduction và Conclusion** cho đề bài sau — đảm bảo nhất quán:

*"Some people believe that university education should be free. Others think students should pay fees. Discuss both views and give your opinion."*

## ✅ Đáp án gợi ý

### Bài tập 1

1. ✅ *"In conclusion, I believe that the benefits of this approach clearly outweigh its drawbacks, making it a worthwhile investment for society."*

2. ✅ *"To sum up, I strongly agree that education should be a government priority, as it creates long-term economic and social benefits for the entire nation."*

3. ✅ *"In conclusion, I largely agree that early childhood education is more important. While later education matters too, building a strong foundation in the early years has the greatest impact on a child's development."*

### Bài tập 2

1. *"In conclusion, I firmly believe that teaching practical skills such as cooking and budgeting should be part of the school curriculum, as these abilities are essential for students' future independence."*

2. *"In conclusion, while technology has some drawbacks in education, I believe its ability to make learning more accessible and engaging makes it a largely positive development."*

3. *"In conclusion, traffic congestion and air pollution are serious urban issues. By investing in public transport and cycling infrastructure, cities can create a cleaner and more efficient transportation system."*

## 💡 Tips cho Conclusion

1. **BẮT ĐẦU viết Conclusion khi còn 5 phút** — kể cả Body 2 chưa hoàn hảo
2. **2 câu là đủ** — đừng cố viết dài
3. **Paraphrase thesis** — đừng copy nguyên từ Introduction
4. **KHÔNG thêm ý mới** — Conclusion = tóm tắt + khẳng định
5. **Đọc lại Introduction trước khi viết Conclusion** — đảm bảo nhất quán
