## 📋 Body Paragraph — Trái tim của essay

Body paragraphs chiếm **60-70% bài viết** và quyết định điểm số. Một body paragraph tốt cần cấu trúc rõ ràng.

### Cấu trúc PEEL

```
P — Point (Topic Sentence)     → Nêu ý chính của đoạn
E — Explain (Giải thích)       → Giải thích tại sao/như thế nào
E — Example (Ví dụ)            → Minh họa bằng ví dụ cụ thể
L — Link (Liên kết)            → Nối lại với thesis/câu hỏi
```

**Mỗi body paragraph = 1 ý chính duy nhất** — đừng nhồi 3-4 ý!

---

## 📝 Từng phần chi tiết

### 1. Topic Sentence (Câu chủ đề)

**Chức năng:** Nêu ý chính của TOÀN BỘ đoạn trong 1 câu.

| Mẫu | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|
| "The main reason is that..." | "The main reason is that outdoor play improves children's health." |
| "One significant advantage is..." | "One significant advantage is the flexibility it offers." |
| "A major problem is..." | "A major problem is the lack of affordable housing." |
| "On the one hand, ..." | "On the one hand, supporters argue that..." |

> ⚠️ **Lỗi phổ biến:** Viết topic sentence quá chung chung → *"There are many reasons"* ❌. Hãy viết CỤ THỂ!

### 2. Explanation (Giải thích)

**Chức năng:** Giải thích **tại sao** ý chính đó đúng hoặc quan trọng.

| Cụm từ mở đầu | Ví dụ |
|----------------|-------|
| "This is because..." | "This is because physical activity strengthens muscles and bones." |
| "The reason for this is that..." | "The reason for this is that exercise releases endorphins." |
| "In other words, ..." | "In other words, regular movement keeps the body functioning well." |
| "This means that..." | "This means that children who play outside are generally healthier." |

### 3. Example (Ví dụ)

**Chức năng:** Minh họa bằng ví dụ cụ thể — làm cho lập luận thuyết phục hơn.

| Cụm từ mở đầu | Ví dụ |
|----------------|-------|
| "For example, ..." | "For example, a study found that children who play outdoors daily have 30% lower obesity rates." |
| "For instance, ..." | "For instance, in Scandinavian countries, outdoor education is mandatory." |
| "A clear example is..." | "A clear example is Japan, where morning exercise is part of school routine." |
| "Research has shown that..." | "Research has shown that active children perform better academically." |

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Ví dụ KHÔNG cần là số liệu thật. Bạn có thể nói "Research suggests..." hoặc "In many countries..." — giám khảo chấm cách lập luận, không fact-check!

### 4. Link (Liên kết)

**Chức năng:** Nối đoạn trở lại thesis hoặc câu hỏi chính.

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| "Therefore, ..." | "Therefore, encouraging outdoor play is essential for children's well-being." |
| "This clearly shows that..." | "This clearly shows that exercise should be a priority in schools." |
| "As a result, ..." | "As a result, governments should invest in outdoor facilities." |

## 📖 Body Paragraph mẫu — Phân tích

**Đề:** *"Some people think that children should spend more time outdoors. To what extent do you agree?"*

> **[P]** The most important reason I agree is that outdoor activities are essential for children's physical development. **[E]** When children play outside, they engage in physical movements such as running, jumping, and climbing, which help strengthen their muscles, improve coordination, and maintain a healthy weight. **[E]** For instance, research conducted in several European countries found that children who spend at least two hours outdoors daily have significantly lower rates of obesity and better cardiovascular health than those who remain indoors. **[L]** This demonstrates that outdoor play is not merely recreational but plays a vital role in children's overall health.

**Phân tích:**
- **P** (Topic Sentence): Nêu ý chính rõ ràng — outdoor = physical development
- **E** (Explain): Giải thích cụ thể — running, jumping, climbing → muscles, coordination
- **E** (Example): Ví dụ — European countries, 2 hours daily, lower obesity
- **L** (Link): Kết nối lại — outdoor play = vital for health

**Số từ:** ~95 từ ✅ (mục tiêu 80-100 từ)

Đọc đoạn văn sau và xác định phần **P, E, E, L**:

> *Many people argue that public transport should be free for all citizens. Free public transport would reduce the number of private cars on the road, which would significantly decrease traffic congestion and air pollution. In cities such as Luxembourg, where public transport became free in 2020, there was a notable reduction in traffic jams and carbon emissions. Clearly, making public transport free is an effective way to create cleaner and more efficient cities.*

## ✅ Đáp án gợi ý

### Bài tập 1

| Phần | Câu |
|------|-----|
| P | "Many people argue that public transport should be free for all citizens." |
| E | "Free public transport would reduce... traffic congestion and air pollution." |
| E | "In cities such as Luxembourg... reduction in traffic jams and carbon emissions." |
| L | "Clearly, making public transport free is an effective way..." |

### Bài tập 2

1. *"One significant benefit of learning a foreign language at a young age is that it enhances children's cognitive development."*
2. *"However, a major drawback of remote work is the sense of isolation it can create."*
3. *"One compelling reason to support free education is that it helps reduce social inequality."*

### Bài tập 4 — Viết lại

> *One significant advantage of this approach is that it helps people save both time and money. By eliminating unnecessary steps in the process, individuals can focus their resources on more productive activities. For instance, studies have shown that streamlined systems can reduce costs by up to 30% while also cutting processing time in half. This demonstrates that efficiency improvements benefit not only individuals but also the broader economy.*

## 💡 Tips cho Body Paragraph

1. **1 đoạn = 1 ý chính** — đừng nhồi nhiều ý
2. **Topic sentence phải CỤ THỂ** — giám khảo đọc câu đầu biết ngay đoạn nói gì
3. **Explain trước, Example sau** — đừng nhảy thẳng vào ví dụ
4. **Ví dụ càng cụ thể càng tốt** — "in Japan" tốt hơn "in some countries"
5. **80-100 từ/đoạn** — quá ngắn thì thiếu phát triển, quá dài thì mất focus
