## ✍️ Writing — Mock Test 3 (60 phút)

### Task 1 (20 phút — 150+ từ)

> The two pie charts below show the main sources of energy in Country X in 2000 and 2020.
>
> Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

**Dữ liệu:**

**Năm 2000:**
| Nguồn | Tỷ lệ |
|--------|--------|
| Oil | 38% |
| Coal | 27% |
| Natural Gas | 20% |
| Nuclear | 10% |
| Renewable | 5% |

**Năm 2020:**
| Nguồn | Tỷ lệ |
|--------|--------|
| Oil | 25% |
| Coal | 12% |
| Natural Gas | 22% |
| Nuclear | 15% |
| Renewable | 26% |

**Key features:**
- Oil và Coal giảm mạnh
- Renewable tăng từ 5% → 26% (tăng gấp 5 lần!)
- Natural Gas gần như không đổi
- Nuclear tăng nhẹ

### Task 2 (40 phút — 250+ từ)

> **Some people think that the best way to reduce crime is to give longer prison sentences. Others, however, believe there are better alternative ways to reduce crime.**
>
> Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

**Gợi ý:**

**View 1 — Longer sentences:**
- Răn đe (deterrence)
- Bảo vệ xã hội (incapacitation)
- Công bằng cho nạn nhân

**View 2 — Alternatives:**
- Giáo dục và tạo việc làm
- Phục hồi chức năng (rehabilitation programs)
- Giải quyết nguyên nhân gốc (poverty, inequality)
- Community service

**Từ vựng nâng cao:**

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa |
|--------|-------|
| deterrent effect | tác dụng răn đe |
| rehabilitation | phục hồi chức năng |
| recidivism rate | tỷ lệ tái phạm |
| restorative justice | tư pháp phục hồi |
| root causes of crime | nguyên nhân gốc rễ tội phạm |
| custodial sentence | án tù |
| community service | lao động công ích |

## 🎤 Speaking — Mock Test 3

*Nghỉ 15 phút rồi bắt đầu! GHI ÂM!*

### Part 1: Interview (4-5 phút)

**Chủ đề 1: Home**
1. Do you live in a house or an apartment?
2. What do you like about where you live?
3. Is there anything you'd like to change about your home?

**Chủ đề 2: Transportation**
4. How do you usually get to work/school?
5. Do you prefer public transport or driving?
6. How has transportation changed in your city?
7. Do you think there will be flying cars in the future?

**Chủ đề 3: Social Media**
8. Do you use social media often?
9. What's the most popular social media platform in your country?
10. Do you think social media has more advantages or disadvantages?

### Part 2: Cue Card (3-4 phút)

> **Describe an achievement you are proud of.**
>
> You should say:
> - what you achieved
> - when it happened
> - how you achieved it
>
> and explain why you are proud of it.

**1 phút chuẩn bị:**
```
Achievement: _______________
When: _______________
How: _______________
Why proud: _______________
```

**Cấu trúc gợi ý:**
1. Giới thiệu thành tựu
2. Bối cảnh và thời gian
3. Quá trình đạt được (khó khăn, nỗ lực)
4. Cảm xúc và ý nghĩa

### Part 3: Discussion (4-5 phút)

1. What kinds of achievements do people value most in your country?
2. Do you think it's important for children to learn about setting goals?
3. Is success more about talent or hard work?
4. How do you think the definition of success has changed over generations?
5. Should governments reward people for their achievements?
