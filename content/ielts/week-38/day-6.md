## 📖 Quick Reading Check (15 phút)

Đọc đoạn văn ngắn (200 từ) và trả lời 5 câu.

**Đoạn văn:**

The concept of "digital detox" — deliberately reducing time spent on electronic devices — has gained significant attention in recent years. Research from the University of Pennsylvania found that limiting social media use to 30 minutes per day led to significant reductions in loneliness and depression over three weeks. Participants reported feeling less anxious and more present in their daily lives.

However, complete disconnection is neither practical nor necessary for most people. Experts recommend a balanced approach: designating specific times for checking email and social media, keeping devices out of the bedroom, and engaging in regular offline activities such as reading, exercise, or face-to-face socializing.

Interestingly, some studies suggest that the problem is not screen time itself but the type of digital activity. Passive consumption — endlessly scrolling through feeds — correlates with negative outcomes, while active use — creating content, meaningful communication — can actually enhance well-being. The key lies in mindful technology use rather than wholesale rejection of digital tools.

**Câu hỏi:**

1. (T/F/NG) The University of Pennsylvania study lasted three months. ___
2. (T/F/NG) 30 minutes of daily social media reduced loneliness. ___
3. Experts recommend keeping devices out of the ___________. ___
4. Passive consumption means ___________. ___
5. Active digital use can __________ well-being. ___

**Kết quả: ___/5**

## ✍️ Quick Writing Check (15 phút)

Viết **1 body paragraph** (80-100 từ) cho đề sau:

> "Some people believe that online education will replace traditional schools. To what extent do you agree?"

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Topic sentence rõ ràng
- [ ] Supporting point + explanation
- [ ] Example cụ thể
- [ ] Ít lỗi ngữ pháp
- [ ] Từ vựng đa dạng
- [ ] 80-100 từ
