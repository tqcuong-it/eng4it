## ✍️ Writing — Mock Test 2 (60 phút)

### Task 1 (20 phút — 150+ từ)

> The line graph below shows the number of tourists visiting three Asian countries (Thailand, Vietnam, and Japan) between 2010 and 2023.
>
> Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

**Dữ liệu:**

| Năm | Thailand | Vietnam | Japan |
|-----|----------|---------|-------|
| 2010 | 15.9M | 5.0M | 8.6M |
| 2013 | 26.5M | 7.6M | 10.4M |
| 2016 | 32.6M | 10.0M | 24.0M |
| 2019 | 39.8M | 18.0M | 31.9M |
| 2020 | 6.7M | 3.8M | 4.1M |
| 2023 | 28.0M | 12.6M | 25.1M |

**Gợi ý key features:**
- Xu hướng tăng chung 2010-2019
- Sụt giảm mạnh 2020 (COVID-19)
- Phục hồi nhưng chưa về mức cũ (2023)
- Thailand luôn dẫn đầu, Japan tăng nhanh nhất

### Task 2 (40 phút — 250+ từ)

> **In many countries, the gap between the rich and the poor is increasing. What problems does this cause? What solutions can be offered?**

**Gợi ý cấu trúc:**

**Introduction:** Paraphrase + thesis

**Body 1 — Problems:**
- Tội phạm và bất ổn xã hội tăng
- Bất bình đẳng tiếp cận giáo dục, y tế
- Stress tâm lý và chia rẽ cộng đồng

**Body 2 — Solutions:**
- Thuế lũy tiến (progressive taxation)
- Đầu tư giáo dục công
- Chương trình phúc lợi xã hội
- Nâng cao lương tối thiểu

**Conclusion:** Tóm tắt problems + solutions

**Từ vựng nâng cao:**

| Cụm từ | Nghĩa |
|--------|-------|
| wealth disparity | chênh lệch giàu nghèo |
| social mobility | cơ hội thăng tiến xã hội |
| income inequality | bất bình đẳng thu nhập |
| welfare programs | chương trình phúc lợi |
| progressive taxation | thuế lũy tiến |
| poverty alleviation | xóa đói giảm nghèo |

## 🎤 Speaking — Mock Test 2

*Nghỉ 15 phút, uống nước, thư giãn, rồi bắt đầu! Nhớ GHI ÂM!*

### Part 1: Interview (4-5 phút)

**Chủ đề 1: Work/Study**
1. Do you work or are you a student?
2. What do you enjoy most about your work/studies?
3. Would you like to change your job/field of study?

**Chủ đề 2: Food**
4. What's your favorite type of food?
5. Do you prefer cooking at home or eating out?
6. Has your diet changed in recent years?
7. Do you think people in your country eat healthily?

**Chủ đề 3: Weather**
8. What's the weather like in your country?
9. Do you prefer hot or cold weather?
10. Does the weather affect your mood?

### Part 2: Cue Card (3-4 phút)

> **Describe a skill that took you a long time to learn.**
>
> You should say:
> - what the skill is
> - how you learned it
> - why it took a long time
>
> and explain how you felt when you finally mastered it.

**1 phút chuẩn bị:**
```
Skill: _______________
How learned: _______________
Why long time: _______________
Feeling when mastered: _______________
```

### Part 3: Discussion (4-5 phút)

1. What skills do you think are most important in today's world?
2. Do you think schools focus enough on practical skills?
3. Is it better to learn a skill quickly or take time to master it?
4. How has technology changed the way we learn new skills?
5. Do you think some people are naturally more talented, or is it all about practice?
