## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Mini Test

### 📝 PHẦN 1: Ngữ pháp (20 câu)

**A. Used to (câu 1-5)**

1. She ______ (live) in the countryside when she was young.
2. Did you ______ (celebrate) Tet with your grandparents?
3. He didn't ______ (like) spicy food as a child.
4. They ______ (watch) fireworks every New Year. (Bây giờ không xem nữa)
5. I ______ (not travel) abroad before I was 25.

**B. Past Perfect (câu 6-10)**

6. When I arrived, the concert ______ already ______ (start).
7. After she ______ (visit) the museum, she went to the temple.
8. He ______ (not eat) Vietnamese food before he came to Hanoi.
9. ______ they ______ (book) the hotel before the trip? (Câu hỏi)
10. We were tired because we ______ (walk) all day.

**C. Too / Enough (câu 11-15)**

11. The soup is ______ hot to eat right now. (quá)
12. Is the restaurant big ______ for 20 people? (đủ)
13. There is ______ much sugar in this dessert. (quá)
14. The food is delicious ______ to serve at a festival. (đủ)
15. There are ______ many tourists in the old town. (quá)

**D. Quantifiers (câu 16-18)**

16. There are ______ beautiful temples in Hue. (một số)
17. She doesn't have ______ experience traveling abroad. (nhiều)
18. How ______ heritage sites are there in Vietnam? (bao nhiêu)

**E. Compound Sentences (câu 19-20)**

19. Hoi An is very old, ______ it is still beautiful.
20. We should protect our heritage, ______ we will lose it forever.

### 📝 PHẦN 2: Từ vựng (10 câu)

Chọn đáp án đúng:

21. A place where you can see paintings and sculptures: ______
    - a) concert  b) gallery  c) palace

22. Something you buy to remember a trip: ______
    - a) luggage  b) souvenir  c) ingredient

23. The opposite of "polite": ______
    - a) creative  b) rude  c) exotic

24. A traditional Vietnamese soup: ______
    - a) dessert  b) appetizer  c) dish (pho)

25. A person who visits another country: ______
    - a) foreigner  b) musician  c) ancestor

26. Fireworks, parades, and costumes are part of a: ______
    - a) museum  b) festival  c) landmark

27. ______ means to keep something safe from damage: ______
    - a) restore  b) preserve  c) compose

28. The plan of places to visit on a trip: ______
    - a) itinerary  b) departure  c) arrival

29. The beliefs, customs, and way of life of a group: ______
    - a) rhythm  b) culture  c) portion

30. A very famous and important work of art: ______
    - a) lyric  b) talent  c) masterpiece

### 📝 PHẦN 3: Đọc hiểu

Đọc đoạn văn và trả lời câu hỏi:

> Vietnam is a country with a rich **culture** and many beautiful **heritage** sites. Tourists love to **explore** **ancient** **temples**, taste **delicious** **cuisine**, and enjoy **folk** **music** **performances**.
>
> In the past, people **used to** travel by boat along the Mekong River. Now, **many** tourists fly to different cities. After they **had visited** the famous **landmarks**, they often buy **souvenirs** for their families.
>
> Vietnamese food is not **too** spicy **for** most visitors, **and** the **portions** are big **enough** to share. **Some** restaurants serve **traditional** dishes, **but** others offer modern **cuisine**. Every meal is a **celebration** of Vietnamese **culture**!

31. What do tourists love to do in Vietnam?
32. How did people use to travel in the past?
33. What do tourists often do after visiting landmarks?
34. Is Vietnamese food too spicy for most visitors?
35. What is every meal described as?

| 12 | **enough** | adj + enough: đủ lớn |
| 13 | **too** | too much + danh từ không đếm được |
| 14 | **enough** | adj + enough: đủ ngon |
| 15 | **too** | too many + danh từ đếm được |
