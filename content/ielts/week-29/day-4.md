## 📝 Đề bài

> *Some people believe that technology has made our lives more complicated rather than simpler. To what extent do you agree or disagree?*
>
> *Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.*

### 🧠 Brainstorm (3 phút)

**Agree (phức tạp hơn):**
- Information overload — quá nhiều thông tin → stress, khó tập trung
- Always connected — không thể "ngắt kết nối", làm việc 24/7
- Too many choices — quá nhiều app, dịch vụ → khó chọn

**Disagree (đơn giản hơn):**
- Communication dễ dàng hơn (video call, messaging)
- Online banking, shopping, booking — tiết kiệm thời gian
- Access to information — học bất cứ điều gì online

**Quan điểm gợi ý:** Partially agree — technology đơn giản hóa nhiều thứ nhưng cũng tạo ra sự phức tạp mới

### 📐 Dàn ý

```
Introduction:
- Paraphrase đề bài
- Thesis: Partially agree — technology simplifies daily tasks but creates new complexities

Body 1 (Technology đơn giản hóa):
- Main idea: Daily tasks are easier
- Support: Online banking, e-commerce, GPS navigation
- Example: What took hours (going to bank, shopping) now takes minutes

Body 2 (Technology tạo phức tạp mới):
- Main idea: Digital life creates new challenges
- Support: Information overload, always-on culture, privacy concerns
- Example: Constant notifications, social media pressure

Conclusion:
- Restate opinion: Technology is a double-edged sword
- Final thought: We need to use it wisely
```

### 📄 Bài mẫu (Band 6.5)

> It is often argued that modern technology has made people's lives more complex instead of easier. While I acknowledge that technology has simplified many aspects of daily life, I partially agree that it has also introduced new forms of complexity.
>
> On the one hand, technology has undeniably made numerous everyday tasks more convenient and efficient. For instance, people can now manage their finances through online banking, purchase goods from the comfort of their homes via e-commerce platforms, and navigate unfamiliar cities using GPS applications. Activities that previously required significant time and effort, such as visiting a bank or searching for information in a library, can now be completed in a matter of minutes. This has freed up considerable time for work, leisure, and personal development.
>
> On the other hand, the rapid advancement of technology has created several new challenges that did not exist before. One significant issue is information overload, as people are constantly bombarded with news, advertisements, and social media updates, which can lead to stress and difficulty concentrating. Furthermore, the expectation of being always available through smartphones and email has blurred the boundaries between work and personal life, making it harder for people to truly relax. Privacy concerns have also emerged as a major issue, with personal data being collected and sometimes misused by technology companies.
>
> In conclusion, while technology has greatly simplified practical tasks and improved efficiency, it has simultaneously introduced new forms of complexity related to mental health, work-life balance, and privacy. Therefore, I believe it is essential for individuals to use technology mindfully and set clear boundaries to enjoy its benefits without being overwhelmed by its drawbacks.

**Số từ:** 252 từ ✅

## 🔍 Phân tích bài mẫu

### Cấu trúc

| Đoạn | Chức năng | Số câu |
|------|----------|--------|
| Introduction | Paraphrase + Thesis statement | 2 |
| Body 1 | Ý 1 (đơn giản hóa) + Evidence + Example | 4 |
| Body 2 | Ý 2 (phức tạp hơn) + Evidence + Example | 4 |
| Conclusion | Restate + Final thought | 2 |

### Từ vựng hay

| Từ/cụm | Nghĩa | Dùng khi |
|---------|-------|----------|
| undeniably | không thể phủ nhận | Nhấn mạnh sự thật rõ ràng |
| in a matter of minutes | chỉ trong vài phút | So sánh trước/sau |
| information overload | quá tải thông tin | Nói về vấn đề digital |
| blurred the boundaries | xóa nhòa ranh giới | Work-life balance |
| use technology mindfully | dùng công nghệ có ý thức | Kết luận |

### Cấu trúc câu hay

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|
| While + clause, + main clause | *While technology has simplified..., it has also introduced...* |
| For instance, + example | *For instance, people can now manage...* |
| Activities that previously + V-ed... can now + V | *Activities that previously required... can now be completed...* |
| One significant issue is... | *One significant issue is information overload...* |
