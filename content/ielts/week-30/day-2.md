## 📖 Formal Alternatives — Thay thế từ phổ biến

### Động từ mô tả xu hướng (Task 1)

| ❌ Quá đơn giản | ✅ Formal hơn | Nghĩa |
|----------------|-------------|-------|
| go up | increase, rise, climb, surge | tăng |
| go down | decrease, decline, fall, drop, plummet | giảm |
| stay the same | remain stable/constant/unchanged | giữ nguyên |
| go up and down | fluctuate | dao động |
| reach the top | peak at, reach a peak of | đạt đỉnh |
| reach the bottom | hit a low of, bottom out at | chạm đáy |

### Tính từ + Trạng từ mô tả mức độ

| Mức độ | Adjective | Adverb |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Rất lớn | dramatic, sharp, steep | dramatically, sharply, steeply |
| Lớn | significant, considerable, substantial | significantly, considerably, substantially |
| Vừa | moderate, steady, gradual | moderately, steadily, gradually |
| Nhỏ | slight, marginal, modest | slightly, marginally, modestly |

**Cách dùng:**
- *There was a **significant increase** in sales.* (adj + noun)
- *Sales **increased significantly**.* (verb + adv)
- *Sales **saw a significant increase**.* (subject + saw + adj + noun)

### Từ thay thế cho Task 2

| ❌ Informal/Simple | ✅ Formal | Ví dụ |
|-------------------|----------|-------|
| a lot of | a considerable number of, numerous | *numerous studies have shown...* |
| good | beneficial, advantageous, positive | *this policy has beneficial effects* |
| bad | detrimental, adverse, negative | *the adverse effects of pollution* |
| important | crucial, essential, significant | *education is crucial for development* |
| think | believe, argue, contend, maintain | *some experts contend that...* |
| show | demonstrate, illustrate, indicate, reveal | *research demonstrates that...* |
| big | substantial, considerable, major | *a major challenge facing society* |
| get better | improve, enhance, ameliorate | *to improve living standards* |
| get worse | deteriorate, worsen, decline | *air quality has deteriorated* |
| because | due to, owing to, as a result of | *due to rapid urbanisation* |

## 🔗 Collocations mạnh cho Writing

### Task 1 Collocations

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------------|-------|-------|
| account for | chiếm | *Oil accounted for 42% of energy.* |
| stand at | đứng ở mức | *The figure stood at 500 in 2010.* |
| see a rise/fall | chứng kiến tăng/giảm | *The period saw a sharp rise in...* |
| experience growth | trải qua tăng trưởng | *The sector experienced steady growth.* |
| witness a decline | chứng kiến sụt giảm | *The industry witnessed a decline.* |
| level off | chững lại | *Growth levelled off after 2015.* |
| remain steady at | duy trì ổn định ở | *Sales remained steady at 200.* |

### Task 2 Collocations

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------------|-------|-------|
| play a crucial role | đóng vai trò quan trọng | *Education plays a crucial role in...* |
| have a profound impact on | có tác động sâu sắc | *Technology has had a profound impact on...* |
| raise awareness of | nâng cao nhận thức | *Campaigns raise awareness of...* |
| pose a threat to | đặt ra mối đe dọa | *Pollution poses a serious threat to...* |
| take measures to | thực hiện biện pháp | *Governments should take measures to...* |
| shed light on | làm sáng tỏ | *This study sheds light on...* |
| bridge the gap between | thu hẹp khoảng cách | *Education can bridge the gap between...* |

## ✍️ Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Thay thế từ informal

Viết lại các câu sau dùng từ formal hơn:

1. *Pollution is a **big** problem that **makes** the environment **bad**.*
2. *The chart shows that sales **went up a lot** in 2020.*
3. *I **think** that education is **very important** for **getting** a good job.*
4. *There are **a lot of** reasons why people move to cities.*
5. *The government should **do** something to **fix** this problem.*

### ✅ Gợi ý đáp án

1. *Pollution is a **major/significant** issue that has **detrimental/adverse** effects on the environment.*
2. *The chart indicates that sales **rose dramatically/surged significantly** in 2020.*
3. *I **believe/contend** that education is **crucial/essential** for **securing** a well-paid position.*
4. *There are **numerous/a considerable number of** factors contributing to urban migration.*
5. *The government should **implement measures/take action** to **address/resolve** this issue.*

### Bài tập 2: Điền collocation

1. Technology has ___ a profound ___ on daily life.
2. The government should ___ measures ___ reduce pollution.
3. Education ___ a crucial ___ in economic development.
4. Climate change ___ a serious ___ to biodiversity.

**Đáp án:** 1. had...impact 2. take...to 3. plays...role 4. poses...threat

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Không cần học hết — chọn **5-10 collocations** bạn thích nhất và dùng chúng trong mọi bài viết. Dùng đi dùng lại cho đến khi thuộc nằm lòng! 📝
