## 🚫 10 lỗi ngữ pháp phổ biến nhất

### 1. Subject-Verb Agreement

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| The number of students **have** increased. | The number of students **has** increased. |
| A number of people **is** affected. | A number of people **are** affected. |
| The data **shows** a trend. | The data **show** a trend. |

> **Quy tắc:** "The number of" + singular verb. "A number of" + plural verb.

### 2. Tense Consistency

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| In 2010, the figure **increases** to 500. | In 2010, the figure **increased** to 500. |
| Currently, people **worked** from home. | Currently, people **work** from home. |

> **Quy tắc:** Dữ liệu quá khứ → past tense. Xu hướng chung/hiện tại → present tense.

### 3. Articles (a/an/the)

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| There was **increase** in sales. | There was **an increase** in sales. |
| **A** percentage of people | **The** percentage of people (đã xác định) |
| In **the** 2020, ... | In **∅** 2020, ... |

> **Quy tắc:** Đếm được số ít cần article. Năm đứng một mình không cần "the".

### 4. Countable vs Uncountable Nouns

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| many **informations** | much **information** |
| less **people** | fewer **people** |
| a large amount of **students** | a large number of **students** |

> **Quy tắc:** information, advice, research, evidence, knowledge → uncountable.

### 5. Prepositions

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| increase **of** 20% | increase **of** 20% ✅ / increase **by** 20% ✅ |
| increase **to** 500 **from** 300 | increase **from** 300 **to** 500 |
| according **with** the data | according **to** the data |
| depend **of** | depend **on** |

### 6. Passive Voice (Task 1 Process/Map)

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| Workers **harvest** the beans. | The beans **are harvested**. |
| They **built** a new road. | A new road **was built**. |

> **Quy tắc:** Task 1 Process/Map thường dùng passive. Focus vào **đối tượng**, không phải người thực hiện.

### 7. Comparative & Superlative

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| more **bigger** | **bigger** / **much bigger** |
| the **most highest** | the **highest** |
| Country A is high**er** than Country B **three times** | Country A is **three times higher** than Country B |

### 8. Run-on Sentences

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| The figure increased **,** it reached 500. | The figure increased **and** reached 500. |
| People work from home **,** they save time. | People work from home**, which** saves them time. |

> **Quy tắc:** Không nối 2 câu hoàn chỉnh chỉ bằng dấu phẩy. Dùng: and, which, ; hoặc tách thành 2 câu.

### 9. Word Form

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| There was a **significant increase** → OK | The figure **significant** increased → **significantly** |
| It is **importance** to note | It is **important** to note |
| The **develop** of technology | The **development** of technology |

> **Quy tắc:** Adjective + Noun. Adverb + Verb. Kiểm tra word form trước khi nộp bài.

### 10. Relative Clauses

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng |
|--------|---------|
| The country **who** has the highest GDP | The country **which/that** has the highest GDP |
| People **which** work from home | People **who** work from home |

## ✍️ Bài tập: Tìm và sửa lỗi

**Đoạn văn sau có 10 lỗi ngữ pháp. Hãy tìm và sửa:**

> The bar chart shows the number of student who study abroad in 2010 and 2020. Overall, there was increase in all countries. The number of students have risen significantly. In the 2010, USA had the most highest number at 500,000. By 2020, it increased to 800,000, it became the leading destination. Less students chose to study in UK compared to USA. The data shows that many informations can be gathered from this chart. It is importance to note that the figure increased significant over the decade.

### ✅ Đáp án

| # | Lỗi | Sửa |
|---|------|------|
| 1 | student → **students** | Plural |
| 2 | was increase → **was an increase** | Article |
| 3 | have risen → **has risen** | Subject-verb agreement |
| 4 | In the 2010 → **In 2010** | Article |
| 5 | most highest → **highest** | Superlative |
| 6 | ..., it became → **... and became** / **..., becoming** | Run-on |
| 7 | Less students → **Fewer students** | Countable |
| 8 | many informations → **much information** | Uncountable |
| 9 | importance → **important** | Word form |
| 10 | significant → **significantly** | Word form (adverb) |

## 📝 Bài tập viết: Sửa bài cũ

1. Lấy **1 bài Task 1** bạn đã viết ở Tuần 29
2. Gạch chân tất cả **lỗi ngữ pháp** (dùng checklist 10 lỗi ở trên)
3. Viết lại bài đã sửa
4. So sánh 2 phiên bản — đếm số lỗi đã sửa

### 📋 Grammar Checklist khi kiểm tra bài

- [ ] Subject-verb agreement (đặc biệt với "the number of")
- [ ] Tense nhất quán (past/present)
- [ ] Articles (a/an/the) đúng chỗ
- [ ] Countable vs uncountable nouns
- [ ] Prepositions (from/to, by, of, in)
- [ ] Passive voice cho Process/Map
- [ ] Comparatives/superlatives đúng
- [ ] Không có run-on sentences
- [ ] Word form (noun/verb/adj/adv) đúng
- [ ] Relative pronouns (who/which/that)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Mỗi lần viết xong, dành **3 phút cuối** chỉ để kiểm tra ngữ pháp. Đọc lại từng câu một — bạn sẽ bắt được rất nhiều lỗi! ✏️
