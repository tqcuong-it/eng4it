## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Present Perfect vs Past Simple

### 📖 Khi nào dùng thì nào?

Đây là sự phân biệt **quan trọng nhất** trong IELTS! Nhiều người Việt hay nhầm lẫn hai thì này.

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|----------------|-------------|
| Không nói **khi nào** cụ thể | Nói rõ **khi nào** |
| Còn liên quan đến **hiện tại** | Đã xong, **không liên quan** hiện tại |
| have/has + V3 | V2 |

### 🔑 So sánh cụ thể

| Present Perfect ✅ | Past Simple ✅ |
|-------------------|---------------|
| I **have visited** Paris. (đã đến — không nói khi nào) | I **visited** Paris **in 2019**. (đã đến — năm 2019) |
| She **has worked** here for 3 years. (vẫn đang làm) | She **worked** here for 3 years. (đã nghỉ rồi) |
| I **have lost** my key. (mất — hiện tại vẫn chưa tìm thấy) | I **lost** my key **yesterday**. (mất hôm qua) |

### Dấu hiệu nhận biết

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|----------------|-------------|
| ever, never | yesterday |
| already, yet | last week/month/year |
| for, since | ago |
| just | in 2020 |
| recently | on Monday |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:** Nếu câu có **thời gian cụ thể** (yesterday, last year, in 2020, ago) → **Past Simple**. Nếu **không nói khi nào** hoặc dùng for/since → **Present Perfect**.

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chọn Present Perfect hoặc Past Simple

Chọn dạng đúng:

1. I (have worked / worked) here since 2021.
2. She (has finished / finished) the report yesterday.
3. They (have visited / visited) the factory last month.
4. He (has been / was) a teacher for 20 years. (vẫn đang là)
5. We (have discussed / discussed) the plan two days ago.
6. I (have never been / never was) to America.
7. She (has just arrived / just arrived) at the office.
8. He (has resigned / resigned) last Friday.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Chia động từ đúng thì

Chia động từ trong ngoặc ở **Present Perfect** hoặc **Past Simple**:

1. I ______ (start) this job in 2022.
2. She ______ (not / reply) to my email yet.
3. They ______ (complete) the project last week.
4. He ______ (work) overtime every day since Monday.
5. We ______ (discuss) the issue yesterday.
6. I ______ (already / confirm) the meeting.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. I have started this job in 2020.
2. She finished the task already.
3. He has went to the meeting yesterday.
4. They have discussed the plan last week.
5. I worked here since 2019.
6. She has been absent yesterday.

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Is your job making youستress?](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2024-0425)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> My name is Duc. I **started** my first job in 2018. I **worked** at a small company for two years. Then I **moved** to a bigger company in 2020.
>
> I have **worked** at this company **since** 2020. I have **learned** many new **skills**. My manager **assigned** me a big project last month and I have **already** **completed** it. I **arrived** late once last week, but I have been **punctual** every other day. I am **busy** but **efficient**!

*(80 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. When did Duc start his first job?
2. How long has he worked at his current company?
3. What did the manager assign last month?
4. Has Duc completed the project?
5. Was Duc late last week?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | He started his first job in 2018. (Past Simple — thời gian cụ thể) |
| 2 | He has worked there since 2020. (Present Perfect — vẫn đang làm) |
| 3 | The manager assigned him a big project. |
| 4 | Yes, he has already completed it. |
| 5 | Yes, he arrived late once last week. |
