## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Present Perfect với for / since

### 📖 For / Since dùng khi nào?

Trong Present Perfect, **for** và **since** dùng để nói về **khoảng thời gian** — hành động bắt đầu trong quá khứ và **vẫn tiếp tục đến hiện tại**.

- **for** + **khoảng thời gian** (bao lâu): for 3 years, for 6 months, for a long time
- **since** + **mốc thời gian** (từ khi nào): since 2020, since Monday, since I graduated

### 🔑 Phân biệt for và since

| for (khoảng thời gian) | since (mốc thời gian) |
|------------------------|----------------------|
| for **two years** | since **2024** |
| for **six months** | since **January** |
| for **a long time** | since **last week** |
| for **three hours** | since **9 AM** |
| for **ten minutes** | since **I was a child** |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:**
> - **for** → **"For how long?"** (bao lâu?) → khoảng thời gian
> - **since** → **"Since when?"** (từ khi nào?) → mốc thời gian

### Ví dụ

| Câu | Nghĩa |
|-----|-------|
| I have worked here **for** five years. | Tôi đã làm ở đây **được** 5 năm. |
| I have worked here **since** 2021. | Tôi đã làm ở đây **từ** năm 2021. |
| She has been a manager **for** three months. | Cô ấy làm quản lý **được** 3 tháng. |
| She has been a manager **since** April. | Cô ấy làm quản lý **từ** tháng 4. |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chọn for hoặc since

1. I have lived in Japan ______ 2019.
2. She has worked here ______ ten years.
3. They have known each other ______ a long time.
4. He has been a doctor ______ he graduated.
5. We have waited ______ two hours.
6. I have studied English ______ last September.
7. She has been promoted twice ______ she joined the company.
8. He has had this job ______ six months.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Viết câu với for hoặc since

Viết câu Present Perfect:

1. I / work / at this company / 2020 → ______
2. She / be / a nurse / fifteen years → ______
3. They / live / in this city / they were children → ______
4. He / not / have / a holiday / six months → ______
5. We / know / our boss / a long time → ______
6. I / apply / for three jobs / last month → ______

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. I have worked here since five years.
2. She has been a teacher for 2015.
3. He has lived in Hanoi since a long time.
4. They have known each other for last year.
5. We have waited since three hours.
6. She has had this position for she started.

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Job interviews](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2023-0720)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> My name is Trang. I have worked as a software **engineer** for seven years. I have worked at three different **companies** since I **graduated**. Last month, I had a job **interview** for a new **position**.
>
> The **interviewer** asked about my **strengths** and **weaknesses**. I felt **confident** because I have **prepared** for a long time. I have **applied** for this **opportunity** since December. Yesterday, they called me — I got the **job**! I am very happy.

*(80 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. How long has Trang worked as an engineer?
2. How many companies has she worked at?
3. What did the interviewer ask about?
4. How long has she applied for the opportunity?
5. Did she get the job?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | She has worked as an engineer for seven years. |
| 2 | She has worked at three different companies. |
| 3 | The interviewer asked about her strengths and weaknesses. |
| 4 | She has applied since December. |
| 5 | Yes, she got the job. |
