## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Present Perfect với ever / never

### 📖 Ever / Never dùng khi nào?

Trong Present Perfect, **ever** và **never** dùng để hỏi và nói về **kinh nghiệm** — điều bạn đã hoặc chưa từng làm trong đời.

- **ever** = đã bao giờ (dùng trong câu hỏi)
- **never** = chưa bao giờ (dùng trong câu phủ định)

### 🔑 Công thức

| Loại câu | Công thức | Ví dụ |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Câu hỏi | **Have/Has** + S + **ever** + V3? | **Have** you **ever worked** abroad? |
| Trả lời Có | Yes, I **have**. / Yes, I have + V3. | Yes, I **have**. I have worked in Japan. |
| Trả lời Không | No, I **haven't**. / No, I have **never** + V3. | No, I **have never worked** abroad. |

> 🧠 **Mẹo:** **ever** đứng giữa have/has và V3. **never** cũng đứng giữa — nhưng câu đã là phủ định nên KHÔNG thêm not.
> - ✅ I have **never** been late.
> - ❌ ~~I haven't never been late.~~ (sai — phủ định kép!)

### Ví dụ thêm

| Câu hỏi | Trả lời |
|----------|---------|
| Have you ever **led** a team? | Yes, I have. I have led a team of five. |
| Has she ever **presented** at a meeting? | No, she has never presented at a meeting. |
| Have they ever **managed** a project? | Yes, they have managed many projects. |
| Has he ever **trained** new employees? | No, he has never trained anyone. |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Viết câu hỏi với ever

Viết câu hỏi Present Perfect với **ever**:

1. you / work / in a team → ______?
2. she / organize / a big event → ______?
3. they / achieve / their sales target → ______?
4. he / present / in English → ______?
5. you / solve / a difficult problem at work → ______?
6. she / lead / a project → ______?

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Trả lời với never

Trả lời phủ định dùng **never**:

1. Have you ever worked abroad? → No, I ______.
2. Has he ever managed a big team? → No, he ______.
3. Have they ever missed a deadline? → No, they ______.
4. Has she ever analyzed financial data? → No, she ______.
5. Have you ever trained new staff? → No, I ______.
6. Has he ever been late to work? → No, he ______.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: ever hay never?

Điền **ever** hoặc **never** vào chỗ trống:

1. Have you ______ worked as a teacher?
2. I have ______ been to London.
3. Has she ______ organized a conference?
4. He has ______ missed a deadline. He is very reliable.
5. Have they ______ used this software?
6. We have ______ had a problem with teamwork.

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Have you ever worked in a team? |
| 2 | Has she ever organized a big event? |
| 3 | Have they ever achieved their sales target? |
| 4 | Has he ever presented in English? |
| 5 | Have you ever solved a difficult problem at work? |
| 6 | Has she ever led a project? |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | have never worked abroad | I → have never + V3 |
| 2 | has never managed a big team | he → has never + V3 |
| 3 | have never missed a deadline | they → have never + V3 |
| 4 | has never analyzed financial data | she → has never + V3 |
| 5 | have never trained new staff | I → have never + V3 |
| 6 | has never been late to work | he → has never + V3 |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | ever | Câu hỏi → dùng ever |
| 2 | never | Câu khẳng định mang nghĩa phủ định → never |
| 3 | ever | Câu hỏi → dùng ever |
| 4 | never | Nghĩa phủ định (chưa bao giờ trễ) → never |
| 5 | ever | Câu hỏi → dùng ever |
| 6 | never | Nghĩa phủ định (chưa bao giờ có vấn đề) → never |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Soft skills](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2024-0111)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> My name is Huy. I work as a **project** **manager**. I have **managed** many **projects** in my **career**. I have **ever** needed good **skills** like **teamwork** and **communication**.
>
> I have **trained** new **employees** and I have **presented** at many meetings. I have **never** missed a **deadline**. My **boss** says I am **reliable** and **responsible**. I want to **improve** my **skills** and **achieve** more **goals** this year.

*(75 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What is Huy's job?
2. Has Huy managed many projects?
3. Has Huy ever missed a deadline?
4. What does Huy's boss say about him?
5. What does Huy want to do this year?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | He is a project manager. |
| 2 | Yes, he has managed many projects. |
| 3 | No, he has never missed a deadline. |
| 4 | His boss says he is reliable and responsible. |
| 5 | He wants to improve his skills and achieve more goals. |
