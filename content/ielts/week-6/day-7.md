## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Mini Test

### 📝 PHẦN 1: Ngữ pháp (30 câu)

**A. Present Perfect — Chia động từ (10 câu)**

1. I ______ (work) at this company for five years.
2. She ______ (never / visit) London.
3. ______ you ______ (ever / manage) a big project?
4. They ______ (already / finish) the report.
5. He ______ (not / reply) to the email yet.
6. We ______ (know) each other since 2018.
7. She ______ (just / receive) a promotion.
8. I ______ (not / see) the new office yet.
9. ______ he ______ (sign) the contract yet?
10. They ______ (live) in this city for a long time.

**B. Present Perfect vs Past Simple (10 câu)**

Chọn dạng đúng:

11. She (has started / started) this job in 2021.
12. I (have worked / worked) here since March.
13. They (have visited / visited) the factory last Friday.
14. He (has never been / never was) late to work.
15. We (have completed / completed) the project two days ago.
16. She (has already submitted / already submitted) the report.
17. I (have met / met) the new manager yesterday.
18. He (has been / was) a doctor for 20 years. (vẫn đang là)
19. They (have discussed / discussed) the problem last Monday.
20. I (have just finished / just finished) my presentation.

**C. Superlatives (10 câu)**

Điền dạng so sánh nhất:

21. This is ______ (good) company I have ever worked for.
22. She is ______ (experienced) employee in our team.
23. That was ______ (hard) interview I have ever had.
24. He has ______ (high) salary in the department.
25. It is ______ (important) skill for my career.
26. This is ______ (bad) decision we have ever made.
27. She is ______ (reliable) person in the office.
28. That was ______ (long) meeting this month.
29. He is ______ (professional) manager I know.
30. This is ______ (big) challenge in my career.

### 📝 PHẦN 2: Đọc hiểu (1 đoạn văn — 90 từ)

Đọc đoạn văn và trả lời câu hỏi:

> My name is Mai. I have **worked** as an **accountant** for ten years. I **started** my **career** at a small **company** in 2016. I **worked** there for four years. **Since** 2020, I have **worked** at the **biggest** **company** in the city.
>
> I have **already** received two **promotions**. My **boss** says I am the **most reliable** **employee**. I have **never** missed a **deadline**. My **goal** is to become the **best** **manager** in the **industry**. I have earned three **certificates** and I want to **develop** more **skills**.

*(90 từ)*

**Câu hỏi:**

1. What is Mai's profession?
2. When did Mai start her career?
3. How long has Mai worked at the biggest company?
4. How many promotions has Mai received?
5. Has Mai ever missed a deadline?
6. What is Mai's career goal?
7. How many certificates has Mai earned?
8. Find 2 examples of **Superlatives** in the text.
9. Find 2 examples of **Present Perfect with for/since**.
10. Find 1 example of **Present Perfect with never**.

### ✅ Đáp án Phần 1: Ngữ pháp

**A. Present Perfect (10 câu)**

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | have worked | for five years → Present Perfect |
| 2 | has never visited | never + Present Perfect |
| 3 | Have ... ever managed | ever + câu hỏi Present Perfect |
| 4 | have already finished | already + Present Perfect |
| 5 | hasn't replied | not yet + Present Perfect |
| 6 | have known | since 2018 → Present Perfect |
| 7 | has just received | just + Present Perfect |
| 8 | haven't seen | not yet + Present Perfect |
| 9 | Has ... signed | yet + câu hỏi Present Perfect |
| 10 | have lived | for a long time → Present Perfect |

**B. Present Perfect vs Past Simple (10 câu)**

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 11 | started | in 2021 → Past Simple |
| 12 | have worked | since March → Present Perfect |
| 13 | visited | last Friday → Past Simple |
| 14 | has never been | never → Present Perfect |
| 15 | completed | two days ago → Past Simple |
| 16 | has already submitted | already → Present Perfect |
| 17 | met | yesterday → Past Simple |
| 18 | has been | for 20 years + vẫn đang → Present Perfect |
| 19 | discussed | last Monday → Past Simple |
| 20 | have just finished | just → Present Perfect |

**C. Superlatives (10 câu)**

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 21 | the best | good → best (bất quy tắc) |
| 22 | the most experienced | 3+ âm → the most |
| 23 | the hardest | 1 âm → -est |
| 24 | the highest | 1 âm → -est (high → highest) |
| 25 | the most important | 3 âm → the most |
| 26 | the worst | bad → worst (bất quy tắc) |
| 27 | the most reliable | 3 âm → the most |
| 28 | the longest | 1 âm → -est |
| 29 | the most professional | 4 âm → the most |
| 30 | the biggest | 1 âm, CVC → gấp đôi + -est |
