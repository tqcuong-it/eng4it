## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Present Perfect với already / yet

### 📖 Already / Yet dùng khi nào?

- **already** = đã ... rồi (dùng trong câu **khẳng định** — nhấn mạnh việc đã xong **sớm hơn dự kiến**)
- **yet** = chưa / đã ... chưa? (dùng trong câu **phủ định** và **câu hỏi**)

### 🔑 Vị trí trong câu

| Từ | Vị trí | Ví dụ |
|----|--------|-------|
| **already** | Giữa have/has và V3 | I have **already** finished the report. |
| **yet** | Cuối câu | I haven't finished the report **yet**. |
| **yet** (câu hỏi) | Cuối câu | Have you finished the report **yet**? |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:**
> - **already** = "rồi!" → đứng ở **giữa** (đã xong rồi!)
> - **yet** = "chưa?" → đứng ở **cuối** (xong chưa?)

### Ví dụ chi tiết

| Câu | Nghĩa |
|-----|-------|
| I have **already** sent the email. | Tôi **đã** gửi email **rồi**. |
| She has **already** approved the document. | Cô ấy **đã** phê duyệt tài liệu **rồi**. |
| He hasn't submitted the report **yet**. | Anh ấy **chưa** nộp báo cáo. |
| Have you signed the contract **yet**? | Bạn **đã** ký hợp đồng **chưa**? |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Điền already hoặc yet

1. I have ______ finished the meeting notes. (đã xong rồi)
2. Has she called the client ______? (đã chưa?)
3. They haven't received the email ______. (chưa)
4. He has ______ prepared the presentation. (đã xong rồi)
5. We haven't signed the contract ______. (chưa)
6. Have you submitted your report ______? (đã chưa?)

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

Viết câu Present Perfect với **already** hoặc **yet**:

1. she / already / send / the email → ______
2. they / not / approve / the plan / yet → ______
3. you / finish / the report / yet? → ______
4. I / already / attend / the conference → ______
5. he / not / sign / the document / yet → ______
6. the manager / already / postpone / the meeting → ______

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Trả lời câu hỏi

Trả lời theo gợi ý (✅ = already, ❌ = not yet):

1. Have you printed the documents? (✅) → Yes, I ______.
2. Has she submitted the report? (❌) → No, she ______.
3. Have they attended the meeting? (✅) → Yes, they ______.
4. Has he emailed the client? (❌) → No, he ______.
5. Have you prepared the presentation? (✅) → Yes, I ______.
6. Has she approved the budget? (❌) → No, she ______.

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Working from home](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2023-0406)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> It is 3 PM and I am still at the **office**. I have **already** **attended** two **meetings** today. I have **already** **submitted** my weekly **report**. But I haven't **finished** the **presentation** for the **client** **yet**.
>
> My **colleague** has **already** **sent** me the **documents** I need. The **manager** hasn't **approved** the new **contract** **yet**. I have a busy **schedule** today, but I will meet the **deadline**!

*(75 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. How many meetings has the writer attended?
2. Has the writer submitted the weekly report?
3. Has the writer finished the presentation yet?
4. What has the colleague already sent?
5. Has the manager approved the new contract?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | The writer has attended two meetings. |
| 2 | Yes, the writer has already submitted it. |
| 3 | No, the writer hasn't finished it yet. |
| 4 | The colleague has already sent the documents. |
| 5 | No, the manager hasn't approved it yet. |
