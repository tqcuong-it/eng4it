## 🎯 Mục tiêu hôm nay

Luyện trả lời **20 chủ đề Part 1** phổ biến nhất. Mỗi câu trả lời **2-3 câu** — không quá ngắn (thiếu nội dung), không quá dài (lan man).

### Quy tắc vàng cho Part 1:
1. **Answer + Extend** — Trả lời trực tiếp + mở rộng 1-2 câu
2. **Không nói quá 30 giây** mỗi câu
3. **Tự nhiên** — như đang nói chuyện, không đọc thuộc lòng

## 📚 20 Chủ đề Part 1 + Model Answers

### 1. 🏠 Home / Accommodation

**Q: Do you live in a house or an apartment?**

> I live in a small apartment in the city center. It's quite convenient because it's close to my workplace and there are lots of shops nearby. I've been living there for about two years now.

**Useful phrases:** *in the city center, quite convenient, close to..., I've been living there for...*

---

### 2. 💼 Work / Study

**Q: What do you do for a living?**

> I work as a software engineer at a tech company. I mainly focus on building web applications, which I find really interesting because technology is always changing. It keeps me on my toes!

**Useful phrases:** *for a living, mainly focus on, I find it really interesting, keeps me on my toes*

---

### 3. 🎵 Music

**Q: What kind of music do you like?**

> I'm quite into pop and indie music. I usually listen to music when I'm commuting or working out at the gym. It really helps me relax and boosts my mood.

**Useful phrases:** *quite into, when I'm commuting, boosts my mood*

---

### 4. 📖 Reading

**Q: Do you enjoy reading?**

> Yes, I'm a big fan of reading, especially non-fiction books about technology and self-improvement. I try to read at least 30 minutes before bed every night — it helps me unwind after a long day.

**Useful phrases:** *a big fan of, especially..., helps me unwind*

---

### 5. 🍔 Food / Cooking

**Q: Do you like cooking?**

> To be honest, I'm not the best cook, but I enjoy trying simple recipes on weekends. I find it quite therapeutic, and it's definitely healthier than eating out all the time.

**Useful phrases:** *to be honest, not the best at..., quite therapeutic, healthier than...*

---

### 6. 🌤️ Weather

**Q: What's the weather like in your city?**

> The weather in my city is quite varied throughout the year. Summers can be extremely hot and humid, while winters are usually mild. Personally, I prefer autumn because the temperature is just right.

**Useful phrases:** *quite varied, throughout the year, extremely hot and humid, the temperature is just right*

---

### 7. 🏃 Exercise / Sports

**Q: Do you do any sports?**

> I try to go jogging three or four times a week, usually in the evening. I also do some yoga on weekends to stay flexible. I find that regular exercise really helps me sleep better and feel more energetic.

**Useful phrases:** *three or four times a week, stay flexible, regular exercise, feel more energetic*

---

### 8. 📱 Technology

**Q: How often do you use your phone?**

> I'd say I use my phone pretty much all day long — for work emails, social media, and navigation. Sometimes I think I'm a bit too dependent on it, so I try to have some screen-free time in the evenings.

**Useful phrases:** *pretty much all day long, a bit too dependent on, screen-free time*

---

### 9. ✈️ Travel

**Q: Do you like traveling?**

> Absolutely! I love exploring new places and experiencing different cultures. I usually take one or two trips a year, and I prefer traveling to places with rich history and great food.

**Useful phrases:** *exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, rich history*

---

### 10. 👫 Friends

**Q: How often do you see your friends?**

> I try to meet up with my close friends at least once a week, usually for dinner or coffee. We've known each other since university, so we always have a lot to catch up on.

**Useful phrases:** *meet up with, at least once a week, catch up on*

---

### 11. 🎬 Movies / TV

**Q: What kind of movies do you enjoy?**

> I'm really into science fiction and thriller movies. I love films that keep you guessing until the very end. I usually watch movies on streaming platforms rather than going to the cinema.

**Useful phrases:** *really into, keep you guessing, streaming platforms, rather than*

---

### 12. 🛍️ Shopping

**Q: Do you prefer shopping online or in stores?**

> I mostly shop online these days because it's more convenient and I can easily compare prices. But for clothes, I still prefer going to the store so I can try things on before buying.

**Useful phrases:** *mostly, more convenient, compare prices, try things on*

---

### 13. 🌊 Holidays / Weekends

**Q: What do you usually do on weekends?**

> On Saturdays, I usually catch up on housework and run some errands. On Sundays, I prefer to relax — maybe watch a movie, go for a walk, or meet friends for brunch.

**Useful phrases:** *catch up on housework, run errands, go for a walk*

---

### 14. 🏫 Neighborhood

**Q: Do you like the area where you live?**

> Yes, I really like my neighborhood. It's quite quiet and safe, and there are plenty of parks nearby where I can go for a walk. The only downside is that it's a bit far from the city center.

**Useful phrases:** *quite quiet and safe, plenty of, the only downside is*

---

### 15. 🌿 Nature / Gardens

**Q: Do you like spending time in nature?**

> Definitely! I find being in nature very refreshing, especially after a stressful week at work. I sometimes go hiking in the mountains on weekends — it really clears my mind.

**Useful phrases:** *very refreshing, stressful week, clears my mind*

---

### 16. 🎨 Art / Museums

**Q: Are you interested in art?**

> I wouldn't say I'm an expert, but I do appreciate art, especially photography and modern art. I visit galleries occasionally, and I find it fascinating to see how artists express their ideas visually.

**Useful phrases:** *I wouldn't say I'm an expert, I do appreciate, I find it fascinating*

---

### 17. 🚗 Transport

**Q: How do you usually get to work?**

> I usually take the train to work because it's the fastest and most reliable option. The commute takes about 40 minutes, and I usually use that time to listen to podcasts or read.

**Useful phrases:** *the fastest and most reliable, the commute takes about, use that time to*

---

### 18. 🎉 Celebrations

**Q: What's your favorite festival or celebration?**

> I'd say my favorite celebration is Lunar New Year. It's a time when the whole family gets together, and we have a big feast. I love the atmosphere — everyone is in such a good mood.

**Useful phrases:** *I'd say, the whole family gets together, the atmosphere, in a good mood*

---

### 19. 📝 Languages

**Q: Are you learning any other languages?**

> Yes, I'm currently learning English, obviously, and I've also picked up a bit of Japanese since I live in Japan. Learning a new language is challenging but very rewarding.

**Useful phrases:** *currently learning, picked up a bit of, challenging but rewarding*

---

### 20. 💤 Sleep / Daily Routine

**Q: Are you a morning person or a night owl?**

> I'm definitely more of a night owl. I tend to be most productive in the evening, and I usually stay up until midnight. I'm trying to become more of a morning person, but it's a real struggle!

**Useful phrases:** *more of a night owl, most productive, a real struggle*

## 🎙️ Phân tích Model Answer

Hãy xem cấu trúc chung của một câu trả lời Part 1 tốt:

| Phần | Vai trò | Ví dụ |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Direct answer** | Trả lời thẳng câu hỏi | "I live in a small apartment" |
| **Extension 1** | Thêm chi tiết/lý do | "It's quite convenient because..." |
| **Extension 2** | Thêm thông tin cá nhân | "I've been living there for..." |

### ❌ Tránh:
- Trả lời 1 từ: "Yes" / "No"
- Nói quá dài, lan man sang chủ đề khác
- Học thuộc lòng — giám khảo nhận ra ngay!

### ✅ Nên:
- Trả lời 2-3 câu tự nhiên
- Dùng **linking words**: *because, so, but, actually, to be honest*
- Thêm **personal touch**: kinh nghiệm, sở thích, cảm xúc

## 🗣️ Pronunciation Tips

### Word Stress quan trọng trong Part 1:

| Từ | Stress sai ❌ | Stress đúng ✅ |
|----|-------------|--------------|
| convenient | con-VEN-ient | con-**VE**-nient |
| technology | tech-NO-lo-gy | tech-**NOL**-o-gy |
| atmosphere | AT-mos-phere | **AT**-mos-phere |
| experience | ex-PE-ri-ence | ex-**PE**-ri-ence |
| comfortable | com-FOR-ta-ble | **COM**-for-ta-ble |

### Intonation:
- Câu hỏi Yes/No → giọng **lên** ở cuối ↗️
- Câu hỏi Wh- → giọng **xuống** ở cuối ↘️
- Khi liệt kê → giọng **lên** ở mỗi item, **xuống** ở item cuối

## ✏️ Bài tập tự luyện

### Bước 1: Chọn 10 chủ đề bất kỳ từ 20 chủ đề trên
### Bước 2: **Thu âm** câu trả lời (dùng điện thoại)
- Mỗi câu trả lời: **15-25 giây**
- Nói tự nhiên, KHÔNG đọc
### Bước 3: Nghe lại và check:
- [ ] Có trả lời trực tiếp câu hỏi không?
- [ ] Có mở rộng 1-2 câu không?
- [ ] Có dùng linking words không?
- [ ] Phát âm có tự nhiên không? Word stress đúng chưa?
- [ ] Có nói quá nhanh/quá chậm không?

### Bước 4: Nói lại lần 2 — cải thiện những điểm yếu

> 🎯 **Target:** Hoàn thành ít nhất **10 câu trả lời** và thu âm được **5 câu** hài lòng!
