## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Adjectives -ed / -ing

### 📖 Tính từ -ed và -ing khác nhau thế nào?

Đây là lỗi **rất phổ biến** của người Việt! Hãy nhớ:

| Đuôi | Dùng cho | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|-------|
| **-ed** | **NGƯỜI** (cảm thấy) | Người đó CẢM THẤY thế nào | I am **bored**. (Tôi thấy chán.) |
| **-ing** | **VẬT / VIỆC** (gây ra) | Vật/việc đó GÂY RA cảm giác | The movie is **boring**. (Phim đó chán.) |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ siêu đơn giản:**
> - **-ed** = "tôi CẢM THẤY..." (người)
> - **-ing** = "cái đó LÀM CHO..." (vật/việc)

### Các cặp thường gặp

| -ed (người cảm thấy) | -ing (vật/việc gây ra) |
|----------------------|----------------------|
| bor**ed** (chán) | bor**ing** (gây chán) |
| excit**ed** (hào hứng) | excit**ing** (thú vị) |
| tir**ed** (mệt) | tir**ing** (gây mệt) |
| interest**ed** (quan tâm) | interest**ing** (thú vị) |
| surpris**ed** (ngạc nhiên) | surpris**ing** (gây ngạc nhiên) |
| disappoint**ed** (thất vọng) | disappoint**ing** (gây thất vọng) |
| confus**ed** (bối rối) | confus**ing** (gây bối rối) |
| relax**ed** (thư giãn) | relax**ing** (gây thư giãn) |

### Ví dụ so sánh

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng | Giải thích |
|--------|---------|------------|
| I am **boring**. | I am **bored**. | "Tôi thấy chán" → dùng -ed |
| The game is **excited**. | The game is **exciting**. | "Trò chơi gây hào hứng" → dùng -ing |
| She is **interesting** in music. | She is **interested** in music. | "Cô ấy quan tâm đến" → dùng -ed |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chọn -ed hoặc -ing

1. The book is very ______. (interest)
2. I am ______ about the trip. (excite)
3. This exercise is ______. (tire)
4. She feels ______ after the massage. (relax)
5. The news was ______. (surprise)
6. He is ______ with the result. (disappoint)
7. The movie is ______. (bore)
8. I am ______ by this grammar rule. (confuse)

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Sửa lỗi sai

1. I am very boring today. Nothing to do.
2. The test was very stressed.
3. She is interesting in learning English.
4. The trip was very tired.
5. He is very exciting about the party.
6. The lesson is very confused.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Điền từ phù hợp

Dùng từ trong ngoặc ở dạng -ed hoặc -ing:

1. I feel ______ when I am alone. (lonely — không đổi)
2. Running every day is ______. (tire)
3. The children are ______ to see Santa Claus. (excite)
4. The doctor's answer was ______. I didn't understand. (confuse)
5. She was ______ to hear the bad news. (shock)
6. It was a very ______ day at the hospital. (stress)

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Emotions](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2024-0425)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Today I feel many emotions. In the morning, I am **excited** because I have a day off. But then I get a phone call — my friend is **sick**. I feel **worried** about her.
>
> I visit her at the **hospital**. She looks **tired** but she smiles. I bring her flowers and she feels **surprised** and **happy**. "Thank you! That's so **nice**!" she says.
>
> I am **grateful** that she is getting better. Helping others is never **boring** — it is always **rewarding**. I go home feeling **calm** and **relaxed**.

*(80 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. How does the person feel in the morning?
2. Why is the person worried?
3. How does the friend look at the hospital?
4. What does the person bring?
5. How does the person feel at the end?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | Excited. | Câu "I am excited because I have a day off" |
| 2 | Because the friend is sick. | Câu "my friend is sick. I feel worried" |
| 3 | Tired. | Câu "She looks tired but she smiles" |
| 4 | Flowers. | Câu "I bring her flowers" |
| 5 | Calm and relaxed. | Câu "I go home feeling calm and relaxed" |
