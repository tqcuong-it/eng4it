## 🎯 Mục tiêu hôm nay

Để đạt **Band 6.0+** cho Grammatical Range & Accuracy, bạn cần dùng được **complex structures** khi nói. Hôm nay học 3 cấu trúc quan trọng nhất:

- 🔀 **Conditionals** (câu điều kiện)
- 🔄 **Passive Voice** (bị động)
- 🔗 **Relative Clauses** (mệnh đề quan hệ)

---

## 🔀 Conditionals (Câu điều kiện)

### Type 0: General Truth (Sự thật chung)
> **If/When + present simple, present simple**

| Ví dụ Speaking | Tình huống |
|---------------|-----------|
| "**When** it rains, I usually stay home and read." | Part 1: Weather |
| "**If** you eat too much junk food, you gain weight." | Part 3: Health |

### Type 1: Real / Likely Future (Có thể xảy ra)
> **If + present simple, will + V**

| Ví dụ Speaking | Tình huống |
|---------------|-----------|
| "**If** I get a promotion, I**'ll** probably move to a bigger apartment." | Part 1: Work |
| "**If** governments invest more in education, the economy **will** improve." | Part 3: Education |

### Type 2: Unreal / Hypothetical (Giả định — không có thật)
> **If + past simple, would + V**

| Ví dụ Speaking | Tình huống |
|---------------|-----------|
| "**If** I **had** more free time, I **would** travel around Europe." | Part 1: Travel |
| "**If** I **were** the prime minister, I **would** invest more in renewable energy." | Part 3: Environment |
| "**If** money **weren't** an issue, I **would** quit my job and become an artist." | Part 2: Dreams |

### Type 3: Unreal Past (Giả định quá khứ — đã không xảy ra)
> **If + past perfect, would have + V3**

| Ví dụ Speaking | Tình huống |
|---------------|-----------|
| "**If** I **had studied** harder, I **would have gotten** a scholarship." | Part 2: Regrets |
| "**If** I **hadn't moved** to Japan, I **wouldn't have met** my wife." | Part 2: Decisions |

### Mixed Conditionals (Trộn — Band 7+)
> **If + past perfect, would + V** (quá khứ → hiện tại)

| Ví dụ Speaking |
|---------------|
| "**If** I **had been born** in a different country, my life **would be** completely different now." |
| "**If** I **hadn't learned** English, I **wouldn't be** working in Japan today." |

### 🎤 Practice: Dùng Conditionals trong Speaking

**Q: What would you do if you won the lottery?**
> "If I won the lottery, I'd first pay off my mortgage and set up a college fund for my daughter. Then I'd probably invest in starting my own tech company — that's always been my dream. If I still had money left over, I'd donate some to educational charities because I believe everyone deserves equal access to education."

**Q: Do you have any regrets?**
> "If I had started learning English earlier, I think my career would have progressed even faster. But honestly, I try not to dwell on regrets — if I hadn't taken the path I did, I wouldn't be where I am today, and I'm quite happy with my life."

## 🔄 Passive Voice (Bị động)

### Khi nào dùng Passive trong Speaking?
- Khi **không biết/không quan trọng** ai làm
- Khi muốn nhấn mạnh **đối tượng** chịu tác động
- Khi nói về **quy trình, quy luật xã hội**

### Cấu trúc: **Subject + be + V3 (+ by agent)**

| Active | Passive |
|--------|---------|
| People use technology every day. | Technology **is used** every day. |
| The government should ban plastic bags. | Plastic bags **should be banned**. |
| They built this temple 500 years ago. | This temple **was built** 500 years ago. |
| Companies are replacing workers with AI. | Workers **are being replaced** by AI. |

### 🎤 Practice: Dùng Passive trong Speaking

**Part 1:**
> "My city **is known for** its beautiful temples and delicious street food."
> "I **was raised** in a small town in central Vietnam."

**Part 2:**
> "The event **was organized** by our university's student council."
> "The park **is surrounded by** tall buildings and busy roads."

**Part 3:**
> "I think more money **should be invested** in public healthcare."
> "Traditional crafts **are being forgotten** by younger generations."
> "The education system **needs to be reformed** to meet modern demands."

### Passive + Modal Verbs (Rất hữu ích cho Part 3):

| Structure | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| should be + V3 | "Students **should be encouraged** to think critically." |
| could be + V3 | "This problem **could be solved** by better policies." |
| needs to be + V3 | "The law **needs to be updated** to reflect modern values." |
| is expected to be + V3 | "AI **is expected to be used** in most industries." |

## 🔗 Relative Clauses (Mệnh đề quan hệ)

### Defining (Xác định — cần thiết cho nghĩa câu)

| Relative Pronoun | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|-----------------|---------|-------|
| **who** | người | "People **who** exercise regularly live longer." |
| **which/that** | vật | "Books **that** I read as a child influenced me greatly." |
| **where** | nơi chốn | "The city **where** I grew up has changed a lot." |
| **when** | thời gian | "I remember the day **when** I arrived in Japan." |
| **whose** | sở hữu | "I have a friend **whose** father is a doctor." |

### Non-defining (Không xác định — thêm thông tin)

> Dùng **dấu phẩy** trước và sau

| Ví dụ |
|-------|
| "My hometown, **which is in central Vietnam**, is famous for its beaches." |
| "My father, **who retired last year**, now spends his time gardening." |
| "Tokyo, **where I currently work**, is one of the biggest cities in the world." |

### 🎤 Practice: Dùng Relative Clauses trong Speaking

**Part 1:**
> "I live in Saitama, **which is** a prefecture just north of Tokyo."
> "The person **who inspired** me most is my father."

**Part 2:**
> "I'd like to talk about a teacher **who** had a huge impact on my life."
> "It was a book **that** completely changed my perspective on life."
> "The city **where** I spent my childhood holds many wonderful memories."

**Part 3:**
> "People **who** are exposed to different cultures tend to be more open-minded."
> "Countries **that** invest in education usually have stronger economies."
> "The internet, **which** has connected billions of people, has both advantages and disadvantages."

## 🎯 Combining All Three — Band 7 Answers

**Q: How has your city changed over the years?**

> "My hometown, **which is** a coastal city in Vietnam, **has been transformed** significantly over the past decade. **If** you **had visited** 10 years ago, you **wouldn't** recognize it today. New shopping malls **have been built**, and traditional markets **that** used to be the heart of the community **are being replaced** by modern supermarkets. **If** the government **invested** more in preserving historical areas, I think the city **would** maintain more of its original character. The old temples, **which were built** hundreds of years ago, **should be protected** rather than demolished for development."

👆 Câu trả lời này dùng: ✅ Relative clauses ✅ Passive voice ✅ Conditionals (Type 2 & 3) ✅ Mixed tenses

## 🗣️ Pronunciation Tips cho Grammar Structures

### Conditionals — nối âm tự nhiên:
| Viết | Nói |
|------|-----|
| If I had | /ɪfaɪ**d**/ (If I'd) |
| would have | /wʊd**əv**/ |
| could have been | /kʊd**əv**bɪn/ |

### Passive — stress vào past participle:
> "It was **BUILT** in the 18th century."
> "They should be **EN**couraged to..."

### Relative Clauses — pause nhẹ trước non-defining:
> "My city [pause] which is in central Vietnam [pause] is beautiful."

## ✏️ Bài tập tự luyện

### Bài 1: Grammar Transformation (15 phút)
Biến đổi 5 câu simple thành complex dùng conditionals, passive, hoặc relative clauses:

1. "I like my job." → "My job, **which involves** solving technical problems, is something I really enjoy."
2. "Technology changed education." → "Education **has been transformed** by technology."
3. "I want to travel more." → "**If** I **had** more vacation days, I **would** travel more."

### Bài 2: Speaking with Grammar Focus (20 phút)
Trả lời 5 câu hỏi — mỗi câu phải dùng ít nhất **2 complex structures**:
1. Describe your hometown
2. What would you change about your education?
3. How should governments deal with pollution?
4. Describe a person who influenced you
5. Do you think life was better in the past?

### Bài 3: Error Correction (10 phút)
Sửa lỗi trong các câu sau:
1. "If I would have more time, I will travel." ❌
2. "The building who was built 100 years ago is beautiful." ❌
3. "The problem should solved by the government." ❌

**Đáp án:**
1. "If I **had** more time, I **would** travel." ✅
2. "The building **which/that** was built 100 years ago is beautiful." ✅
3. "The problem should **be** solved by the government." ✅

> 🎯 **Target:** Dùng tự nhiên ít nhất **2 complex structures** trong mỗi câu trả lời Part 3!
