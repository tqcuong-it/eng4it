## 🌍 Environment Vocabulary

### Core Collocations:

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| **climate change** | biến đổi khí hậu | Climate change is the biggest threat we face. |
| **carbon footprint** | lượng khí thải carbon | We should all try to reduce our carbon footprint. |
| **renewable energy** | năng lượng tái tạo | Many countries are investing in renewable energy. |
| **global warming** | nóng lên toàn cầu | Global warming is causing extreme weather events. |
| **air/water pollution** | ô nhiễm không khí/nước | Air pollution is a serious problem in big cities. |
| **single-use plastic** | nhựa dùng 1 lần | We should ban single-use plastic bags. |
| **endangered species** | loài có nguy cơ tuyệt chủng | Many endangered species need urgent protection. |
| **sustainable development** | phát triển bền vững | Sustainable development balances growth and conservation. |
| **eco-friendly** | thân thiện môi trường | I try to buy eco-friendly products. |
| **carbon emissions** | khí thải carbon | Governments must reduce carbon emissions. |
| **deforestation** | phá rừng | Deforestation is destroying vital ecosystems. |
| **biodiversity** | đa dạng sinh học | We need to protect biodiversity for future generations. |

### Advanced Phrases:

- **"raise awareness about"** — nâng cao nhận thức về
- **"take drastic measures"** — thực hiện biện pháp quyết liệt
- **"have a devastating impact on"** — có tác động tàn khốc lên
- **"the point of no return"** — điểm không thể quay lại
- **"leave a better world for future generations"** — để lại thế giới tốt hơn cho thế hệ sau

### 🎤 Sample Questions + Answers (Environment):

**Q: What do you think is the biggest environmental problem today?**

> I'd say **climate change** is undoubtedly the most pressing environmental issue we face today. It's having a **devastating impact on** ecosystems worldwide — from melting polar ice caps to increasingly **extreme weather events**. What worries me most is that if we don't **take drastic measures** to reduce **carbon emissions** soon, we might reach **the point of no return**. I think governments need to invest much more heavily in **renewable energy** and **sustainable development** to address this crisis.

**Q: What can individuals do to help the environment?**

> There are plenty of small but meaningful steps individuals can take. For instance, reducing our **carbon footprint** by using public transport, cutting down on **single-use plastic**, and supporting **eco-friendly** brands. We can also **raise awareness** among friends and family about environmental issues. While individual actions alone won't solve the problem, they create momentum for larger systemic changes.

## 💻 Technology Vocabulary

### Core Collocations:

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| **artificial intelligence (AI)** | trí tuệ nhân tạo | AI is transforming many industries. |
| **social media** | mạng xã hội | Social media has changed how we communicate. |
| **digital divide** | khoảng cách số | The digital divide between rich and poor is growing. |
| **data privacy** | quyền riêng tư dữ liệu | Data privacy is a major concern these days. |
| **screen time** | thời gian dùng màn hình | Too much screen time can affect your health. |
| **cutting-edge technology** | công nghệ tiên tiến nhất | Japan is known for cutting-edge technology. |
| **tech-savvy** | am hiểu công nghệ | Young people are generally more tech-savvy. |
| **cybersecurity** | an ninh mạng | Cybersecurity is crucial in the digital age. |
| **remote work** | làm việc từ xa | Remote work has become the new normal. |
| **automation** | tự động hóa | Automation is replacing many manual jobs. |
| **digital literacy** | kỹ năng số | Schools should teach digital literacy. |
| **online platform** | nền tảng trực tuyến | Online platforms have revolutionized education. |

### Advanced Phrases:

- **"a double-edged sword"** — con dao hai lưỡi
- **"blur the line between... and..."** — xóa nhòa ranh giới giữa... và...
- **"keep up with technological advances"** — theo kịp tiến bộ công nghệ
- **"become overly dependent on"** — trở nên phụ thuộc quá mức vào
- **"bridge the digital divide"** — thu hẹp khoảng cách số

### 🎤 Sample Questions + Answers (Technology):

**Q: How has technology changed people's lives?**

> Technology has fundamentally transformed almost every aspect of our lives. **Remote work** has become the new normal, allowing people to work from anywhere. **Social media** has revolutionized communication — we can now connect with anyone instantly. **Online platforms** have democratized education, making knowledge accessible to millions. However, technology is **a double-edged sword** — while it brings convenience, issues like **data privacy**, excessive **screen time**, and people becoming **overly dependent on** devices are real concerns.

**Q: Do you think AI will replace human workers?**

> That's a complex question. **Automation** and **AI** are certainly replacing some manual and repetitive jobs — that's already happening in manufacturing and customer service. However, I don't think AI will completely replace humans because there are things that require creativity, empathy, and critical thinking that machines simply can't replicate. The key is to **keep up with technological advances** by continuously learning new skills. I think **digital literacy** should be a priority in education so people can adapt to the changing job market.

## ✏️ Bài tập tự luyện

### Bài 1: Vocabulary Drill (15 phút)
- Đọc to tất cả collocations — 3 lần mỗi cụm
- Chú ý pronunciation và word stress

### Bài 2: Sentence Building (15 phút)
- Dùng **10 collocations** để tạo câu riêng (không copy model answers)
- Thu âm mỗi câu

### Bài 3: Speaking Practice (20 phút)
- Trả lời 4 câu hỏi sample ở trên bằng **lời riêng**
- Cố gắng dùng ít nhất **5 collocations mới** trong mỗi câu
- Thu âm và nghe lại

### Bài 4: Paraphrase Challenge (10 phút)
- Lấy 1 model answer → nói lại cùng ý nhưng **đổi từ vựng**
- So sánh 2 versions

> 🎯 **Target:** Thuộc ít nhất **15 collocations mới** và dùng được trong câu trả lời!
