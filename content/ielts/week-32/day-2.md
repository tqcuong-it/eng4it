## 📚 Education Vocabulary

### Core Collocations:

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| **higher education** | giáo dục đại học | Higher education is becoming more expensive. |
| **critical thinking** | tư duy phản biện | Schools should focus more on critical thinking. |
| **rote learning** | học thuộc lòng | Rote learning is still common in many Asian countries. |
| **student loan** | khoản vay sinh viên | Many graduates struggle with student loans. |
| **academic pressure** | áp lực học tập | Academic pressure can affect mental health. |
| **lifelong learning** | học suốt đời | Lifelong learning is essential in today's world. |
| **vocational training** | đào tạo nghề | Vocational training provides practical job skills. |
| **curriculum reform** | cải cách chương trình | We need curriculum reform to match modern needs. |
| **extracurricular activities** | hoạt động ngoại khóa | Extracurricular activities develop soft skills. |
| **equal access to education** | tiếp cận giáo dục bình đẳng | Every child deserves equal access to education. |
| **distance learning** | học từ xa | Distance learning has grown rapidly since 2020. |
| **tuition fees** | học phí | Rising tuition fees are a barrier for many students. |

### Advanced Phrases:

- **"nurture creativity"** — nuôi dưỡng sáng tạo
- **"bridge the gap between theory and practice"** — thu hẹp khoảng cách giữa lý thuyết và thực hành
- **"a well-rounded education"** — giáo dục toàn diện
- **"learn by doing"** — học qua thực hành
- **"equip students with real-world skills"** — trang bị kỹ năng thực tế cho sinh viên

### 🎤 Sample Questions + Answers (Education):

**Q: Do you think the education system needs to change?**

> Absolutely. I think most education systems are still too focused on **rote learning** and exams rather than developing **critical thinking** and creativity. We need **curriculum reform** that helps **equip students with real-world skills** — things like problem-solving, communication, and financial literacy. I also believe **vocational training** should be given equal respect as **higher education**, because not everyone needs a university degree to have a successful career. The goal should be **a well-rounded education** that prepares young people for the actual challenges they'll face.

**Q: Is distance learning as effective as traditional classroom learning?**

> **Distance learning** has certainly improved dramatically, especially with modern technology. It offers flexibility and **equal access to education** regardless of location — someone in a rural village can now take courses from top universities. However, I don't think it's fully replaced traditional learning yet. Face-to-face interaction with teachers and peers is incredibly valuable, especially for younger students who need social development. The ideal approach, I think, is blended learning — combining the best of both worlds.

## 🏙️ Society Vocabulary

### Core Collocations:

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| **income inequality** | bất bình đẳng thu nhập | Income inequality is a growing global concern. |
| **aging population** | dân số già hóa | Japan has a rapidly aging population. |
| **work-life balance** | cân bằng công việc-cuộc sống | Maintaining work-life balance is challenging. |
| **gender equality** | bình đẳng giới | We've made progress on gender equality but there's more to do. |
| **social welfare** | phúc lợi xã hội | Strong social welfare systems protect the vulnerable. |
| **cultural diversity** | đa dạng văn hóa | Cultural diversity enriches society. |
| **crime rate** | tỷ lệ tội phạm | The crime rate has decreased in recent years. |
| **public awareness** | nhận thức cộng đồng | We need to raise public awareness about mental health. |
| **generation gap** | khoảng cách thế hệ | The generation gap is widening due to technology. |
| **urban migration** | di cư đô thị | Urban migration puts pressure on city infrastructure. |
| **community spirit** | tinh thần cộng đồng | Community spirit is weaker in big cities. |
| **social isolation** | cô lập xã hội | Social media paradoxically increases social isolation. |

### Advanced Phrases:

- **"a pressing issue"** — vấn đề cấp bách
- **"the root cause of"** — nguyên nhân gốc rễ của
- **"play a pivotal role in"** — đóng vai trò then chốt trong
- **"have far-reaching consequences"** — có hậu quả sâu rộng
- **"a shift in mindset"** — sự thay đổi tư duy

### 🎤 Sample Questions + Answers (Society):

**Q: What are the biggest challenges facing your country today?**

> I think one of the most **pressing issues** in Japan is the **aging population**. The birth rate has been declining for decades, which means there are fewer working-age people to support the growing elderly population. This has **far-reaching consequences** for the economy, healthcare system, and **social welfare** programs. Another challenge is **work-life balance** — Japanese work culture is notorious for long hours, which affects both mental health and the birth rate. I think there needs to be **a shift in mindset** about work culture to address these interconnected problems.

**Q: How has society changed in the last 50 years?**

> Society has changed dramatically in so many ways. Perhaps the biggest change is in **gender equality** — women now have far more opportunities in education and the workplace than 50 years ago. **Cultural diversity** has also increased significantly due to globalization and migration. On the flip side, **community spirit** seems to have weakened, especially in urban areas where people barely know their neighbors. **Social isolation** has become more common, paradoxically despite being more "connected" than ever through technology. There's also been a widening **generation gap** — young people today have completely different values and priorities compared to their grandparents.

## ✏️ Bài tập tự luyện

### Bài 1: Collocation Match (10 phút)
- Che cột "Ví dụ" → nhìn collocation → tự đặt câu
- Check lại xem câu có tự nhiên không

### Bài 2: Topic Monologue (20 phút)
- Nói 2 phút về: **"Changes in education in your country"**
- Nói 2 phút về: **"The biggest social problem today"**
- Thu âm, nghe lại — đã dùng bao nhiêu collocations mới?

### Bài 3: Quick-Fire Answers (15 phút)
- Trả lời 4 sample questions ở trên bằng **lời riêng**
- Target: dùng ít nhất **5 collocations** mỗi câu
- Thu âm mỗi câu

### Bài 4: Vocabulary Review (10 phút)
- Viết **10 collocations** mới nhất vào flashcard
- Mặt trước: tiếng Anh | Mặt sau: nghĩa + câu ví dụ

> 🎯 **Target:** Thuộc **15 collocations mới** và dùng tự nhiên trong câu trả lời!
