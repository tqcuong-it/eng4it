## 🏥 Health Vocabulary

### Core Collocations:

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| **mental health** | sức khỏe tinh thần | Mental health is just as important as physical health. |
| **balanced diet** | chế độ ăn cân bằng | A balanced diet is key to staying healthy. |
| **sedentary lifestyle** | lối sống ít vận động | A sedentary lifestyle increases health risks. |
| **physical fitness** | thể chất | Regular exercise improves physical fitness. |
| **healthcare system** | hệ thống y tế | Japan has an excellent healthcare system. |
| **work-related stress** | stress do công việc | Work-related stress is a major health concern. |
| **life expectancy** | tuổi thọ trung bình | Life expectancy has increased dramatically. |
| **preventive medicine** | y học dự phòng | Preventive medicine is more cost-effective than treatment. |
| **junk food** | đồ ăn nhanh/rác | Excessive junk food consumption leads to obesity. |
| **well-being** | sức khỏe tổng thể | Companies should prioritize employee well-being. |
| **public health** | sức khỏe cộng đồng | Public health campaigns save millions of lives. |
| **chronic disease** | bệnh mãn tính | Chronic diseases like diabetes are on the rise. |

### Advanced Phrases:

- **"take a toll on"** — gây tổn hại cho
- **"lead an active lifestyle"** — sống năng động
- **"raise awareness about mental health"** — nâng cao nhận thức về sức khỏe tinh thần
- **"prevention is better than cure"** — phòng bệnh hơn chữa bệnh
- **"the epidemic of loneliness"** — đại dịch cô đơn

### 🎤 Sample Questions + Answers (Health):

**Q: Why do many people have unhealthy lifestyles?**

> I think there are several factors contributing to unhealthy lifestyles. Firstly, modern work culture encourages **sedentary lifestyles** — people sit at desks for 8-10 hours a day, which **takes a toll on** their physical health. Secondly, **junk food** is often cheaper and more convenient than healthy options, making it the default choice for busy workers. **Work-related stress** also plays a major role — when people are exhausted, they're less likely to exercise or cook a **balanced diet**. I think **raising awareness** about these issues and making healthy options more accessible are crucial steps.

**Q: How can governments improve public health?**

> Governments can take several approaches. **Preventive medicine** should be a priority — investing in regular health check-ups, vaccination programs, and **public health** campaigns is much more cost-effective than treating **chronic diseases** after they develop. They could also regulate the food industry — for example, taxing sugary drinks and requiring clearer nutrition labels. Additionally, I think governments should address **mental health** more seriously, as **the epidemic of loneliness** and stress is a growing crisis. Providing free counseling services and promoting **well-being** in workplaces would make a significant difference.

## 🎭 Culture Vocabulary

### Core Collocations:

| Collocation | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| **cultural heritage** | di sản văn hóa | We must protect our cultural heritage. |
| **traditional customs** | phong tục truyền thống | Traditional customs are fading in modern cities. |
| **cultural identity** | bản sắc văn hóa | Globalization can threaten cultural identity. |
| **cultural exchange** | giao lưu văn hóa | Cultural exchange promotes mutual understanding. |
| **generation gap** | khoảng cách thế hệ | There's a growing generation gap in cultural values. |
| **cultural shock** | sốc văn hóa | I experienced culture shock when I first moved abroad. |
| **cultural awareness** | nhận thức văn hóa | Cultural awareness is essential for international work. |
| **folk tradition** | truyền thống dân gian | Folk traditions tell us about our ancestors' lives. |
| **cultural assimilation** | đồng hóa văn hóa | Cultural assimilation can lead to loss of identity. |
| **intangible heritage** | di sản phi vật thể | Languages and music are forms of intangible heritage. |
| **multicultural society** | xã hội đa văn hóa | Living in a multicultural society broadens perspectives. |
| **preserve traditions** | bảo tồn truyền thống | It's important to preserve traditions for future generations. |

### Advanced Phrases:

- **"a melting pot of cultures"** — nồi nấu chảy văn hóa (nơi đa dạng VH)
- **"strike a balance between... and..."** — cân bằng giữa... và...
- **"pass down from generation to generation"** — truyền từ đời này sang đời khác
- **"at the expense of"** — bằng cái giá của
- **"embrace diversity"** — đón nhận sự đa dạng

### 🎤 Sample Questions + Answers (Culture):

**Q: How important is it to preserve traditional culture?**

> I believe it's extremely important to **preserve traditions** and **cultural heritage**, as they form the foundation of our **cultural identity**. **Traditional customs** that have been **passed down from generation to generation** give people a sense of belonging and connection to their roots. For instance, festivals like Lunar New Year in Vietnam or Obon in Japan aren't just celebrations — they carry deep cultural meaning and bring communities together.
>
> However, I think we need to **strike a balance between** preservation and progress. We shouldn't **preserve traditions at the expense of** social development. Some practices may be outdated, and it's okay to adapt them to modern times while keeping their essence. The key is to **embrace diversity** and evolution while honoring our roots.

**Q: Has globalization affected local cultures?**

> Globalization has definitely had a major impact on local cultures, and I'd say it's been both positive and negative. On the positive side, **cultural exchange** has increased dramatically — people now have access to music, food, and art from around the world, which broadens their perspectives. Cities have become **melting pots of cultures**, which I think is wonderful.
>
> On the negative side, there's a real risk of **cultural assimilation** — smaller cultures being absorbed by dominant Western culture. Many young people in Asian countries now prefer Western fashion, music, and food over their own **folk traditions**. This widening **generation gap** in cultural values is concerning. I think the solution is to actively promote **cultural awareness** and education so that people appreciate both global culture and their own heritage.
