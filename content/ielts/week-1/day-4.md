## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Ôn tập giữa tuần

### 📖 So sánh Present Simple vs Present Continuous

| | Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|---|---|---|
| **Dùng khi** | Thói quen, sự thật | Đang xảy ra ngay bây giờ |
| **Dấu hiệu** | every day, always, usually | now, right now, at the moment |
| **Công thức** | S + V(s/es) | S + am/is/are + V-ing |
| **Ví dụ** | I **eat** rice every day. | I **am eating** rice now. |

### ✏️ Mini Test giữa tuần (10 câu)

**Phần A: Chọn thì đúng (Present Simple hoặc Present Continuous)**

1. She ______ (drink) coffee every morning.
2. Look! The baby ______ (sleep).
3. I usually ______ (eat) rice for lunch.
4. Right now, he ______ (read) a book.
5. They always ______ (go) to work at eight.

**Phần B: Chia động từ đúng**

6. My wife ______ (cook) dinner now. She always ______ (cook) at six.
7. I ______ (have) two children. My brother ______ (have) three children.
8. We ______ (learn) English now. We ______ (learn) every day.

**Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai**

9. He don't like cold water.
10. She is eat rice now.

### ✏️ Bài tập viết: Tự giới thiệu (50 từ)

Viết một đoạn văn ngắn **50 từ** giới thiệu bản thân. Dùng các từ đã học!

**Gợi ý:**
- My name is ______
- I live in ______
- I work as a ______
- I have ______ (family)
- I like ______ (food/drink)
- Every day, I ______

**Ví dụ mẫu:**

> My name is Cuong. I live in Japan. I work as a developer. I have a beautiful wife and one baby girl.
>
> I always eat rice for lunch. I usually drink two cups of coffee in the morning. I like fish and vegetables. I learn English every day.

*(48 từ)*

---


### ✅ Đáp án Mini Test

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | drinks | "every morning" = thói quen → Present Simple, She → +s |
| 2 | is sleeping | "Look!" = đang xảy ra → Present Continuous |
| 3 | eat | "usually" = thói quen → Present Simple, I → nguyên mẫu |
| 4 | is reading | "Right now" = đang xảy ra → Present Continuous |
| 5 | go | "always" = thói quen → Present Simple, They → nguyên mẫu |
| 6 | is cooking / cooks | "now" → Continuous / "always at six" → Simple |
| 7 | have / has | I → have / My brother (He) → has |
| 8 | are learning / learn | "now" → Continuous / "every day" → Simple |
| 9 | He **doesn't** like cold water. | He → dùng doesn't (không phải don't) |
| 10 | She is **eating** rice now. | Present Continuous: is + V-**ing** (không phải is + V) |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghỉ nhẹ! 🎵

Hôm nay đã ôn tập nhiều rồi. Thay vì BBC, hãy **nghe 1 bài hát tiếng Anh** bạn yêu thích và cố đọc theo lời (lyrics)!

Gợi ý bài dễ nghe:
- *Yesterday* — The Beatles
- *Count on Me* — Bruno Mars
- *Hello* — Adele

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> I am **hungry**! I **want** to **eat** something **delicious**. I **usually** eat **rice** with **fish** or **chicken**. But today, I **want** **bread** and **egg**.
>
> I am also **thirsty**. I **always** **drink** **coffee** in the **morning**, but now it is **evening**. I **want** **cold** **water** and some **hot** **tea**.
>
> My **wife** is cooking **vegetable** soup. It is very **delicious**! I **like** her **food**.

*(65 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. Is the person **hungry** or **thirsty**?
2. What does he **usually** eat?
3. What does he want to drink?
4. **Who** is cooking?
5. Is the soup delicious?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | Both! He is hungry and thirsty. | "I am hungry" và "I am also thirsty" |
| 2 | Rice with fish or chicken. | "I usually eat rice with fish or chicken" |
| 3 | Cold water and hot tea. | "I want cold water and some hot tea" |
| 4 | His wife. | "My wife is cooking" |
| 5 | Yes, it is very delicious. | "It is very delicious" |
