## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Relative Clauses với WHO

### 📖 Relative Clauses là gì?

**Relative Clauses** (Mệnh đề quan hệ) là mệnh đề **bổ sung thông tin** cho danh từ đứng trước nó. Hôm nay chúng ta học **WHO** — dùng để nói thêm về **người**.

### 🔑 Quy tắc dùng WHO

**WHO** thay thế cho **chủ ngữ là người** trong mệnh đề:

| Hai câu riêng | Kết hợp với WHO |
|----------------|-----------------|
| The man is my teacher. He works here. | The man **who works here** is my teacher. |
| I know a girl. She speaks 5 languages. | I know a girl **who speaks 5 languages**. |
| The doctor is very kind. She helped me. | The doctor **who helped me** is very kind. |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:** WHO = "**người mà**" → The man **who** works here = Người đàn ông **mà** làm việc ở đây

### Cấu trúc

```
Danh từ (người) + WHO + động từ + ...
```

Ví dụ:
- The **student who studies** hard will pass the exam. (Học sinh **mà học** chăm sẽ đỗ.)
- I like **people who communicate** clearly. (Tôi thích **người mà giao tiếp** rõ ràng.)
- The **woman who apologized** was very polite. (Người phụ nữ **mà xin lỗi** rất lịch sự.)

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Nối câu bằng WHO

Nối hai câu thành một câu dùng **who**:

1. The man is a doctor. He lives next door.
2. I have a friend. She speaks English well.
3. The teacher is very strict. He teaches math.
4. The woman is my sister. She works at a bank.
5. I know a person. He can translate Vietnamese to English.
6. The student passed the exam. She studied every day.
7. My neighbor is very friendly. He always greets me.
8. The boy is my son. He is talking to the teacher.
9. The girl won the prize. She expressed her ideas clearly.
10. I met a man. He can speak 6 languages.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Điền WHO vào đúng vị trí

Thêm **who** vào vị trí phù hợp:

1. The person ______ called you is my boss.
2. Students ______ study hard get good results.
3. I like people ______ communicate honestly.
4. The doctor ______ treated me was very kind.
5. Anyone ______ disagrees can speak now.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

Mỗi câu có 1 lỗi sai. Tìm và sửa:

1. The man which works here is my friend.
2. The woman who she speaks English is my teacher.
3. I know a person who can translates this.
4. People who is kind are always happy.
5. The student who study hard will succeed.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | The man **who lives next door** is a doctor. |
| 2 | I have a friend **who speaks English well**. |
| 3 | The teacher **who teaches math** is very strict. |
| 4 | The woman **who works at a bank** is my sister. |
| 5 | I know a person **who can translate** Vietnamese to English. |
| 6 | The student **who studied every day** passed the exam. |
| 7 | My neighbor **who always greets me** is very friendly. |
| 8 | The boy **who is talking to the teacher** is my son. |
| 9 | The girl **who expressed her ideas clearly** won the prize. |
| 10 | I met a man **who can speak 6 languages**. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | who | The person **who** called you → bổ sung thông tin về person |
| 2 | who | Students **who** study hard → bổ sung thông tin về students |
| 3 | who | people **who** communicate → bổ sung thông tin về people |
| 4 | who | The doctor **who** treated me → bổ sung thông tin về doctor |
| 5 | who | Anyone **who** disagrees → bổ sung thông tin về anyone |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | which | The man **who** works here... | Nói về người → dùng who, không dùng which |
| 2 | who she | The woman **who** speaks English... | Sau who không cần thêm she (who đã thay thế) |
| 3 | translates | ...who can **translate** this. | Sau can → động từ nguyên mẫu |
| 4 | is | People who **are** kind... | People số nhiều → dùng are |
| 5 | study | The student who **studies** hard... | Student số ít → thêm -s |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - How to have a conversation](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2023-1109)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Communication is very important in our lives. People **who communicate** well often succeed at work. Good communication means you can **express** your ideas clearly and **respond** to others politely.
>
> Some people **who are shy** find it hard to **discuss** things in groups. But anyone **who practices** every day can improve. Don't be afraid to **apologize** when you make mistakes — people **who admit** their errors earn respect.

*(70 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. Why is communication important?
2. What can shy people do to improve?
3. What happens when you apologize for mistakes?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | People who communicate well often succeed at work. |
| 2 | They can practice every day to improve. |
| 3 | People who admit their errors earn respect. |
