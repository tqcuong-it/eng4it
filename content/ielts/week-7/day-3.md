## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Reported Speech (Câu tường thuật — Giới thiệu)

### 📖 Reported Speech là gì?

**Reported Speech** (Câu tường thuật) dùng khi bạn **thuật lại** lời người khác nói.

| Direct Speech (Lời nói trực tiếp) | Reported Speech (Câu tường thuật) |
|-------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| She **said**: "I am busy." | She **said that** she **was** busy. |
| He **said**: "I like coffee." | He **said that** he **liked** coffee. |
| They **said**: "We are happy." | They **said that** they **were** happy. |

### 🔑 Quy tắc cơ bản

Khi chuyển sang Reported Speech, **lùi thì** (backshift):

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech |
|---------------|-----------------|
| am / is | → **was** |
| are | → **were** |
| like / work (Present Simple) | → **liked / worked** (Past Simple) |
| can | → **could** |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:** Reported Speech = **lùi 1 thì** → Present → Past

### Thay đổi đại từ

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| I | → he / she |
| we | → they |
| my | → his / her |
| our | → their |

Ví dụ:
- "**I** am busy." → She said that **she** was busy.
- "**We** like this." → They said that **they** liked this.

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chuyển sang Reported Speech

Chuyển các câu sau sang câu tường thuật:

1. She said: "I am happy."
2. He said: "I like this phone."
3. They said: "We are busy."
4. She said: "I can call you later."
5. He said: "I want to leave a message."
6. She said: "I have a new phone."
7. He said: "I need to contact my boss."
8. They said: "We are in an emergency."
9. She said: "I work at a hospital."
10. He said: "I understand the problem."

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Chuyển ngược lại — Direct Speech

Viết lại lời nói trực tiếp:

1. He said that he was tired.
2. She said that she liked her new job.
3. They said that they were at home.
4. He said that he could help me.
5. She said that she wanted to call back.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. She said that she is busy. (thì chưa lùi)
2. He said that I liked coffee. (đại từ chưa đổi)
3. They said that they was happy. (chia sai)
4. She said that she cans help. (modal sai)
5. He said that he have a car. (thì chưa lùi)

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Talking on the phone](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2023-0406)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Yesterday, I **called** my friend. She didn't **pick up**, so I left a **voicemail**. Later, she **texted** me. She **said that** she **was** busy at work. She **said that** she **could** **call** me back in the evening.
>
> When she **called**, we had a nice **conversation**. She **said that** she **wanted** to meet this weekend. I **said that** I **was** free on Saturday. We agreed to meet at a café.

*(70 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. Why didn't the friend pick up the phone?
2. What did she say she could do?
3. When will they meet?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | She said that she was busy at work. |
| 2 | She said that she could call back in the evening. |
| 3 | They will meet on Saturday at a café. |
