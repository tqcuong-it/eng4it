## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Passive Voice (Present Simple — Luyện tập)

### 📖 Ôn lại cấu trúc

```
Chủ ngữ + is/are + V3 (past participle) + (by + người/vật)
```

### 🔑 Passive Voice dùng khi nào?

| Trường hợp | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------|
| **Không biết ai làm** | The data **is stored** in the cloud. (Ai lưu? Không quan trọng.) |
| **Nhấn mạnh vật/kết quả** | English **is spoken** worldwide. (Quan trọng là English, không phải ai nói.) |
| **Sự thật chung** | The internet **is used** by billions of people. |
| **Văn phong trang trọng** | Your application **is being reviewed**. |

### Thêm V3 cần nhớ

| V1 | V2 | V3 | Nghĩa |
|----|----|----|-------|
| use | used | **used** | sử dụng |
| store | stored | **stored** | lưu trữ |
| install | installed | **installed** | cài đặt |
| connect | connected | **connected** | kết nối |
| delete | deleted | **deleted** | xoá |
| protect | protected | **protected** | bảo vệ |
| send | sent | **sent** | gửi |
| build | built | **built** | xây dựng |
| find | found | **found** | tìm thấy |
| know | knew | **known** | biết |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chuyển sang Passive Voice

1. Billions of people use the internet every day.
2. They store data in the cloud.
3. People speak English in over 50 countries.
4. Companies develop new software every year.
5. They install the app on millions of devices.
6. Hackers steal personal data online.
7. Engineers build websites for businesses.
8. People send billions of emails every day.
9. They protect the network with strong security.
10. Students use technology in the classroom.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Active hay Passive?

Xác định câu nào là Active (A), câu nào là Passive (P):

1. The website is visited by millions of people. ( )
2. I search for information on Google. ( )
3. The software is updated every month. ( )
4. She deleted all the old files. ( )
5. English is spoken worldwide. ( )
6. The virus was found by the security team. ( )
7. They connect to the wireless network. ( )
8. The data is stored securely. ( )

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Điền is hoặc are

1. The internet ______ used by billions of people.
2. Emails ______ sent every second.
3. The software ______ installed on this computer.
4. New devices ______ connected to the network.
5. Data ______ stored in the cloud.
6. The website ______ visited by many users.
7. These files ______ deleted every week.
8. Technology ______ used in every industry.

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Technology and children](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2024-0321)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> **Technology** has changed our lives in many ways. The **internet** **is used** by billions of people every day. **Emails** **are sent** in seconds, and information **is found** with just one **click**. **Software** **is installed** on our **devices** to help us work, study, and communicate.
>
> However, **security** is a big concern. Personal **data** can be stolen if it **is** not **protected** properly. Strong passwords should be used, and **software** should be **updated** regularly. In our **digital** world, everyone needs to understand basic internet **security**.

*(80 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. How many people use the internet?
2. What can happen if data is not protected?
3. What should people do to stay safe online?
