## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Passive Voice (Câu bị động — Giới thiệu)

### 📖 Passive Voice là gì?

**Active Voice** (Chủ động): Nhấn mạnh **người làm**.
**Passive Voice** (Bị động): Nhấn mạnh **vật/người bị tác động**.

| Active (Chủ động) | Passive (Bị động) |
|--------------------|--------------------|
| The journalist **writes** the article. | The article **is written** by the journalist. |
| They **broadcast** the news at 7 PM. | The news **is broadcast** at 7 PM. |
| The editor **publishes** the report. | The report **is published** by the editor. |

### 🔑 Cấu trúc Passive Voice (Present Simple)

```
Chủ ngữ + is/are + V3 (past participle) + (by + người làm)
```

| Chủ ngữ | is/are | V3 | Ví dụ |
|---------|--------|-----|-------|
| Số ít (it, the news) | **is** | V3 | The news **is broadcast** every day. |
| Số nhiều (they, articles) | **are** | V3 | Articles **are published** online. |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:** Passive = **is/are + V3** (quá khứ phân từ)

### Một số V3 thường gặp

| V1 | V2 | V3 | Nghĩa |
|----|----|----|-------|
| write | wrote | **written** | viết |
| read | read | **read** | đọc |
| speak | spoke | **spoken** | nói |
| make | made | **made** | làm |
| give | gave | **given** | cho |
| see | saw | **seen** | thấy |
| broadcast | broadcast | **broadcast** | phát sóng |
| publish | published | **published** | xuất bản |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chuyển sang Passive Voice

1. The journalist writes the article.
2. They broadcast the news at 8 AM.
3. The editor publishes the report every Monday.
4. Reporters cover important events.
5. People read the newspaper every morning.
6. The channel shows the interview at 9 PM.
7. The press informs the public.
8. They check the facts carefully.
9. The reporter interviews the president.
10. Many people watch the news.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Chuyển sang Active Voice

1. The article is written by the journalist.
2. The news is broadcast every evening.
3. English is spoken in many countries.
4. The report is published online.
5. The event is covered by many reporters.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. The news are broadcast every day. (news = không đếm được)
2. The article is write by a journalist.
3. The reports is published every week.
4. English are spoken worldwide.
5. The interview is showed on TV.

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Fake news](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2024-0725)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Every day, millions of **articles** **are published** online. **News** **is broadcast** on TV, radio, and social media. **Journalists** write **reports** that **are read** by millions of people around the world.
>
> However, not all news can be trusted. **Fake** news **is spread** quickly on social **media**. It is important to check the **source** and verify the **facts**. Good **reporters** always make sure their information **is checked** before it **is published**.

*(75 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. Where is news broadcast?
2. What is spread quickly on social media?
3. What do good reporters do before publishing?
