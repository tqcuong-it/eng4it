## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Relative Clauses với WHICH / THAT

### 📖 WHICH và THAT dùng khi nào?

Hôm qua chúng ta học **WHO** dùng cho **người**. Hôm nay:
- **WHICH** → dùng cho **vật / sự việc**
- **THAT** → dùng cho cả **người và vật** (thay thế who/which)

### 🔑 Quy tắc

| Hai câu riêng | Kết hợp với WHICH/THAT |
|----------------|------------------------|
| The phone is expensive. I bought it. | The phone **which/that I bought** is expensive. |
| I read a book. It was very interesting. | I read a book **which/that was** very interesting. |
| The app is free. It has many features. | The app **which/that has** many features is free. |

> 🧠 **Mẹo nhớ:**
> - **WHO** = người 👤
> - **WHICH** = vật/sự việc 📦
> - **THAT** = cả hai (thay thế được cả who và which) ✨

### Cấu trúc

```
Danh từ (vật) + WHICH/THAT + chủ ngữ + động từ + ...
Danh từ (vật) + WHICH/THAT + động từ + ...
```

Ví dụ:
- The **video that went viral** has 10 million views. (Video **mà lan truyền** có 10 triệu lượt xem.)
- The **content which she creates** is very interesting. (Nội dung **mà cô ấy tạo** rất thú vị.)
- I deleted the **post that got** negative comments. (Tôi xoá bài đăng **mà nhận** bình luận tiêu cực.)

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Nối câu bằng WHICH hoặc THAT

Nối hai câu thành một:

1. The phone is new. I bought it yesterday.
2. The video is funny. She shared it on Facebook.
3. I use an app. It helps me learn English.
4. The comment was rude. He posted it.
5. The website is very useful. It provides free courses.
6. I like the content. She uploads it every week.
7. The notification was important. I received it this morning.
8. The password is too short. You created it.
9. I watched a stream. It was about cooking.
10. The account was blocked. It posted fake news.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Chọn WHO, WHICH hoặc THAT

Điền từ phù hợp:

1. The girl ______ posted this photo is my friend.
2. The app ______ I downloaded is very useful.
3. People ______ share fake news should be careful.
4. The video ______ went viral was very funny.
5. The teacher ______ explains grammar clearly is popular.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. The phone who I bought is expensive.
2. The book which it is on the table is mine.
3. I like videos which is interesting.
4. The person which called me was my boss.
5. The app that it helps me learn is free.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | The phone **which/that I bought** yesterday is new. |
| 2 | The video **which/that she shared** on Facebook is funny. |
| 3 | I use an app **which/that helps** me learn English. |
| 4 | The comment **which/that he posted** was rude. |
| 5 | The website **which/that provides** free courses is very useful. |
| 6 | I like the content **which/that she uploads** every week. |
| 7 | The notification **which/that I received** this morning was important. |
| 8 | The password **which/that you created** is too short. |
| 9 | I watched a stream **which/that was** about cooking. |
| 10 | The account **which/that posted** fake news was blocked. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | who/that | Girl = người → dùng who hoặc that |
| 2 | which/that | App = vật → dùng which hoặc that |
| 3 | who/that | People = người → dùng who hoặc that |
| 4 | which/that | Video = vật → dùng which hoặc that |
| 5 | who/that | Teacher = người → dùng who hoặc that |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | who | The phone **which/that** I bought... | Phone = vật → dùng which/that |
| 2 | which it | The book **which** is on the table... | Bỏ "it" — which đã thay thế |
| 3 | is | videos which **are** interesting | Videos số nhiều → are |
| 4 | which | The person **who/that** called me... | Person = người → dùng who/that |
| 5 | that it | The app **that** helps me... | Bỏ "it" — that đã thay thế |

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Social media is something **that** most people use every day. Apps **which** are popular include Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. People **who** create good **content** can get thousands of **followers**.
>
> However, social media also has problems. Videos **that go viral** sometimes contain fake news. **Notifications** **which** come all day can be distracting. It is important to **block** accounts **that** spread harmful **content** and use social media wisely.

*(75 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. What apps are popular for social media?
2. What can happen if you create good content?
3. What should you do with accounts that spread harmful content?
