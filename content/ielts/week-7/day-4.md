## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Reported Speech với TOLD

### 📖 SAID vs TOLD — khác nhau gì?

| Cách dùng | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| **said** (không cần nói với ai) | He **said** that he was busy. |
| **told** + **somebody** (nói với ai đó) | He **told me** that he was busy. |

> ⚠️ **Lưu ý quan trọng:**
> - ✅ He **said** that... (KHÔNG có "me")
> - ✅ He **told me** that... (PHẢI có người nghe)
> - ❌ ~~He said me that...~~
> - ❌ ~~He told that...~~

### 🔑 Thêm ví dụ

| Direct Speech | Reported Speech (said) | Reported Speech (told) |
|---------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| "I will reply later." | She **said** she would reply later. | She **told me** she would reply later. |
| "We received your email." | They **said** they had received my email. | They **told me** they had received my email. |
| "I can't find the attachment." | He **said** he couldn't find the attachment. | He **told her** he couldn't find the attachment. |

### Lùi thì — bổ sung thêm

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| will | → **would** |
| have / has | → **had** |
| am/is doing | → **was/were doing** |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chuyển sang Reported Speech (dùng TOLD)

1. She said to me: "I will send the email tomorrow."
2. He said to her: "I received your letter."
3. They said to us: "We can't find the attachment."
4. She said to him: "I have written a draft."
5. He said to me: "I will forward this to the manager."
6. She said to them: "I need your email address."
7. He said to her: "I am writing a formal letter."
8. They said to us: "We will reply soon."
9. She said to me: "I checked the spam folder."
10. He said to her: "I can attach the file."

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: SAID hay TOLD?

Chọn **said** hoặc **told**:

1. He ______ that he was busy.
2. She ______ me that the email was important.
3. They ______ that they would reply tomorrow.
4. He ______ her that he had sent the letter.
5. She ______ that the delivery was late.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. He said me that he was tired.
2. She told that she would come.
3. He told me that he will reply soon.
4. She said him that she can't help.
5. They told us that they are busy.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | She **told me** that she **would** send the email the next day. |
| 2 | He **told her** that he **had received** her letter. |
| 3 | They **told us** that they **couldn't** find the attachment. |
| 4 | She **told him** that she **had written** a draft. |
| 5 | He **told me** that he **would** forward that to the manager. |
| 6 | She **told them** that she **needed** their email address. |
| 7 | He **told her** that he **was writing** a formal letter. |
| 8 | They **told us** that they **would** reply soon. |
| 9 | She **told me** that she **had checked** the spam folder. |
| 10 | He **told her** that he **could** attach the file. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | said | Không có người nghe → dùng said |
| 2 | told | Có "me" → dùng told |
| 3 | said | Không có người nghe → dùng said |
| 4 | told | Có "her" → dùng told |
| 5 | said | Không có người nghe → dùng said |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | said me | He **told me** that... | said không đi với người, dùng told |
| 2 | told that | She **said** that... hoặc She **told me** that... | told phải có người nghe |
| 3 | will | He told me that he **would** reply soon. | Lùi thì: will → would |
| 4 | said him | She **told him** that she **couldn't** help. | said không đi với người + can't → couldn't |
| 5 | are | They told us that they **were** busy. | Lùi thì: are → were |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Writing a formal email](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/2023-0907)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Last week, my manager **told me** that I needed to write a **formal** **email** to a client. She **told me** to use a clear **subject** line and **attach** the report. I wrote a **draft** first, then she checked it.
>
> She **said** that the email was good but I should add my **signature**. She also **told me** to **CC** the team leader. After I **sent** the email, the client **replied** the same day. He **said** that he **had received** the **attachment** and everything was fine.

*(75 từ — có sử dụng Reported Speech)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi

1. What did the manager tell the writer to do?
2. What did the manager say about the email?
3. What did the client say?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | She told him to write a formal email and attach the report. |
| 2 | She said the email was good but he should add his signature. |
| 3 | He said that he had received the attachment and everything was fine. |
