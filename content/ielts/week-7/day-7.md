## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Tổng ôn Grammar + Mini Test

### 📖 Ôn tập 3 điểm ngữ pháp

#### 1️⃣ Relative Clauses (Mệnh đề quan hệ)

| Từ | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|----|----------|-------|
| **who** | Người 👤 | The man **who works** here is kind. |
| **which** | Vật 📦 | The phone **which I bought** is new. |
| **that** | Cả hai ✨ | The app **that helps** me learn is free. |

#### 2️⃣ Reported Speech (Câu tường thuật)

| Quy tắc | Ví dụ |
|----------|-------|
| **said** (không có người nghe) | He **said** that he was busy. |
| **told** + somebody | He **told me** that he was busy. |
| Lùi thì: am/is → was, are → were | "I **am** happy." → She said she **was** happy. |
| will → would, can → could | "I **will** call." → She said she **would** call. |

#### 3️⃣ Passive Voice (Câu bị động — Present)

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| **is + V3** (số ít) | English **is spoken** worldwide. |
| **are + V3** (số nhiều) | Emails **are sent** every day. |

### 📝 MINI TEST — Tuần 7

**Phần 1: Từ vựng (10 câu — 2 điểm/câu)**

Chọn đáp án đúng:

**1.** I will ______ you later tonight.
- a) hang  b) call  c) dial  d) ring

**2.** She ______ an interesting article about technology.
- a) broadcast  b) published  c) installed  d) deleted

**3.** Don't share your ______ with anyone.
- a) profile  b) hashtag  c) password  d) content

**4.** The reporter ______ the news from the scene.
- a) forwarded  b) subscribed  c) broadcast  d) texted

**5.** Please ______ this email to the team.
- a) attach  b) forward  c) delete  d) spam

**6.** I need to ______ to the Wi-Fi network.
- a) browse  b) click  c) search  d) connect

**7.** She always ______ about the food at the restaurant.
- a) agrees  b) complains  c) suggests  d) persuades

**8.** The video ______ and got 5 million views in one day.
- a) went viral  b) went digital  c) went online  d) went wireless

**9.** Leave a ______ after the beep.
- a) signal  b) message  c) voicemail  d) connection

**10.** The ______ reviewed the article before publishing.
- a) reporter  b) audience  c) editor  d) caller

**Phần 2: Ngữ pháp (10 câu — 2 điểm/câu)**

**11.** The teacher ______ explains well is very popular.
- a) which  b) who  c) what  d) whom

**12.** The app ______ I downloaded is very useful.
- a) who  b) whose  c) which  d) whom

**13.** She said that she ______ busy.
- a) is  b) was  c) were  d) be

**14.** He told me that he ______ help me.
- a) can  b) will  c) could  d) is

**15.** He ______ that he was tired.
- a) told  b) said  c) told me  d) says

**16.** English ______ in many countries.
- a) speaks  b) is spoken  c) are spoken  d) spoken

**17.** Emails ______ every day around the world.
- a) is sent  b) sends  c) are sent  d) sent

**18.** The person ______ called you is my friend.
- a) which  b) what  c) whose  d) who

**19.** She told ______ that the meeting was cancelled.
- a) that  b) me  c) said  d) to

**20.** The news ______ at 7 PM every evening.
- a) are broadcast  b) is broadcast  c) broadcasts  d) broadcast

---

**Phần 3: Reading (5 câu — 2 điểm/câu)**

Đọc đoạn văn sau và trả lời câu hỏi:

> In today's world, **technology** **is used** in almost every part of our lives. **Emails** **are sent** instead of traditional **letters**. People **who** want to stay in touch use social **media** apps **which** are available on every **device**.
>
> A recent **report** **said that** over 4 billion people **are connected** to the **internet**. **Journalists** write **articles** **that** **are read** by millions. However, **fake** news **is spread** easily, so it is important to check the **source** of information.
>
> My teacher **told me** that good **communication** skills **are needed** in every job. She **said that** people **who** can **express** their ideas clearly will always succeed.

*(90 từ)*

**21.** What is used instead of traditional letters?
- a) Phone calls  b) Emails  c) Newspapers  d) Books

**22.** How many people are connected to the internet?
- a) 1 billion  b) 2 billion  c) 3 billion  d) Over 4 billion

**23.** What is spread easily according to the text?
- a) Technology  b) Social media  c) Fake news  d) Emails

**24.** What did the teacher say about communication skills?
- a) They are not important  b) They are needed in every job  c) They are easy to learn  d) They are only for journalists

**25.** People who can ______ their ideas clearly will succeed.
- a) translate  b) delete  c) express  d) install
