## ✏️ Bài viết 1: Table + Line Graph (Mixed)

### Đề bài

*"The table and line graph below show the number of visitors (in millions) to three national parks from 2015 to 2023."*

**Table — Số liệu chi tiết:**

| Year | Yellowstone | Grand Canyon | Yosemite |
|------|------------|--------------|----------|
| 2015 | 4.1 | 5.5 | 4.0 |
| 2017 | 4.1 | 6.3 | 5.0 |
| 2019 | 4.0 | 6.4 | 4.4 |
| 2021 | 2.1 | 3.8 | 3.3 |
| 2023 | 4.5 | 6.7 | 3.9 |

**⏰ BẤM GIỜ 20 PHÚT — BẮT ĐẦU VIẾT!**

✍️ Viết bài vào vở hoặc file riêng. KHÔNG xem bài mẫu dưới đây cho đến khi viết xong!

---

### Bài mẫu 1 — Band 6+

> *The table presents data on the number of visitors, measured in millions, to three major national parks in the United States over a nine-year period from 2015 to 2023.*
>
> *Overall, Grand Canyon consistently attracted the most visitors throughout the period, while all three parks experienced a significant drop in 2021 before recovering. By 2023, Grand Canyon and Yellowstone had exceeded their 2015 figures, whereas Yosemite had not fully recovered.*
>
> *Grand Canyon was the most popular park, with visitor numbers rising steadily from 5.5 million in 2015 to 6.4 million in 2019. Despite falling sharply to 3.8 million in 2021, the figure rebounded to 6.7 million in 2023, the highest recorded. Yellowstone maintained relatively stable numbers of around 4 million between 2015 and 2019 before dropping to 2.1 million in 2021, then recovering to 4.5 million by 2023.*
>
> *Yosemite initially grew from 4.0 million in 2015 to 5.0 million in 2017. However, after declining to 3.3 million in 2021, it only partially recovered to 3.9 million in 2023, remaining slightly below its 2015 level. This made Yosemite the only park that had not returned to its pre-2021 visitor numbers.*

**Số từ: 187 từ ✅**

## ✏️ Bài viết 2: Process Diagram

### Đề bài

*"The diagram below shows the process of recycling plastic bottles."*

**Các bước:**
1. Consumers place used plastic bottles in recycling bins
2. Bottles are collected by recycling trucks
3. Bottles are taken to a recycling facility
4. Bottles are sorted by type and colour
5. Sorted bottles are cleaned and labels are removed
6. Clean bottles are shredded into small flakes
7. Flakes are melted at high temperature
8. Melted plastic is formed into pellets
9. Pellets are sold to manufacturers
10. Manufacturers use pellets to produce new plastic products

**⏰ BẤM GIỜ 20 PHÚT — BẮT ĐẦU VIẾT!**

✍️ Viết bài! KHÔNG xem bài mẫu trước!

---

### Bài mẫu 2 — Band 6+

> *The diagram illustrates the step-by-step process of recycling plastic bottles, from collection to the production of new plastic products.*
>
> *Overall, there are ten stages in this linear process, beginning with consumers depositing bottles in recycling bins and ending with the manufacture of new plastic items. The process involves both manual and industrial operations.*
>
> *Initially, used plastic bottles are placed in recycling bins by consumers. These bottles are then collected by specialised recycling trucks and transported to a recycling facility. At the facility, the bottles are sorted according to their type and colour before being thoroughly cleaned. During the cleaning stage, any labels are also removed.*
>
> *Following this, the clean bottles are shredded into small flakes using industrial machinery. These flakes are subsequently melted at high temperatures and formed into small pellets. In the final stages of the process, the pellets are sold to various manufacturers, who use them as raw material to produce new plastic products. This demonstrates how used bottles can be transformed into new items through recycling.*

**Số từ: 172 từ ✅**

## 📊 Bảng tự chấm điểm chi tiết

Chấm mỗi bài theo bảng sau (1-9):

### Bài 1 (Table)

| Tiêu chí | Band 5 | Band 6 | Điểm của bạn |
|----------|--------|--------|--------------|
| **Task Achievement** | Mô tả dữ liệu nhưng thiếu/yếu overview | Overview rõ ràng, key features đúng | /9 |
| **Coherence & Cohesion** | Chia đoạn cơ bản, linking words lặp | Chia đoạn logic, linking words đa dạng | /9 |
| **Lexical Resource** | Từ vựng hạn chế, nhiều lặp | Từ vựng đủ đa dạng, paraphrase tốt | /9 |
| **Grammar** | Câu đơn giản, lỗi nhưng hiểu được | Mix câu, lỗi ít, không gây khó hiểu | /9 |

### Bài 2 (Process)

| Tiêu chí | Câu hỏi kiểm tra | Điểm của bạn |
|----------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Task Achievement** | Có đủ các bước? Có overview? | /9 |
| **Coherence & Cohesion** | Linking words trình tự đa dạng? | /9 |
| **Lexical Resource** | Có dùng bị động? Từ vựng process? | /9 |

## 💡 Chiến thuật thi thật

### Khi BÍ không biết viết gì

1. **Quay lại dữ liệu** — Nhìn lại biểu đồ, tìm con số bạn chưa dùng
2. **So sánh** — Luôn có thể thêm 1 câu so sánh
3. **"Interestingly..."** — Nêu 1 điểm đáng chú ý
4. **Kết bài body** — Tóm lại ý cuối cùng

### Checklist trước khi nộp bài (2 phút cuối)

- [ ] Đủ 150 từ?
- [ ] Có overview?
- [ ] Đúng thì động từ?
- [ ] Không có ý kiến cá nhân?
- [ ] Spelling cơ bản đúng?
- [ ] Chia 4 đoạn rõ ràng?
