## 📋 Chiến lược: Tại sao so sánh quan trọng?

Trong Task 1, bạn PHẢI **so sánh và đối chiếu** dữ liệu — đây là yêu cầu cốt lõi của đề bài. Giám khảo đánh giá cao bài viết có:

- ✅ So sánh **tương đồng** (similarities)
- ✅ Đối chiếu **khác biệt** (differences)
- ✅ Nêu **mối quan hệ** giữa các dữ liệu

---

## 📝 Cụm từ so sánh TƯƠNG ĐỒNG

### Cùng xu hướng / Giống nhau

| # | Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|---|--------|-------|
| 1 | **Similarly** | The UK rose to 500. **Similarly**, Australia reached 450. |
| 2 | **Likewise** | Japan saw growth. **Likewise**, Korea experienced an increase. |
| 3 | **In the same way** | **In the same way**, both countries saw declines. |
| 4 | **A similar trend/pattern** | **A similar trend** was observed in France. |
| 5 | **followed a similar trajectory** | Both countries **followed a similar trajectory**. |
| 6 | **mirrored** | Australia's figures **mirrored** those of the UK. |
| 7 | **both... and...** | **Both** the UK **and** Australia experienced growth. |
| 8 | **as did** | The UK increased, **as did** Australia. |

### Gần bằng nhau

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| **almost/nearly identical** | The figures were **almost identical**. |
| **roughly/approximately equal** | Both countries had **roughly equal** numbers. |
| **comparable to** | Japan's figure was **comparable to** Korea's. |
| **on a par with** | The UK was **on a par with** France. |
| **stood at similar levels** | Both countries **stood at similar levels** in 2020. |

---

## 📝 Cụm từ so sánh KHÁC BIỆT

### Đối lập / Trái ngược

| # | Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|---|--------|-------|
| 1 | **In contrast** | The UK grew. **In contrast**, Japan declined. |
| 2 | **Conversely** | **Conversely**, the figure for Japan fell. |
| 3 | **On the other hand** | **On the other hand**, developing countries saw lower rates. |
| 4 | **However** | Sales rose in Q1. **However**, they dropped in Q2. |
| 5 | **While / Whereas** | **While** the UK grew, Japan declined. |
| 6 | **Unlike** | **Unlike** the UK, Japan saw a decline. |
| 7 | **By contrast** | **By contrast**, Africa had the lowest figure. |
| 8 | **differed significantly** | The two countries **differed significantly**. |

### Nêu chênh lệch

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| **X times as much/many as** | The UK had **three times as many** students as Japan. |
| **considerably/significantly more than** | The USA spent **considerably more than** Brazil. |
| **marginally/slightly higher/lower** | France was **marginally higher** than Spain. |
| **the gap between... widened/narrowed** | **The gap between** rich and poor **widened**. |
| **outperformed / outpaced** | China **outperformed** India in exports. |

---

## 📝 Cấu trúc câu so sánh nâng cao

### Cấu trúc 1: While/Whereas + 2 mệnh đề

```
While A increased to X, B decreased to Y.
Whereas the UK saw growth, Japan experienced a decline.
```

### Cấu trúc 2: Compared to/with

```
Compared to Japan (60), the UK had a much higher figure (500).
Sales in 2020, compared with 2019, showed significant growth.
```

### Cấu trúc 3: In comparison to

```
In comparison to developing countries, developed nations had higher rates.
```

### Cấu trúc 4: ...which was X times/percentage points higher

```
The UK stood at 500, which was nearly ten times higher than Japan's figure of 60.
```

### Cấu trúc 5: A, followed by B and C

```
The USA had the highest spending at $500, followed by the UK ($400) and Japan ($300).
```

## 🎧 Bài tập thực hành

### Bài tập 1: Chọn từ nối phù hợp

Điền từ nối so sánh vào chỗ trống:

1. The USA had the highest figure at 800,000. __________, Canada had just 200,000. (đối lập)
2. Both the UK and Australia experienced rapid growth. __________, Japan remained stagnant. (trái ngược)
3. France attracted 100 million tourists. __________, Spain saw 85 million visitors. (tương đồng)
4. __________ the USA invested heavily in education, developing countries spent very little. (trong khi)
5. The gap between urban and rural areas __________ over the decade. (rộng ra)

### Bài tập 2: Viết câu so sánh

Dùng dữ liệu sau để viết câu so sánh:

| Country | 2015 | 2023 |
|---------|------|------|
| China | 200 | 800 |
| India | 150 | 400 |
| Brazil | 100 | 120 |

1. So sánh China và India năm 2023
2. So sánh Brazil 2015 vs 2023
3. So sánh mức tăng trưởng của 3 nước

### Bài tập 3: Viết đoạn Body

Dùng dữ liệu ở Bài tập 2, viết 1 đoạn Body paragraph (4-5 câu) sử dụng ít nhất 3 cụm từ so sánh khác nhau.

## ✅ Đáp án & Giải thích

### Bài tập 1

1. **In contrast / By contrast**
2. **However / Conversely**
3. **Similarly / Likewise**
4. **While / Whereas**
5. **widened**

### Bài tập 2 — Gợi ý

1. *"China's figure in 2023 stood at 800, which was **twice as high as** India's figure of 400."*
2. *"Brazil's figure increased only **marginally**, from 100 in 2015 to 120 in 2023."*
3. *"**While** China experienced the most dramatic growth, quadrupling from 200 to 800, Brazil's figure showed **minimal change**. India, by contrast, saw **moderate** growth, rising from 150 to 400."*

### Bài tập 3 — Gợi ý

*"China led all three countries, surging from 200 in 2015 to 800 in 2023, a fourfold increase. India followed a similar upward trend, with its figure rising considerably from 150 to 400 over the same period. However, India's growth was less dramatic compared to China's. In contrast, Brazil experienced only marginal growth, increasing by just 20 to reach 120 in 2023, which was significantly lower than both China and India."*

## 💡 Tips cho Comparing & Contrasting

1. **Luôn so sánh** — ĐỪNG chỉ liệt kê số liệu riêng lẻ
2. **Mix cụm từ** — Dùng 3-4 cụm từ so sánh khác nhau trong 1 bài
3. **While/Whereas** — Cấu trúc mạnh nhất, dùng 1-2 lần mỗi bài
4. **Đừng so sánh mọi thứ** — Chọn so sánh nổi bật nhất
5. **"Respectively"** — Tiết kiệm từ: *"A and B stood at 100 and 200 respectively"*
