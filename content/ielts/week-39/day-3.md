## ✍️ Last-minute Writing Tips + Templates

### ⭐ Top 10 Tips

#### 1. Quản lý thời gian NGHIÊM NGẶT
- Task 1: **20 phút** (KHÔNG hơn!)
- Task 2: **40 phút** (Task 2 chiếm 2/3 điểm Writing)
- Dành 3-5 phút cuối để kiểm tra lỗi

#### 2. Task 2 quan trọng hơn Task 1
- Task 2 = 2/3 điểm Writing
- Nếu hết giờ, viết Task 2 đầy đủ hơn Task 1

#### 3. LUÔN viết Overview (Task 1)
- Không có overview = KHÔNG quá Band 5
- Overview = 2-3 câu tóm tắt xu hướng CHÍNH

#### 4. LUÔN trả lời ĐÚNG đề (Task 2)
- Đọc đề 2 lần trước khi viết
- "Discuss both views" ≠ "Give your opinion only"
- Off-topic = mất điểm nặng

#### 5. Đếm từ
- Task 1: **ít nhất 150** (viết ~170-180)
- Task 2: **ít nhất 250** (viết ~270-290)
- Thiếu từ = trừ điểm

## 📝 Template — Task 1 (Biểu đồ)

### Introduction (1-2 câu)
> *The [chart type] illustrates/compares [what] in [where/when].*

### Overview (2-3 câu)
> *Overall, it is clear that [main trend 1]. Additionally, [main trend 2].*

### Body 1 (3-4 câu)
> *Looking at the data in more detail, [specific details + numbers]...*
> *[Comparison between groups]...*

### Body 2 (3-4 câu)
> *In contrast / Similarly, [more specific details]...*
> *[Another comparison with numbers]...*

### Từ vựng mô tả xu hướng

| Tăng | Giảm | Không đổi |
|------|------|-----------|
| increased | decreased | remained stable |
| rose | fell | stayed constant |
| grew | declined | leveled off |
| surged | plummeted | plateaued |
| climbed | dropped | fluctuated slightly |

| Mức độ | Từ |
|--------|-----|
| Mạnh | dramatically, significantly, sharply |
| Vừa | moderately, steadily, gradually |
| Nhẹ | slightly, marginally, minimally |

## 📝 Template — Task 2 (Essay)

### Dạng 1: Discuss Both Views + Opinion

**Introduction:**
> *It is often argued that [view 1]. However, others believe that [view 2]. This essay will discuss both perspectives before presenting my own opinion.*

**Body 1 (View 1):**
> *On the one hand, [supporters argue that]... For example, [specific example]... This suggests that [conclusion].*

**Body 2 (View 2):**
> *On the other hand, [opponents contend that]... For instance, [specific example]... As a result, [conclusion].*

**Body 3 (Your opinion):**
> *In my opinion, [your view]... This is because [reason]... Furthermore, [additional support].*

**Conclusion:**
> *In conclusion, while both views have merit, I believe that [restate opinion]. [Final thought].*

### Dạng 2: Advantages/Disadvantages

**Introduction:**
> *[Topic] has become increasingly [common/debated]. This essay will examine both the advantages and disadvantages of [topic].*

**Body 1 — Advantages:**
> *There are several benefits of... Firstly,... Secondly,... For example,...*

**Body 2 — Disadvantages:**
> *However, there are also drawbacks. The main concern is... Additionally,...*

**Conclusion:**
> *In conclusion, [topic] offers both benefits and drawbacks. Overall, I believe [your stance].*

### Dạng 3: Problem/Solution

**Introduction:**
> *[Problem] has become a growing concern in many countries. This essay will discuss the main problems caused by [topic] and suggest possible solutions.*

**Body 1 — Problems:**
> *The most significant problem is... Another issue is... This leads to...*

**Body 2 — Solutions:**
> *Several measures can be taken to address these issues. Firstly,... Secondly,...*

**Conclusion:**
> *In conclusion, while [problem] presents serious challenges, implementing [solutions] could significantly improve the situation.*

## ⚠️ Top 10 lỗi Writing phổ biến

| # | Lỗi | Ví dụ sai → đúng |
|---|------|------------------|
| 1 | Subject-verb agreement | ~~The number of students have~~ → has |
| 2 | Article mistakes | ~~In the education~~ → In education |
| 3 | Run-on sentences | Dùng dấu chấm thay dấu phẩy |
| 4 | "In my opinion, I think" | Chọn 1 — đừng dùng cả 2 |
| 5 | "The amount of people" | → The number of people |
| 6 | No overview (Task 1) | LUÔN viết overview! |
| 7 | Off-topic (Task 2) | Đọc đề 2 lần! |
| 8 | Quá nhiều "very" | → extremely, highly, considerably |
| 9 | Không có linking words | → Furthermore, However, In addition |
| 10 | Copy nguyên đề bài | PARAPHRASE đề! |
