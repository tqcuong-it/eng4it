## 🎧 Full Mock Test — Listening

Hôm nay bạn sẽ làm bài thi **Listening mô phỏng** hoàn chỉnh. Hãy tạo môi trường thi thật!

### Chuẩn bị:
- 🎧 Tai nghe chất lượng tốt
- ⏱️ Bấm giờ 30 phút + 10 phút chép đáp án
- 📝 Bút chì + giấy
- 🤫 Phòng yên tĩnh
- 🚫 KHÔNG dùng từ điển, KHÔNG dừng audio

---

## Section 1: Hội thoại đời thường (10 câu)

**Tình huống:** Một sinh viên gọi điện đặt phòng ký túc xá.

**Questions 1-5: Form Completion**

Complete the form below. Write NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS AND/OR A NUMBER.

| Student Accommodation Form | |
|---|---|
| Name: | Sarah __________ (1) |
| Student ID: | __________ (2) |
| Phone: | 0412 __________ (3) |
| Preferred room type: | __________ (4) |
| Move-in date: | __________ September (5) |

**Questions 6-10: Multiple Choice**

6. The monthly rent includes:
   - A) electricity only
   - B) electricity and water
   - C) electricity, water and internet

7. The kitchen is:
   - A) private for each room
   - B) shared between 2 rooms
   - C) shared on each floor

8. Laundry facilities are located:
   - A) on each floor
   - B) in the basement
   - C) next to the reception

9. The nearest supermarket is:
   - A) 5 minutes walk
   - B) 10 minutes walk
   - C) 15 minutes walk

10. Parking is available for:
    - A) all residents
    - B) residents who pay extra
    - C) residents with a permit only

## Section 2: Độc thoại xã hội (10 câu)

**Tình huống:** Hướng dẫn viên giới thiệu về chương trình tình nguyện môi trường.

**Questions 11-15: Matching**

Match each activity to the correct day.

| Activity | Day |
|----------|-----|
| A. Beach cleanup | |
| B. Tree planting | |
| C. Water quality testing | |
| D. Wildlife survey | |
| E. Community workshop | |

11. Monday → __________
12. Tuesday → __________
13. Wednesday → __________
14. Thursday → __________
15. Friday → __________

**Questions 16-20: Sentence Completion**

Write NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS.

16. Volunteers should bring their own __________ and sunscreen.
17. The program has been running for __________ years.
18. Last year, volunteers collected over __________ tons of waste.
19. The most common type of pollution found is __________.
20. Certificates will be awarded at the __________.

## Section 3: Hội thoại học thuật (10 câu)

**Tình huống:** Hai sinh viên và giáo sư thảo luận về dự án nghiên cứu marketing.

**Questions 21-25: Multiple Choice**

21. The research project focuses on:
    - A) traditional advertising methods
    - B) social media marketing effectiveness
    - C) consumer behavior in stores

22. The sample size for the survey is:
    - A) 200 participants
    - B) 500 participants
    - C) 1,000 participants

23. The main challenge mentioned is:
    - A) getting participants
    - B) analyzing the data
    - C) designing the questionnaire

24. The professor suggests they:
    - A) reduce the sample size
    - B) use both quantitative and qualitative methods
    - C) focus only on one platform

25. The deadline for the first draft is:
    - A) end of March
    - B) mid-April
    - C) end of April

**Questions 26-30: Summary Completion**

Complete the summary. Write NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS.

The students plan to collect data through an online (26) __________ and follow-up (27) __________. They will target users aged (28) __________ to 35. The analysis will use (29) __________ software to identify patterns. Results will be presented in both written and (30) __________ formats.

---

## Section 4: Bài giảng học thuật (10 câu)

**Tình huống:** Bài giảng về tác động của biến đổi khí hậu lên nông nghiệp.

**Questions 31-35: Note Completion**

Write NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS AND/OR A NUMBER.

**Climate Change and Agriculture**
- Global temperatures have risen by (31) __________ °C since 1900
- Crop yields could decrease by (32) __________% by 2050
- Most affected regions: Sub-Saharan Africa and (33) __________
- Water scarcity affects (34) __________% of agricultural land
- New drought-resistant varieties take (35) __________ years to develop

**Questions 36-40: Multiple Choice**

36. The speaker's main argument is that:
    - A) agriculture must adapt to climate change
    - B) climate change will destroy agriculture
    - C) technology alone can solve the problem

37. Vertical farming is described as:
    - A) too expensive to implement
    - B) a promising but limited solution
    - C) the future of all agriculture

38. The example of the Netherlands shows:
    - A) small countries can be agricultural leaders
    - B) technology investment is wasteful
    - C) climate change has no effect there

39. The speaker recommends:
    - A) reducing meat consumption globally
    - B) a combination of traditional and modern approaches
    - C) focusing only on genetic modification

40. Future research priorities include:
    - A) soil health and crop diversity
    - B) pesticide development
    - C) expanding farmland
