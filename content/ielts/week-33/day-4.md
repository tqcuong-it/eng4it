## 🎤 Full Mock Test — Speaking

Ghi âm lại toàn bộ bài thi để nghe lại và tự đánh giá!

### Chuẩn bị:
- 📱 Ứng dụng ghi âm
- ⏱️ Đồng hồ bấm giờ
- 🪑 Ngồi thoải mái, tưởng tượng đang nói với giám khảo
- 💡 Nói to, rõ ràng, tự nhiên

---

## Part 1: Introduction & Interview (4-5 phút)

Trả lời mỗi câu khoảng **20-30 giây**.

### Topic 1: Your Home
1. Do you live in a house or an apartment?
2. What do you like most about your home?
3. Is there anything you would like to change about your home?
4. Do you plan to live there for a long time?

### Topic 2: Technology
5. How often do you use your phone?
6. What apps do you use the most?
7. Do you think people spend too much time on their phones?
8. What technology do you find most useful in your daily life?

### Topic 3: Weather
9. What's the weather like in your city today?
10. Do you prefer hot or cold weather?
11. Does the weather affect your mood?
12. What do you usually do on rainy days?

---

## Part 2: Long Turn (3-4 phút)

**1 phút chuẩn bị** + **2 phút nói**

### Cue Card:

> **Describe a skill that you learned as a child.**
>
> You should say:
> - what the skill was
> - who taught you this skill
> - how you learned it
>
> and explain why this skill is important to you.

### Ghi chú nhanh (1 phút):
- **What:** swimming / cooking / cycling / playing piano...
- **Who:** parent / teacher / grandparent...
- **How:** regular practice / watching and imitating / formal lessons...
- **Why important:** confidence / health / social / lifelong benefit...

---

## Part 3: Discussion (4-5 phút)

Trả lời mỗi câu khoảng **30-60 giây**. Giải thích chi tiết + ví dụ.

1. What skills do you think are important for children to learn today?
2. How has technology changed the way children learn new skills?
3. Do you think children learn better from their parents or from teachers?
4. Some people say that traditional skills like cooking and sewing are no longer important. Do you agree?
5. How do you think education will change in the next 20 years?
6. Is it better to be good at one skill or to have many different skills?

## 📝 Model Answers

{{< spoiler "Xem bài mẫu Part 1" >}}

**Do you live in a house or an apartment?**
"I currently live in a small apartment in the city center. It's quite cozy — just one bedroom, a kitchen, and a living area. I chose it mainly because it's close to my workplace, so I can walk to work in about 15 minutes, which I really enjoy."

**How often do you use your phone?**
"Honestly, probably too much! I'd say I use it several hours a day — mostly for messaging, checking emails, and browsing social media. I also use it for navigation and listening to podcasts during my commute. I've been trying to reduce my screen time recently, though."

{{< /spoiler >}}

{{< spoiler "Xem bài mẫu Part 2" >}}

"I'd like to talk about swimming, which I learned when I was about seven years old. My father taught me during our summer holidays by the sea.

At first, I was absolutely terrified of the water. I remember clinging to my father's arms and refusing to let go. But he was very patient — he started by teaching me to float, then gradually introduced basic strokes. We practiced every day for about two weeks.

The breakthrough moment came when I suddenly realized I was swimming on my own without any support. I felt an incredible sense of achievement — it was one of those moments you never forget as a child.

Swimming has been enormously important to me for several reasons. First, it gave me confidence — not just in the water, but in general. Overcoming that fear taught me that I could push through challenges if I was persistent. Second, it's been a lifelong source of exercise and relaxation. Even now, I try to swim at least once a week because it helps me de-stress after a long day at work.

I'm really grateful my father took the time to teach me, and it's definitely a skill I want to pass on to my own children someday."

{{< /spoiler >}}

{{< spoiler "Xem bài mẫu Part 3" >}}

**What skills do you think are important for children to learn today?**
"I think there's a combination of traditional and modern skills that children need. On the traditional side, skills like critical thinking, communication, and teamwork remain essential — they're the foundation for success in any field. But in today's digital age, I'd add digital literacy and coding as increasingly important. Children who understand technology at a fundamental level will have a significant advantage in the job market. I'd also emphasize emotional intelligence — the ability to understand and manage emotions — because as our world becomes more complex, the ability to collaborate and empathize with others becomes even more valuable."

{{< /spoiler >}}

## 📊 Tiêu chí chấm điểm Speaking

| Tiêu chí | Band 5 | Band 6 | Band 7 |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|
| Fluency | Nói chậm, hay dừng | Khá trôi chảy, đôi khi dừng | Trôi chảy, ít dừng |
| Vocabulary | Cơ bản, lặp lại | Đủ dùng, có từ hay | Đa dạng, dùng đúng |
| Grammar | Lỗi nhiều | Có lỗi nhưng OK | Đa dạng cấu trúc |
| Pronunciation | Khó nghe ở một số từ | Rõ ràng nhìn chung | Tự nhiên, ít lỗi |
