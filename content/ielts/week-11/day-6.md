## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Reported Speech — Commands

### 📖 Tường thuật câu mệnh lệnh là gì?

Khi kể lại lời yêu cầu, mệnh lệnh, hoặc khuyên bảo của ai đó, ta dùng **Reported Commands**.

### 🔑 Cấu trúc

| Loại | Direct | Reported |
|------|--------|----------|
| Mệnh lệnh (+) | "Save money." | He **told me to save** money. |
| Mệnh lệnh (-) | "Don't spend too much." | She **told me not to spend** too much. |
| Yêu cầu | "Please help me." | He **asked me to help** him. |
| Khuyên | "You should invest wisely." | She **advised me to invest** wisely. |

### Các động từ tường thuật thường dùng

| Động từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **told** | bảo | He **told** me **to** pay the bill. |
| **asked** | yêu cầu | She **asked** me **to** lend her money. |
| **advised** | khuyên | The bank **advised** us **to** save more. |
| **warned** | cảnh báo | He **warned** me **not to** invest in that company. |
| **ordered** | ra lệnh | The boss **ordered** them **to** finish the report. |
| **encouraged** | khuyến khích | She **encouraged** him **to** start a business. |
| **reminded** | nhắc nhở | She **reminded** me **to** pay the rent. |

### ⚠️ Quy tắc quan trọng

1. **Luôn dùng to + V nguyên mẫu** (infinitive)
2. **Phủ định:** told + O + **not to** + V
3. **Không lùi thì** như reported statements/questions
4. **Đổi đại từ** cho phù hợp

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chuyển sang Reported Commands

1. "Pay your taxes on time." → The accountant told me ______
2. "Don't borrow too much money." → My father told me ______
3. "Please save more money." → She asked me ______
4. "Invest in education." → The minister advised people ______
5. "Don't waste your salary." → He warned me ______
6. "Open a savings account." → The bank advised us ______
7. "Don't buy things you don't need." → My mother told me ______
8. "Please lend me some money." → He asked me ______
9. "Reduce your expenses." → The financial advisor told her ______
10. "Don't forget to pay the rent." → She reminded me ______

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Chuyển sang Direct Speech

1. She told him to check the budget.
2. He asked me not to spend all my savings.
3. The teacher advised students to learn about the economy.
4. She warned us not to take unnecessary loans.
5. He encouraged me to start saving early.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. He told me save money.
2. She asked me to don't waste money.
3. The teacher told to the students to study harder.
4. He advised me that I should invest wisely.
5. She warned me to not borrow from strangers.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | ...told me **to pay** my taxes on time. | Mệnh lệnh → told + O + to V |
| 2 | ...told me **not to borrow** too much money. | Phủ định → not to V |
| 3 | ...asked me **to save** more money. | Yêu cầu lịch sự → asked + O + to V |
| 4 | ...advised people **to invest** in education. | Khuyên → advised + O + to V |
| 5 | ...warned me **not to waste** my salary. | Cảnh báo phủ định → warned + not to V |
| 6 | ...advised us **to open** a savings account. | Khuyên → advised + O + to V |
| 7 | ...told me **not to buy** things I didn't need. | Phủ định → not to V |
| 8 | ...asked me **to lend** him some money. | Yêu cầu → asked + O + to V |
| 9 | ...told her **to reduce** her expenses. | Mệnh lệnh → told + O + to V |
| 10 | ...reminded me **to pay** the rent. | Nhắc nhở → reminded + O + to V |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | "Check the budget." |
| 2 | "Don't spend all your savings." |
| 3 | "Learn about the economy." |
| 4 | "Don't take unnecessary loans." |
| 5 | "Start saving early." |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | told me save | He told me **to save** money. | Thiếu "to" trước động từ |
| 2 | to don't | She asked me **not to waste** money. | not to V, không phải to don't V |
| 3 | told to the students | The teacher told **the students** to study harder. | told + O trực tiếp, không có "to" |
| 4 | that I should | He advised me **to invest** wisely. | Reported command dùng to V, không dùng that + should |
| 5 | to not borrow | She warned me **not to borrow** from strangers. | "not to" đi liền nhau trước động từ |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - How to be direct](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/ep-231123)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Managing money is an important life skill. A **financial** advisor **told** me **to** create a monthly **budget** and track all my **expenses**. He also **advised** me **to** put part of my **salary** into **savings** every month.
>
> My parents always **warned** me **not to** take **loans** unless it was absolutely necessary. They **encouraged** me **to** live within my **income**.
>
> **Inflation** and **recession** can make life difficult for **consumers**. Experts **remind** people **to** plan ahead and avoid unnecessary **debt**.

*(88 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What did the financial advisor tell the writer to do?
2. What did the advisor also advise?
3. What did the writer's parents warn about?
4. What did the parents encourage the writer to do?
5. What do experts remind people to do?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | To create a monthly budget and track all expenses. |
| 2 | To put part of the salary into savings every month. |
| 3 | Not to take loans unless absolutely necessary. |
| 4 | To live within their income. |
| 5 | To plan ahead and avoid unnecessary debt. |
