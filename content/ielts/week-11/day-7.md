8. She works as a ______ at a homeless ______. (volunteer / shelter)
9. The ______ signed a one-year ______. (tenant / lease)
10. You should create a monthly ______ to manage your ______. (budget / expenses)

### 📝 MINI TEST — Tuần 11 (40 câu)

**Phần 1: Từ vựng — Chọn từ đúng (10 câu)**

1. The city centre is always very ______.
   - a) peaceful  b) crowded  c) rural  d) affordable

2. The ______ decided to renovate the old building.
   - a) tenant  b) witness  c) landlord  d) consumer

3. The police found important ______ at the crime scene.
   - a) evidence  b) election  c) investment  d) charity

4. The ______ passed a new law to reduce pollution.
   - a) volunteer  b) parliament  c) shelter  d) currency

5. Many young people face ______ after finishing university.
   - a) inflation  b) welfare  c) unemployment  d) mortgage

6. ______ rates have been rising, making food more expensive.
   - a) Inflation  b) Election  c) Immigration  d) Donation

7. She cannot ______ to buy a house in the city.
   - a) oppose  b) arrest  c) afford  d) debate

8. The ______ was taken to hospital after the robbery.
   - a) criminal  b) victim  c) judge  d) minister

9. The apartment has a lovely ______ with a view of the park.
   - a) mortgage  b) storey  c) balcony  d) deposit

10. Human ______ must be protected by law.
    - a) savings  b) rights  c) profits  d) expenses

**Phần 2: Comparatives & Superlatives (5 câu)**

11. City life is ______ (expensive) than country life.
12. This is ______ (safe) neighbourhood in the city.
13. Public transport is ______ (convenient) than driving in rush hour.
14. The countryside has ______ (clean) air than the city.
15. She is ______ (good) lawyer in the firm.

**Phần 3: Relative Clauses (5 câu)**

16. The man ______ lives next door is a police officer.
17. Tokyo, ______ is the capital of Japan, has low crime rates.
18. The house ______ we rented last year was very spacious.
19. Mr. Yamada, ______ wife is a judge, works at the university.
20. Is this the shelter ______ they provide free meals?

**Phần 4: Passive Voice (5 câu)**

21. A new law ______ (pass) by parliament last week.
22. The budget ______ (discuss) in the meeting right now.
23. Taxes ______ (increase) next year.
24. The criminal ______ (arrest) by the police yesterday.
25. New regulations ______ (introduce) since January.

**Phần 5: Reported Speech — Questions (5 câu)**

26. "Do you live in the city?" → She asked ______
27. "Where is the nearest hospital?" → He asked ______
28. "Have you ever been a victim of crime?" → She asked ______
29. "Will the government reduce taxes?" → He asked ______
30. "Why are house prices so high?" → She asked ______

**Phần 6: Reported Speech — Commands (5 câu)**

31. "Save money for the future." → My father told me ______
32. "Don't walk alone at night." → The police warned us ______
33. "Please donate to the charity." → She asked me ______
34. "Pay your rent on time." → The landlord reminded him ______
35. "Don't invest all your money in one place." → The advisor warned her ______

**Phần 7: Đọc hiểu (5 câu)**

> Living in a big city has both **advantages** and **disadvantages**. Cities offer better job **opportunities** and more **facilities** such as hospitals and schools. Public transport is usually more **convenient** than in **rural** areas.
>
> However, **urban** life can be stressful. **Pollution** and **traffic** are common problems. The cost of living, which is much higher than in the **countryside**, makes it difficult for many families. The **government** has been **advised** to build more **affordable** housing.
>
> A recent survey **asked** residents **whether** they were happy with their **neighbourhood**. Most people said they wanted a safer and cleaner **environment**.

*(100 từ)*

36. What advantages do cities offer?
37. What are common problems in urban life?
38. What makes life difficult for many families?
39. What has the government been advised to do?
40. What did most residents want?
