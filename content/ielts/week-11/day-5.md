## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Reported Speech — Questions

### 📖 Tường thuật câu hỏi là gì?

Khi muốn kể lại câu hỏi của người khác, ta dùng **Reported Questions** (câu hỏi gián tiếp).

### 🔑 Hai loại câu hỏi

**1. Câu hỏi Yes/No → dùng if / whether**

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| "Do you like this city?" | She **asked if/whether** I **liked** that city. |
| "Are you a volunteer?" | He **asked if/whether** I **was** a volunteer. |
| "Have you donated money?" | She **asked if/whether** I **had donated** money. |
| "Will you join the protest?" | He **asked if/whether** I **would join** the protest. |

**2. Câu hỏi Wh- → giữ nguyên từ hỏi**

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| "Where do you live?" | She **asked where** I **lived**. |
| "What is the problem?" | He **asked what** the problem **was**. |
| "Why are people protesting?" | She **asked why** people **were protesting**. |
| "How can we help?" | He **asked how** we **could help**. |

### ⚠️ Quy tắc quan trọng

1. **Lùi thì** (tense backshift): do → did, is → was, will → would, have → had
2. **Đổi đại từ**: I → he/she, you → I, my → his/her
3. **Đổi trạng từ**: this → that, here → there, today → that day
4. **Bỏ dấu hỏi** — câu tường thuật là câu trần thuật
5. **Không đảo ngữ**: ❌ asked where **did** I live → ✅ asked where I **lived**

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chuyển sang Reported Questions

1. "Do you support this charity?" → She asked ______
2. "Where is the nearest shelter?" → He asked ______
3. "Have you ever volunteered?" → She asked ______
4. "Why is unemployment so high?" → He asked ______
5. "Will the government help the homeless?" → She asked ______
6. "Are you aware of the issue?" → He asked ______
7. "What can we do about poverty?" → She asked ______
8. "Is healthcare free in your country?" → He asked ______
9. "How many people donated?" → She asked ______
10. "Did you join the protest?" → He asked ______

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Chuyển sang Direct Questions

1. She asked if I was a volunteer.
2. He asked where the donation centre was.
3. She asked whether I had seen the protest.
4. He asked what the biggest social issue was.
5. She asked if the elderly received enough support.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. She asked did I support the charity.
2. He asked where was the shelter.
3. She asked if I have ever volunteered.
4. He asked me that whether I would donate.
5. She asked what can we do about inequality.

---


### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | She asked **if/whether I supported** that charity. | Yes/No → if/whether, support → supported |
| 2 | He asked **where the nearest shelter was**. | Wh-question, is → was |
| 3 | She asked **if/whether I had ever volunteered**. | have → had |
| 4 | He asked **why unemployment was so high**. | is → was |
| 5 | She asked **if/whether the government would help** the homeless. | will → would |
| 6 | He asked **if/whether I was aware** of the issue. | are → was |
| 7 | She asked **what we could do** about poverty. | can → could |
| 8 | He asked **if/whether healthcare was free** in my country. | is → was, your → my |
| 9 | She asked **how many people had donated**. | did donate → had donated |
| 10 | He asked **if/whether I had joined** the protest. | did join → had joined |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | "Are you a volunteer?" |
| 2 | "Where is the donation centre?" |
| 3 | "Have you seen the protest?" |
| 4 | "What is the biggest social issue?" |
| 5 | "Do the elderly receive enough support?" |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | asked did I | She asked **if/whether I supported** the charity. | Không đảo ngữ trong reported speech |
| 2 | where was the shelter | He asked **where the shelter was**. | Không đảo ngữ — S + V |
| 3 | have | She asked if I **had** ever volunteered. | Lùi thì: have → had |
| 4 | that whether | He asked me **whether** I would donate. | Không dùng "that" trước if/whether |
| 5 | can we do | She asked **what we could do** about inequality. | Lùi thì: can → could, không đảo ngữ |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Helping the homeless](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/ep-200206)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> **Social issues** affect everyone in **society**. **Poverty** and **unemployment** are problems that many countries face. When people lose their jobs, they may become **homeless** and need to stay at a **shelter**.
>
> Many organisations and **volunteers** work hard to help. They raise **awareness** and collect **donations** for those in need. The **government** also provides **healthcare** and **welfare** services.
>
> A reporter asked a volunteer **whether** the situation was improving. The volunteer said that more people were helping, but there was still a lot of work to do.

*(90 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What happens when people lose their jobs?
2. What do volunteers do to help?
3. What services does the government provide?
4. What did the reporter ask the volunteer?
5. What did the volunteer say about the situation?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | They may become homeless and need to stay at a shelter. |
| 2 | They raise awareness and collect donations. |
| 3 | Healthcare and welfare services. |
| 4 | The reporter asked whether the situation was improving. |
| 5 | More people were helping, but there was still a lot of work to do. |
