## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Comparatives & Superlatives Review

### 📖 Ôn lại so sánh hơn & so sánh nhất

**Comparatives** (so sánh hơn) dùng để so sánh **2 thứ**:
- The city is **bigger than** the village.

**Superlatives** (so sánh nhất) dùng để so sánh **3 thứ trở lên**:
- Tokyo is **the biggest** city in Japan.

### 🔑 Quy tắc

| Loại tính từ | Comparative | Superlative | Ví dụ |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 âm tiết | + **-er** | + **-est** | big → bigg**er** → bigg**est** |
| 1 âm tiết kết thúc -e | + **-r** | + **-st** | large → larg**er** → larg**est** |
| 2 âm tiết kết thúc -y | bỏ y + **-ier** | bỏ y + **-iest** | busy → bus**ier** → bus**iest** |
| 2+ âm tiết | **more** + adj | **the most** + adj | convenient → **more** convenient → **the most** convenient |
| Bất quy tắc | | | good → **better** → **the best** |

### Các trường hợp bất quy tắc

| Tính từ | Comparative | Superlative |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| good | better | the best |
| bad | worse | the worst |
| far | farther/further | the farthest/furthest |
| many/much | more | the most |
| little | less | the least |

### Cấu trúc câu

| So sánh | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Hơn | S + be + **adj-er / more adj + than** + O | Cities are **bigger than** villages. |
| Nhất | S + be + **the adj-est / the most adj** | Tokyo is **the most crowded** city. |
| Bằng | S + be + **as adj as** + O | This town is **as peaceful as** my hometown. |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Điền dạng so sánh đúng

1. The city is ______ (noisy) than the countryside.
2. Rural areas are ______ (peaceful) than urban areas.
3. This is ______ (good) restaurant in town.
4. Pollution in cities is ______ (bad) than in villages.
5. Public transport is ______ (convenient) than driving.
6. My village is ______ (small) village in the province.
7. Life in the city is ______ (expensive) than in the country.
8. The air here is ______ (clean) than in the city centre.
9. She is ______ (happy) person I know.
10. This suburb is ______ (quiet) as my old neighbourhood.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Viết câu so sánh

Dùng từ gợi ý để viết câu hoàn chỉnh:

1. Tokyo / big / Osaka → ______
2. countryside / quiet / city → ______
3. this / bad / traffic jam / year → ______
4. living in suburbs / cheap / living in centre → ______
5. she / good / student / class → ______

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. The city is more bigger than the town.
2. This is the most peaceful place I ever saw.
3. Rural life is gooder than urban life.
4. He is the more intelligent student in the class.
5. My house is as big than yours.

---


### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | noisier | 2 âm tiết kết thúc -y → bỏ y + -ier |
| 2 | more peaceful | 2 âm tiết → more + adj |
| 3 | the best | good → the best (bất quy tắc), so sánh nhất |
| 4 | worse | bad → worse (bất quy tắc) |
| 5 | more convenient | 3 âm tiết → more + adj |
| 6 | the smallest | 1 âm tiết → the + adj-est |
| 7 | more expensive | 3 âm tiết → more + adj |
| 8 | cleaner | 1 âm tiết → adj-er |
| 9 | the happiest | 2 âm tiết kết thúc -y → the + bỏ y + -iest |
| 10 | as quiet | So sánh bằng: as + adj + as |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Tokyo is bigger than Osaka. |
| 2 | The countryside is quieter than the city. |
| 3 | This is the worst traffic jam this year. |
| 4 | Living in the suburbs is cheaper than living in the centre. |
| 5 | She is the best student in the class. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | more bigger | The city is **bigger** than the town. | Không dùng more + -er cùng lúc |
| 2 | ever saw | This is the most peaceful place I **have ever seen**. | Superlative + present perfect |
| 3 | gooder | Rural life is **better** than urban life. | good → better (bất quy tắc) |
| 4 | the more intelligent | He is **the most intelligent** student in the class. | Superlative dùng the most |
| 5 | big than | My house is as big **as** yours. | So sánh bằng: as...as |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Urbanisation](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/ep-200130)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Many people move from the **countryside** to the **city** for better **opportunities**. **Urban** areas have more jobs, hospitals, and schools. Public transport is also more **convenient**.
>
> However, cities are often **crowded** and noisy. **Pollution** is a serious problem. The cost of living is much **higher** than in **rural** areas.
>
> Some people prefer the **peaceful** life in the **suburbs**. They enjoy the fresh air and friendly **community**. Each **lifestyle** has its own **advantages** and **disadvantages**.

*(88 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. Why do many people move to cities?
2. What problems do cities often have?
3. What do some people prefer about suburban life?
4. Is the cost of living higher in cities or rural areas?
5. Does each lifestyle have only advantages?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | For better opportunities — more jobs, hospitals, and schools. |
| 2 | Cities are often crowded, noisy, and have pollution problems. |
| 3 | They enjoy the fresh air and friendly community. |
| 4 | The cost of living is higher in cities. |
| 5 | No, each lifestyle has both advantages and disadvantages. |
