## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Passive Voice — All Tenses Review

### 📖 Passive Voice là gì?

**Passive Voice** (câu bị động) dùng khi muốn nhấn mạnh **hành động** hoặc **đối tượng bị tác động**, thay vì người thực hiện.

**Cấu trúc:** S + **be** + **V3 (past participle)** + (by + agent)

### 🔑 Passive ở các thì

| Thì | Active | Passive |
|-----|--------|---------|
| Present Simple | They **pass** the law. | The law **is passed**. |
| Present Continuous | They **are debating** the policy. | The policy **is being debated**. |
| Past Simple | They **elected** a new president. | A new president **was elected**. |
| Past Continuous | They **were discussing** the reform. | The reform **was being discussed**. |
| Present Perfect | They **have announced** the budget. | The budget **has been announced**. |
| Past Perfect | They **had approved** the law. | The law **had been approved**. |
| Future Simple | They **will increase** taxes. | Taxes **will be increased**. |
| Modal verbs | They **must follow** the rules. | The rules **must be followed**. |

### Khi nào dùng Passive?

| Trường hợp | Ví dụ |
|------------|-------|
| Không biết ai làm | My wallet **was stolen**. |
| Không quan trọng ai làm | The law **was passed** in 2020. |
| Nhấn mạnh đối tượng | The president **was elected** by the people. |
| Văn phong trang trọng (IELTS Writing) | It **is believed** that education is important. |

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Chuyển sang Passive Voice

1. The government passed a new law. → A new law ______
2. Citizens elect the president every five years. → The president ______
3. They are building a new parliament. → A new parliament ______
4. Someone has stolen the documents. → The documents ______
5. They will announce the results tomorrow. → The results ______
6. People must pay taxes. → Taxes ______
7. The minister was writing a report. → A report ______
8. They had approved the budget before the meeting. → The budget ______
9. The police are investigating the case. → The case ______
10. People should respect the law. → The law ______

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Chuyển sang Active Voice

1. The new policy was introduced by the government.
2. The election will be held next month.
3. The budget has been approved by parliament.
4. New regulations are being discussed.
5. The reform must be supported by all citizens.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. The law was pass last year.
2. A new president has elected.
3. The report is being write by the minister.
4. Taxes will increased next year.
5. The decision was been made yesterday.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | A new law **was passed** (by the government). | Past Simple passive |
| 2 | The president **is elected** every five years. | Present Simple passive |
| 3 | A new parliament **is being built**. | Present Continuous passive |
| 4 | The documents **have been stolen**. | Present Perfect passive |
| 5 | The results **will be announced** tomorrow. | Future Simple passive |
| 6 | Taxes **must be paid**. | Modal passive |
| 7 | A report **was being written** (by the minister). | Past Continuous passive |
| 8 | The budget **had been approved** before the meeting. | Past Perfect passive |
| 9 | The case **is being investigated** (by the police). | Present Continuous passive |
| 10 | The law **should be respected**. | Modal passive |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | The government **introduced** the new policy. |
| 2 | They **will hold** the election next month. |
| 3 | Parliament **has approved** the budget. |
| 4 | They **are discussing** new regulations. |
| 5 | All citizens **must support** the reform. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | pass | The law was **passed** last year. | Cần V3 (past participle) |
| 2 | has elected | A new president **has been elected**. | Thiếu "been" — Present Perfect passive |
| 3 | write | The report is being **written** by the minister. | write → written (V3) |
| 4 | will increased | Taxes **will be increased** next year. | Thiếu "be" — Future passive |
| 5 | was been | The decision **was made** yesterday. | Past Simple passive: was + V3, không cần been |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Does your vote matter?](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/ep-240516)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> In a **democracy**, **elections** are held regularly. **Citizens** have the right to **vote** for their leaders. The **president** or prime **minister** is chosen by the people.
>
> The **government** creates **policies** to improve people's lives. **Taxes** are collected to pay for public services like healthcare and education. The national **budget** is **debated** in **parliament** every year.
>
> Sometimes, **reforms** are **opposed** by some groups. However, in a healthy democracy, different opinions are respected and **freedom** of speech is protected.

*(85 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What happens regularly in a democracy?
2. Who chooses the president or prime minister?
3. Why are taxes collected?
4. Where is the national budget debated?
5. What is protected in a healthy democracy?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Elections are held regularly. |
| 2 | The people choose the president or prime minister. |
| 3 | To pay for public services like healthcare and education. |
| 4 | In parliament. |
| 5 | Different opinions and freedom of speech are protected. |
