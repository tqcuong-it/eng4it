## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Defining Relative Clauses

### 📖 Defining Relative Clauses là gì?

**Defining Relative Clauses** (mệnh đề quan hệ xác định) dùng để **xác định rõ** danh từ đứng trước — cho biết đang nói về người/vật nào. **Không có dấu phẩy** và **không thể bỏ đi** vì câu sẽ mất nghĩa.

### 🔑 Đại từ quan hệ

| Đại từ | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **who** | người | The man **who** lives next door is friendly. |
| **which** | vật / ý tưởng | The house **which** has a red door is mine. |
| **that** | người hoặc vật | The apartment **that** we rented is spacious. |
| **where** | nơi chốn | The neighbourhood **where** I live is quiet. |
| **whose** | sở hữu | The landlord **whose** house we rent is kind. |

### Khi nào dùng who / which / that?

| Trường hợp | Dùng | Ví dụ |
|------------|------|-------|
| Chủ ngữ là người | **who** / **that** | The tenant **who/that rents** this flat is young. |
| Chủ ngữ là vật | **which** / **that** | The building **which/that stands** on the corner is old. |
| Tân ngữ là người | **who** / **that** / bỏ | The landlord (**who/that**) I called was helpful. |
| Tân ngữ là vật | **which** / **that** / bỏ | The apartment (**which/that**) we saw was nice. |

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Khi đại từ quan hệ là **tân ngữ**, bạn có thể **bỏ** nó đi:
> - The house **(that/which)** I like is expensive. → The house I like is expensive. ✅

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Điền who, which, that, where hoặc whose

1. The woman ______ sold us the house was very helpful.
2. The apartment ______ we rented has two bedrooms.
3. I know a place ______ you can find cheap accommodation.
4. The landlord ______ property we rent lives abroad.
5. The building ______ is next to the park is new.
6. People ______ live in the suburbs often drive to work.
7. The lease ______ he signed is for two years.
8. Is this the neighbourhood ______ you grew up?
9. The tenant ______ moved out left the apartment clean.
10. The house ______ roof was damaged needs renovation.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Nối hai câu thành một câu dùng mệnh đề quan hệ

1. The man is my landlord. He lives upstairs.
2. The house is very old. It has a beautiful garden.
3. I met a woman. She works in real estate.
4. This is the apartment. We want to rent it.
5. The neighbourhood is very safe. I live there.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Sửa lỗi sai

1. The man which lives next door is a teacher.
2. The house who I bought is near the station.
3. I know a person, who can help you find a flat.
4. The building where is on the corner has a shop.
5. She is the woman that her husband is a landlord.

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | who/that | Đứng trước là người (woman), làm chủ ngữ |
| 2 | which/that | Đứng trước là vật (apartment), làm tân ngữ |
| 3 | where | Nơi chốn (a place) |
| 4 | whose | Sở hữu (property của landlord) |
| 5 | which/that | Đứng trước là vật (building), làm chủ ngữ |
| 6 | who/that | Đứng trước là người (people), làm chủ ngữ |
| 7 | which/that | Đứng trước là vật (lease), làm tân ngữ |
| 8 | where | Nơi chốn (neighbourhood) |
| 9 | who/that | Đứng trước là người (tenant), làm chủ ngữ |
| 10 | whose | Sở hữu (roof của house) |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | The man **who/that** lives upstairs is my landlord. |
| 2 | The house **which/that** has a beautiful garden is very old. |
| 3 | I met a woman **who/that** works in real estate. |
| 4 | This is the apartment **(which/that)** we want to rent. |
| 5 | The neighbourhood **where** I live is very safe. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Lỗi sai | Sửa đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | which | The man **who/that** lives next door... | Người → dùng who/that, không dùng which |
| 2 | who | The house **which/that** I bought... | Vật → dùng which/that, không dùng who |
| 3 | dấu phẩy | I know a person **who** can help... | Defining clause → không có dấu phẩy |
| 4 | where | The building **which/that** is on the corner... | where không làm chủ ngữ, cần which/that |
| 5 | that her | She is the woman **whose** husband is a landlord. | Sở hữu → dùng whose |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Renting a home](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/ep-200924)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> Finding good **accommodation** is not easy. Many young people **rent** an **apartment** because they cannot **afford** to buy a house. The **rent** in big cities is very high, and **tenants** often have to pay a large **deposit**.
>
> Some people prefer a **furnished** flat because they do not need to buy furniture. Others want a **spacious** home with a **balcony**. The most important thing is to live in a safe **neighbourhood** **where** people feel comfortable.

*(82 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. Why do many young people rent instead of buying?
2. What do tenants often have to pay?
3. Why do some people prefer a furnished flat?
4. What do some people want in a home?
5. What is the most important thing about where you live?

### ✅ Đáp án Reading

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Because they cannot afford to buy a house. |
| 2 | They often have to pay a large deposit. |
| 3 | Because they do not need to buy furniture. |
| 4 | A spacious home with a balcony. |
| 5 | To live in a safe neighbourhood where people feel comfortable. |
