## 🌙 Buổi tối (40-60 phút) — Grammar: Non-defining Relative Clauses

### 📖 Non-defining Relative Clauses là gì?

**Non-defining Relative Clauses** (mệnh đề quan hệ không xác định) dùng để **thêm thông tin phụ** về danh từ — thông tin này có thể bỏ đi mà câu vẫn có nghĩa.

**Luôn có dấu phẩy** ngăn cách!

### 🔑 So sánh Defining vs Non-defining

| | Defining | Non-defining |
|---|---------|-------------|
| Dấu phẩy | ❌ Không có | ✅ Có dấu phẩy |
| Bỏ được không? | ❌ Không (câu mất nghĩa) | ✅ Có thể bỏ |
| Dùng **that**? | ✅ Được | ❌ Không dùng that |
| Ví dụ | The man **who stole** the car was caught. | Mr. Smith, **who is a judge**, lives here. |

### Cấu trúc

| Đại từ | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **, who** | người | My neighbour, **who is a police officer**, helped me. |
| **, which** | vật / ý tưởng | The new law, **which was passed last year**, reduced crime. |
| **, where** | nơi chốn | Tokyo, **where I live**, is very safe. |
| **, whose** | sở hữu | The judge, **whose decision was fair**, is well respected. |

> ⚠️ **Lưu ý quan trọng:**
> - ❌ KHÔNG dùng **that** trong non-defining clauses
> - ✅ The police officer, **who** caught the thief, was brave.
> - ❌ ~~The police officer, **that** caught the thief, was brave.~~

### ✏️ Bài tập 1: Thêm dấu phẩy vào vị trí đúng

1. My father who is a lawyer works in the city centre.
2. The new prison which was built last year is very modern.
3. Officer Tanaka who caught the thief received an award.
4. The courthouse where the trial took place is very old.
5. The victim whose wallet was stolen reported to police.

### ✏️ Bài tập 2: Nối hai câu dùng Non-defining Relative Clause

1. The judge made a fair decision. She has 20 years of experience.
2. The new law reduced crime by 30%. It was introduced last year.
3. My neighbourhood is very safe. I have lived there for five years.
4. The police officer was very brave. His name is Yamada.
5. The prison holds 500 inmates. It was built in 2010.

### ✏️ Bài tập 3: Chọn Defining hay Non-defining? Điền đúng

1. The woman ______ reported the crime was very scared. (who / , who)
2. Officer Lee ______ has worked here for 10 years is retiring. (, who / that)
3. The law ______ protects children is very important. (which / , which)
4. Tokyo ______ I was born is one of the safest cities. (where / , where)
5. The criminal ______ stole the car was caught. (who / , who)

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 1

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | My father**,** who is a lawyer**,** works in the city centre. | Thông tin thêm về "my father" — đã xác định rồi |
| 2 | The new prison**,** which was built last year**,** is very modern. | Thông tin thêm về "the new prison" |
| 3 | Officer Tanaka**,** who caught the thief**,** received an award. | Tên riêng → luôn dùng non-defining |
| 4 | The courthouse**,** where the trial took place**,** is very old. | Thông tin thêm về nơi chốn đã xác định |
| 5 | Không thêm dấu phẩy — đây là defining clause | Cần xác định "which victim" |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 2

| Câu | Đáp án |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | The judge, **who has 20 years of experience**, made a fair decision. |
| 2 | The new law, **which was introduced last year**, reduced crime by 30%. |
| 3 | My neighbourhood, **where I have lived for five years**, is very safe. |
| 4 | The police officer, **whose name is Yamada**, was very brave. |
| 5 | The prison, **which was built in 2010**, holds 500 inmates. |

### ✅ Đáp án Bài tập 3

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | who | Defining — cần xác định "which woman" |
| 2 | , who | Non-defining — Officer Lee đã xác định (tên riêng) |
| 3 | which | Defining — cần xác định "which law" |
| 4 | , where | Non-defining — Tokyo đã xác định (tên riêng) |
| 5 | who | Defining — cần xác định "which criminal" |

## 😴 Trước ngủ (10-15 phút) — Nghe + Đọc

### 🎧 Listening: Nghe BBC (5 phút)

👉 [BBC Learning English - Is crime going up?](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/english/features/6-minute-english/ep-230105)

> 💡 **Mẹo:** Nghe lần 1 không nhìn text. Nghe lần 2 vừa nghe vừa đọc transcript.

### 📖 Reading: Đoạn đọc ngắn (5 phút)

> **Crime** is a problem in many countries. The **police**, who work hard every day, try to keep people safe. When a **criminal** is caught, there is usually a **trial** in court.
>
> During the trial, the **judge** listens to the **evidence** and **witnesses**. If the person is found **guilty**, they may receive a **punishment** such as **prison** or a **fine**. If they are **innocent**, they are free to go.
>
> Public **safety**, which is everyone's responsibility, can be improved through education and better **laws**.

*(88 từ)*

### ❓ Câu hỏi đọc hiểu

1. What do the police try to do every day?
2. What happens when a criminal is caught?
3. What does the judge listen to during a trial?
4. What happens if a person is found guilty?
5. How can public safety be improved?

| 2 | There is usually a trial in court. |
| 3 | The judge listens to the evidence and witnesses. |
| 4 | They may receive a punishment such as prison or a fine. |
| 5 | Through education and better laws. |
